=== Return of the Blossoming Blade Episode 1 ===

  (The world... it was burning. A crimson inferno, fueled by the blood of countless warriors.)
  (At the heart of it all, a single figure stood, battered and broken, yet unyielding.)
  CHEONG MYEONG (thinking): (Thought) That bastard... The Heavenly Demon. Even after all this, he still wouldn't die.
  HEAVENLY DEMON: Kukuku... Plum Blossom Sword Saint. You are magnificent. But even you cannot defeat true despair!
  CHEONG MYEONG (thinking): (Thought) Despair? Hah! I am Cheong Myeong of Mount Hua! I am the Plum Blossom Sword Saint! I will not fall!
  [WHOOSH! CRACKLE! SHATTER!]
  CHEONG MYEONG: Plum Blossom Sword Art... Last Blossom!
  [BOOM! THUD! GURGLE...]
  (The ultimate technique. The final sacrifice. The Heavenly Demon fell, and with him, the Plum Blossom Sword Saint.)
  CHEONG MYEONG (thinking): (Thought) My body... it gave out. So this is it, huh? At least... I protected Mount Hua. I protected the Murim.
  (Darkness. Then... a faint light.)
  CHEONG MYEONG (thinking): (Thought) What is this? A ceiling? And... why is it so dusty? Where am I?
  [GROAN]
  CHEONG MYEONG (thinking): (Thought) My body... it's so small. So weak! What happened to my muscles? My qi? This isn't right!
  [COUGH! COUGH!]
  CHEONG MYEONG (thinking): (Thought) This rotten body... it can barely hold itself together. Did I... reincarnate? After hundreds of years?
  (He dragged himself to a dusty, cracked mirror. A gaunt, unfamiliar face stared back.)
  CHEONG MYEONG (thinking): (Thought) My face... this isn't mine! And this room... it's so dilapidated. Is this even Mount Hua?
  [CREAK]
  CHEONG MYEONG (thinking): (Thought) I need to get out of here. I need to find out what happened. Mount Hua... my Mount Hua. It better not have fallen into ruin!
  (He stumbled out of the room, down a rickety corridor, and into a courtyard. What he saw made his blood run cold.)
  CHEONG MYEONG (thinking): (Thought) This... this is Mount Hua? This desolate, crumbling place? Where are the disciples? Where is the grandeur? The Plum Blossom Forest is overgrown, the training grounds are empty!
  [WIND WHISTLES]
  CHEONG MYEONG (thinking): (Thought) Those damn disciples! After I sacrificed everything, after I single-handedly saved the Murim, they let my sect fall into such a state?! Unbelievable! Unforgivable!
  CHEONG MYEONG (thinking): (Thought) My qi... it's practically non-existent. But even with this pathetic body, I am still Cheong Myeong!
  CHEONG MYEONG (thinking): (Thought) I'll restore Mount Hua. I'll make it glorious again. And those lazy, incompetent descendants... they'll pay for this! Just you wait, Mount Hua! Your ancestor has returned!
  [SIGH... (followed by a determined grunt)]
  (A new chapter for the Plum Blossom Sword Saint had begun, not as a revered hero, but as a furious, reincarnated ancestor in a world that had forgotten him.)

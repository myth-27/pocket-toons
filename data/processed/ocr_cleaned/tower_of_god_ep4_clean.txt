=== Tower of God | Episode 4 | Headon's Floor ===

SUMMARY: Baam reflects on his past with Rachel before facing Headon's test. Observers Headon and Evan discuss the impossible nature of the test, which requires Baam to be swallowed by a giant eel and stab it from the inside with the Black March. Despite their doubts and attempts to make him give up, Baam surprisingly attempts this exact method, succeeding in stabbing the eel but failing to pop the ball, leaving the observers baffled.

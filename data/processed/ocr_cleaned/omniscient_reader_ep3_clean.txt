=== Episode 3 ===

SUMMARY: A Dokkaebi appears on a subway, halting it and plunging it into darkness. The protagonist recognizes the creature from a novel and realizes a catastrophic event is unfolding. The Dokkaebi announces a "paywall" for life and initiates "Main Scenario #1: Prove Your Value," demanding passengers kill living organisms within 30 minutes or face death.

=== GOD OF THE HIGH SCHOOL Chapter 1: The Match With Gods (Episode 1) ===

Later, the focus shifts to Mori Jin, a 17-year-old Taekwondo prodigy with an undefeated record, who is invited to the "God of High School" competition by Commissioner R, promising to grant any wish to the winner. Mori challenges R to a fight, loses, and then decides to take the competition seriously, as invitations are sent worldwide.

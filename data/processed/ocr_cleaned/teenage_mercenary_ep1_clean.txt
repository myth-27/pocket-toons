=== Teenage Mercenary Episode 1 ===

  (Ten years. Ten years since I was picked up from that crashed plane.)
  (Ten years since I started living a 'normal' life.)
  Ijin Yu (thinking): ...
  Ijin Yu (thinking): The smell of chalk dust, the murmur of students, the distant sound of a bell... it's all still so foreign.
  [RUSTLE]
  Ijin Yu (thinking): Even after all this time, the instincts remain. The urge to scan my surroundings, identify threats, plan escape routes.
  [CLICK (of a pen)]
  Ijin Yu (thinking): But here, in this classroom, the only 'threat' is a pop quiz.
  Yu Dayun: Oppa! There you are!
  [THUMP THUMP (running footsteps)]
  Ijin Yu: Dayun.
  Yu Dayun: I've been looking for you everywhere! Are you ready to go home? Grandpa made your favorite!
  Ijin Yu: Yeah. Let's go.
  Ijin Yu (thinking): Her smile... it's a constant reminder of what I'm protecting. What I'm living for now.
  (As they walked through the school gates, a group of older students loitered by a convenience store, their eyes scanning the younger kids.)
  Bully 1: Hey, look what we have here. Little Miss Perfect, Yu Dayun.
  Yu Dayun: Oh no...
  Bully 2: Heard you got the top score on the English test again. Must be nice, having everything handed to you.
  Bully 1: How about you 'share' some of that good fortune with us? Like, your lunch money?
  Ijin Yu: Leave her alone.
  [CRUNCH (gravel underfoot)]
  Bully 1: Oh? And who's this scrawny guy? Her bodyguard?
  Bully 2: He looks like he'd snap in half if I breathed on him.
  Ijin Yu: I said, leave her alone.
  Ijin Yu (thinking): My body tensed, ready. My eyes tracked their movements, their tells. The slight shift in weight, the clenching of a fist.
  Ijin Yu (thinking): It would be so easy to disarm them. To incapacitate them before they even realized what was happening.
  Bully 1: Or what, kid? You gonna cry to your mommy?
  [SWISH (Bully 1's arm swings)]
  Ijin Yu (thinking): I sidestepped, a blur. My hand shot out, not to strike, but to intercept.
  [GRIP! (Ijin catches Bully 1's wrist)]
  Bully 1: Agh! What the—?! Let go!
  Ijin Yu (thinking): My grip was firm, precise. Just enough pressure on a nerve point to cause discomfort, not injury. A warning.
  Bully 2: Hey, what are you doing to him?!
  Ijin Yu (thinking): My gaze met Bully 2's. Cold. Unflinching. The kind of look that had stopped men twice their age in their tracks.
  [GULP]
  Bully 1: Okay, okay! I get it! Just let go!
  Ijin Yu (thinking): I released him. He stumbled back, rubbing his wrist, a new fear in his eyes.
  Bully 2: Let's go! This guy's weird!
  [SCRAMBLE (bullies running away)]
  Yu Dayun: Oppa! Wow! You were so cool!
  Ijin Yu: It's nothing. Let's go home.
  Ijin Yu (thinking): I still have to be careful. Can't let my past bleed into their lives too much. Not yet.
  Yu Jin-ho (Grandpa): Ah, there are my favorite grandchildren! Dinner's ready!
  Ijin Yu (thinking): The warmth of their home, the scent of Grandpa's cooking... it's a different kind of battlefield. One I'm still learning to navigate.
  Ijin Yu (thinking): But I'll protect it. With everything I have.

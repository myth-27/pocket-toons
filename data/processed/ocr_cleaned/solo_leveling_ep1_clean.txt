=== Solo Leveling, Episode 1: Blood ===

SUMMARY: In 2033 Seoul, a news report discusses recent bug attacks and the lack of 'Butterflies' to handle them. Maehwa, a student who recently recovered from an accident and memory loss, expresses her joy about going to school and wanting a normal life, despite her sister's warning to be careful about 'you-know-what'. During a history lesson about the origin of the 'Bugs' and the creation of 'Butterflies' (humans with powers from the original Butterfly Goddess), students get excited about a live Butterfly sighting. Later, Maehwa is bullied by classmates who question her strange aversion to sharp objects and her scar. When they intentionally cut her hand to make her bleed, Maehwa's blood causes a strange, burning reaction, confirming her sister's mysterious warning.

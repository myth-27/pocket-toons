=== Nano Machine Episode 1 ===

  (The Demonic Cult. A place where only the strong survive, and the weak are discarded like trash. For Cheon Yeo-Woon, the illegitimate son of the Lord, every day was a battle for existence.)
  Cheon Yeo-Woon (thinking): (Thought) My body still aches from yesterday's 'training.' They call it training, but it's just an excuse to beat the 'Prince of the Orphanage' to a pulp.
  [GRUNT]
  Cheon Yeo-Woon (thinking): (Thought) I can't give up. Not now. Not ever. Mother... I promised I would survive.
  [PANT PANT]
  (Hidden in the desolate corners of the Cult's vast estate, Cheon Yeo-Woon practiced the basic forms, movements he'd stolen glances of from other cadets. His movements were clumsy, his body weak, but his spirit burned.)
  Bully 1: Well, well, look what we have here. The 'Prince' is trying to play warrior.
  [FOOTSTEPS (CRUNCHING GRAVEL)]
  Bully 2: Still alive, Yeo-Woon? We thought you'd finally learned your place after yesterday's lesson.
  Cheon Yeo-Woon (thinking): (Thought) Dammit. It's them again. The sons of the Blade Clan Elder and the Wise Clan Elder.
  Cheon Yeo-Woon: What do you want?
  Bully 1: Oh, nothing much. Just came to remind you of your status. A lowly bastard, born of a servant. You disgrace the Lord's bloodline.
  Bully 2: You should just disappear. The Demonic Cult has no place for trash like you.
  [WHOOSH (A quick punch)]
  Cheon Yeo-Woon: ACK!
  [THUD (Yeo-Woon falls)]
  Cheon Yeo-Woon (thinking): (Thought) They're not even trying. Just playing with their food.
  Bully 1: Still glaring? Fool. You think you can stand against us?
  [KICK! CRACK!]
  Cheon Yeo-Woon: URGH!
  Bully 2: Let's teach him a proper lesson this time. One he won't forget.
  (Blow after blow rained down on Yeo-Woon. His vision blurred, his body screamed in protest, but he refused to cry out. To show weakness was to invite more suffering.)
  [PUNCH! KICK! PUNCH! (Repeated, muffled)]
  Cheon Yeo-Woon (thinking): (Thought) Is this it? Am I going to die here? No... I can't... I won't...
  Bully 1: He's barely conscious. Let's finish this.
  Bully 2: Throw him into the Abandoned Well. Let him rot there. No one will find him.
  [GRAB. DRAG. SCRAPE.]
  Cheon Yeo-Woon (thinking): (Thought) The Abandoned Well... it's said to be cursed. A place where the Cult's failures are forgotten...
  [PUSH! SPLASH!]
  Cheon Yeo-Woon (thinking): (Thought) Cold... so cold... I can't breathe...
  (Plunged into the icy depths, Cheon Yeo-Woon's battered body struggled. Darkness enveloped him, and consciousness began to fade. This was the end. Or was it?)
  [GURGLE (Water)]
  Cheon Yeo-Woon (thinking): (Thought) Mother... I'm sorry... I couldn't...
  [BZZZZT... (Faint, high-pitched hum)]
  (As his eyes closed, a faint, ethereal blue light pulsed in the darkness, drawing closer to the dying boy.)

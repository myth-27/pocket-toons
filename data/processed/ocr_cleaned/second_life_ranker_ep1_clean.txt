=== Second Life Ranker Episode 1 ===

  (The world changed drastically a decade ago. A colossal structure, an 'Obelisk,' appeared, connecting dimensions and drawing in those who sought power, wealth, or glory.)
  (They called it 'The Tower.' My twin brother, Jeong-woo, was one of them. He vanished without a trace, leaving me alone in a world that no longer made sense.)
  Yeon-woo (thinking): (Thought) Ten years. Ten years since he disappeared. Ten years since I felt truly alive.
  Yeon-woo: Another day, another empty sunrise.
  [THUD]
  (A package, long delayed, arrived. Inside, a worn leather journal and an old pocket watch. His watch. My brother's.)
  Yeon-woo (thinking): (Thought) This... this is Jeong-woo's. But how...?
  [CRACKLE]
  (Day 1 in the Tower. It's exhilarating! The challenges are immense, but the rewards... they're beyond anything I imagined. I can feel myself growing stronger.)
  [FLIP]
  (We formed a party, 'Arthia.' Ananta, Agares, Triton... they're good people. We're climbing fast. We're going to reach the top, I know it!)
  Yeon-woo (thinking): (Thought) Arthia... I remember him mentioning them in a letter, years ago.
  [FLIP]
  (The 20th floor. We found a hidden piece, the 'Philosopher's Stone.' It's incredible power, but... Ananta has been acting strange. And Agares... his eyes hold a darkness I haven't seen before.)
  Yeon-woo (thinking): (Thought) Strange? What happened?
  [FLIP]
  (They betrayed me. All of them. They wanted the Stone for themselves. They ambushed me on the 25th floor. I barely escaped, but I'm gravely injured. I'm hiding with the One-horned tribe, but I don't know how much longer I can last.)
  Yeon-woo (thinking): (Thought) Betrayed? No... it can't be. He trusted them!
  [RUSTLE]
  (Yeon-woo... if you're reading this, I'm probably gone. They're coming for me. I've left everything I have, all my abilities, my knowledge, my power... in this watch. It's a key. A key to the Tower, and to my revenge.)
  (Take it. Take my strength. Climb the Tower. Find out why. And make them pay. For me, Yeon-woo. For us.)
  [CRUMBLE]
  Yeon-woo (thinking): (Thought) Jeong-woo... my brother... he was murdered. Betrayed by those he called friends.
  Yeon-woo: Ananta. Agares. Triton. Arthia.
  Yeon-woo (thinking): (Thought) My brother's last wish... his dying plea. I will grant it.
  [CRACK]
  Yeon-woo: The Tower. So that's where you are, Jeong-woo. And that's where I'm going.
  Yeon-woo: I will climb. I will become strong. Strong enough to tear down everything they built. Strong enough to make them regret the day they ever touched you.
  Yeon-woo: My name is Cha Yeon-woo. And I will avenge my brother.
  [WHOOSH]

=== My In-Laws Are Obsessed With Me Episode 1 ===

  (The softest silk sheets, a bed larger than my old apartment, and a ceiling adorned with frescoes I could only dream of seeing in a museum.)
  Yvon (thinking): This isn't my room.
  Yvon (thinking): No, this isn't my world at all. Not anymore.
  (It had been a week since I woke up in the body of Yvon, the commoner bride of Grand Duke Kaelus de Valois. The villainess destined to die a gruesome death in the novel, 'The Lady of the Frozen North'.)
  Yvon (thinking): A commoner, forced into a political marriage with the most feared man in the Empire. And his family? Even more terrifying. I'm doomed.
  [CREAK]
  (The heavy oak doors to my bedchamber swung open. Two maids, dressed impeccably, curtsied so low their heads almost touched the floor.)
  Maid 1 (Elara): Good morning, Your Grace. Did you sleep well?
  Yvon: Good morning. Yes, thank you.
  Yvon (thinking): They're always so… attentive. Too attentive. Is this their way of watching me? Reporting my every move to the Grand Duke?
  Maid 2 (Seraphina): We've prepared your bath, Your Grace. The water is infused with rose petals and lavender, just as you seemed to enjoy yesterday.
  Yvon: Oh, thank you.
  Yvon (thinking): Yesterday? I just picked a random scent. Do they really pay attention to such trivial things? Or are they trying to lull me into a false sense of security?
  [SPLASH]
  (The bath was indeed perfect. As I dressed, the maids fussed over my hair and gown, their movements gentle, almost reverent.)
  Elara: This sapphire gown truly brings out the color of your eyes, Your Grace. You look breathtaking.
  Yvon: Thank you, Elara.
  Yvon (thinking): Breathtaking? The original Yvon was described as plain. They must be mocking me. Or perhaps trying to gain my trust before they deliver the poisoned tea.
  (My paranoia was a constant companion. In the novel, the Grand Duke's family despised Yvon, seeing her as an unworthy commoner. They made her life a living hell before her eventual execution.)
  (But so far, they'd only been… kind. Suspiciously kind.)
  Seraphina: Breakfast is served in the main dining hall, Your Grace. The Grand Duchess Mother is already waiting.
  Yvon (thinking): The Grand Duchess Mother?
  Yvon (thinking): Oh, no. This is it. The first direct confrontation with one of the main villains. She's the one who orchestrated Yvon's downfall!
  [CLACK-CLACK]
  (My heart pounded as I followed the maids through the opulent corridors. Each step felt like walking towards my doom. The dining hall was vast, filled with morning light streaming through stained-glass windows.)
  (At the head of the long table sat a woman with silver hair, her posture regal, her gaze sharp.)
  Grand Duchess Mother: Yvon, my dear. You're finally here. Come, sit. I had the kitchen prepare your favorite almond pastries.
  Yvon (thinking): Almond pastries?
  Yvon (thinking): How could she possibly know my favorite? I haven't even told anyone! This is too much. She’s trying to disarm me. I need to be careful.
  Yvon: Thank you, Your Grace. That's… very thoughtful.
  Grand Duchess Mother: Nonsense, child. You are family now. And speaking of family… Kaelus will be joining us shortly. He insisted on seeing you before his morning duties.
  Yvon (thinking): K-Kaelus?
  (My blood ran cold. The Grand Duke. The man whose very name struck fear into the hearts of nobles and commoners alike. The man who, in the novel, personally ordered Yvon's execution.)
  [FOOTSTEPS (heavy, deliberate)]
  (A shadow fell over the entrance. Tall, imposing, with eyes the color of a winter sky and a presence that commanded silence.)
  Grand Duke Kaelus: Good morning, Mother. Yvon.
  Yvon: G-Grand Duke.
  Yvon (thinking): His gaze… it's so intense. He's looking right through me. Is he already plotting my demise? I need to stay alive. I *will* stay alive. No matter what this terrifying family throws at me.

=== Weak Hero Episode 1 ===

  (Eunjang High. A school like any other, filled with the usual hierarchy. The strong rule, the weak obey. Or so they thought.)
  (And then there was Gray Yeon. The quietest kid in class. The one who always had his nose in a book. The one everyone overlooked.)
  STUDENT 1: Hey, isn't that Gray Yeon? He's always so quiet.
  STUDENT 2: Yeah, barely speaks. Probably scared of his own shadow.
  Gray Yeon: ...
  Gray Yeon (thinking): (They always talk. They always assume. It's fine. Let them.)
  (After class, the hallways emptied quickly. But some lingered. Like Min-jun and his crew, a group known for preying on the 'weak'.)
  [FOOTSTEPS ECHO]
  Min-jun: Well, well, well. Look what we have here. The little bookworm, all alone.
  Gray Yeon (thinking): (Min-jun. Known for his short temper and even shorter fuse. Predictable.)
  Min-jun: What's wrong, nerd? Cat got your tongue? Or are you just too busy calculating how many punches it'll take to knock you out?
  Min-jun's Crony 1: Heh, one good hit and he'll crumble.
  Gray Yeon: I'm not interested in your games. Just leave me alone.
  Min-jun: Oh, a tough guy now? You think you can just walk away from us?
  [GRAB (Min-jun grabs Gray's arm)]
  Gray Yeon (thinking): (His grip is tight. Standard. But his stance is off-balance. Predictable.)
  Min-jun: Let's teach this little punk a lesson about respect.
  [SWISH (Gray ducks under Min-jun's swing)]
  Min-jun: Huh?!
  Gray Yeon (thinking): (Too slow. Too wide.)
  [THWACK (Gray slams his textbook into Min-jun's temple)]
  Min-jun: AGH! What the—?!
  Gray Yeon (thinking): (A textbook. Hardcover. Enough force. Now, create distance.)
  [PUSH (Gray shoves Min-jun, sending him stumbling)]
  Min-jun's Crony 2: Boss! You okay?
  Min-jun: You little rat! I'm gonna break every bone in your body!
  [ROAR]
  Gray Yeon (thinking): (Anger makes him sloppy. Good.)
  (Min-jun charged, a wild, unrefined punch aimed at Gray's head. But Gray wasn't fighting fair.)
  [CLANG (Gray yanks a fire extinguisher off the wall)]
  Min-jun: What are you—?!
  [HISS (Gray sprays Min-jun directly in the face)]
  Min-jun: GAH! MY EYES! I CAN'T SEE!
  Min-jun's Crony 1: Boss!
  Gray Yeon (thinking): (Disorientation. Temporary blindness. Perfect.)
  [WHUMP (Gray uses the extinguisher to slam Min-jun against the wall)]
  Min-jun: Ugh...
  Gray Yeon (thinking): (He's dazed. Now, the finish.)
  (Gray didn't hesitate. He pulled a pen from his pocket. Not just any pen. A heavy, metal-cased one.)
  [CLICK (Pen cap removed)]
  Min-jun's Crony 2: Hey! What are you doing?!
  Gray Yeon (thinking): (Pressure points. Temples, neck, back of the hand. Small targets, big impact.)
  [JAB! (Gray jabs the pen into Min-jun's pressure point on the neck)]
  Min-jun: A-ACK! Ngh...!
  (Min-jun's eyes rolled back. His body went limp, sliding down the wall. Unconscious.)
  Min-jun's Crony 1: Boss! He... he knocked him out with a pen!
  Min-jun's Crony 2: You psycho!
  Gray Yeon (thinking): (Two left. They're scared. Good.)
  Gray Yeon: Do you want to end up like him?
  [GULP]
  Min-jun's Crony 1: N-no! We're leaving!
  [SCRAMBLE (Cronies drag Min-jun away, panicking)]
  Gray Yeon: ...
  Gray Yeon (thinking): (They never learn. The strong aren't always the biggest. Or the loudest.)
  Gray Yeon (thinking): (They just haven't figured out my rules yet.)
  (Gray Yeon calmly picked up his fallen textbook, dusted it off, and walked away, leaving the lingering scent of extinguisher foam and fear in his wake.)

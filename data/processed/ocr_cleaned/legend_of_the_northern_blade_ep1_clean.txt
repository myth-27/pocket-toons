=== Legend of the Northern Blade Episode 1 ===

  (For centuries, the Northern Heavenly Sect stood as the impenetrable shield of the Central Plains, guarding against the monstrous hordes of the Northern Darkness. Their leader, the Northern Blade, was a legend whispered in awe and fear.)
  (But even the mightiest shield can be shattered by a serpent's bite from within.)
  [CLASH! ROAR! SCREAMS!]
  (The Central Plains Martial Alliance, fearing the Northern Heavenly Sect's power, conspired. Betrayal bloomed, and the fortress, once a beacon of hope, became a tomb.)
  [CRACK! (Sound of bone)]
  (Jin Kwan-Ho, the Third Generation Northern Blade, fought with the fury of a cornered dragon. He protected his son, Jin Mu-Won, until his last breath.)
  Jin Kwan-Ho: Mu-Won... Live...
  [THUD! (Body hitting the ground)]
  Young Mu-Won: Father...!
  (With the Northern Blade's death, the Central Plains Martial Alliance stripped the sect of its name, its legacy, and its heir. Jin Mu-Won was condemned to live out his days in the desolate, crumbling Northern Fortress, a prisoner in his own home.)
  (Ten years passed. The snow buried the memories, but not the scars.)
  [WIND HOWLS]
  Jin Mu-Won (thinking): Another day.
  (His life was a monotonous cycle of survival. Foraging for meager food, maintaining the decaying structure, enduring the biting cold. And always, the watchful eyes of the Alliance's guards, ensuring the last heir of the Northern Blade remained a forgotten ghost.)
  Jin Mu-Won (thinking): They think I'm just a broken relic. A prisoner waiting to die.
  [CRUNCH (Snow underfoot)]
  (But in the deepest, most secluded parts of the fortress, under the shroud of night, Jin Mu-Won was not simply surviving. He was remembering. He was learning.)
  [SWISH! WHIP! (Sound of precise, swift movements)]
  Jin Mu-Won (thinking): The basics... Father's teachings... 'The true martial artist finds strength not in technique, but in will.'
  (He practiced the fundamental forms, the forbidden techniques his father had left behind in fragmented scrolls and whispered memories. Each movement was a prayer, each strike a vow.)
  [WHOOSH! THWACK! (Impact of practice strike)]
  Jin Mu-Won (thinking): They took everything. My family, my home, my name. But they couldn't take my will. They couldn't take what flows in my blood.
  (The Central Plains Martial Alliance believed the Northern Blade was dead, buried under a mountain of snow and lies. They believed the last ember of the Northern Heavenly Sect had been extinguished.)
  Jin Mu-Won (thinking): They are wrong.
  [CRACKLE (Subtle energy)]
  (In the heart of the desolate North, a new blade was being forged, silent and unseen. A blade that would one day cut through the darkness and reclaim its legacy.)
  [WIND CHIMES (Distant, mournful)]

=== Hero Killer Episode 1 ===

  (The city of Astra. A beacon of hope, they say. A testament to human resilience, protected by those with extraordinary gifts.)
  (But even beacons cast long shadows. And in those shadows, the truth often hides.)
  Zero (thinking): Another day, another parade. Another hero basking in the manufactured adoration of the masses.
  [WIND WHISTLES]
  Zero (thinking): Captain Justice. The 'Invincible Shield of Astra.' More like the 'Invincible Ego.'
  [CROWD CHEERS (DISTANT)]
  Zero (thinking): He saves a cat from a tree, ignores the corporate fraud that bleeds the city dry. He stops a bank robbery, while his 'charity' funnels millions into offshore accounts.
  [CLICK. WHIRR.]
  Zero (thinking): My ocular implant zooms in. His smile is too wide, too practiced. The crowd roars. Sheep.
  Zero (thinking): They see the cape, the muscles, the 'hero.' I see the cracks in the facade. The vulnerabilities. The greed.
  [KEYBOARD CLACKS (SOFTLY)]
  Zero (thinking): Phase One: Infiltration complete. The drone is in position, disguised as a standard news feed unit. It's broadcasting a clean signal now.
  Zero (thinking): Soon, it will broadcast something else entirely.
  Captain Justice: My fellow citizens! It is an honor, as always, to stand before you. To be your shield, your sword, your... Justice!
  [CROWD ERUPTS IN APPLAUSE]
  Zero: Queue the feed. Now.
  [BEEP. STATIC FRITZ. (ON ALL PUBLIC SCREENS)]
  Zero (thinking): The crowd murmurs. Captain Justice looks annoyed. Good.
  Captain Justice: What is this? A technical glitch? Security, get this sorted!
  [VIDEO PLAYS (ON SCREENS) - A grainy recording of Captain Justice accepting a briefcase full of cash from a known crime boss.]
  Zero (thinking): The murmurs turn to gasps. Then angry shouts.
  Captain Justice: No! This is... this is doctored! A smear campaign! Don't believe it!
  [ANOTHER VIDEO PLAYS - Captain Justice laughing as a building collapses in the background, a small, non-powered civilian trapped, while he poses for a camera crew.]
  Zero (thinking): His face is pale. The 'Invincible Shield' is crumbling.
  [SIRENS WAIL (APPROACHING)]
  Zero (thinking): Time for my grand entrance. And his grand exit.
  [GRAPPLE HOOK FIRES. ZING!]
  (Zero descends from the rooftop, a dark silhouette against the setting sun, landing silently behind the now-panicked hero.)
  Captain Justice: Who... who are you?! This is a mistake! I'm Captain Justice!
  Zero: Not anymore.
  [ZAP! (A small, focused energy pulse from Zero's gauntlet)]
  Captain Justice: A-ARGH! My... my powers! They're... gone!
  Zero: Temporarily. Just long enough for the authorities to arrive. And for the public to see you for what you truly are.
  [THUD. (Captain Justice collapses)]
  Zero (thinking): Another hero falls. Another illusion shattered.
  Zero (thinking): This city doesn't need saviors. It needs an exterminator.
  (As police swarmed the scene, Zero vanished into the shadows, leaving behind a disgraced hero and a city questioning everything it thought it knew.)

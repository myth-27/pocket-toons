=== Roxana Episode 1 ===

  (The Agriche family. A name whispered with dread, a shadow cast over the continent. Within its gilded cages, life was cheap, and cruelty was currency. To be born an Agriche was to inherit a legacy of blood, madness, and power.)
  Roxana (thinking): (thought) This world... it's the same one from that twisted, dark novel. The one where I, Roxana Agriche, am destined to be a villainess, a beautiful monster, a mere stepping stone for the true protagonist.
  Roxana (thinking): (thought) And my fate? A gruesome, agonizing end, orchestrated by the very male lead I’m supposed to torment. Cassis Pedelian. The hero who will rise from the ashes of our cruelty and burn us all.
  Jeremy: Hahaha! Look at it squirm! Is that all you've got, you pathetic worm? Dance for me!
  [WHIP! CRACK! (A pained whimper)]
  Roxana (thinking): (thought) Jeremy. My younger brother. A monster in the making, even at this tender age. He's torturing a captured beast, a rare golden stag. A common, sickening pastime here at the Agriche estate.
  Roxana (thinking): (thought) I remember this scene from the novel. It's a precursor to his escalating sadism, his descent into pure, unadulterated evil. If I don't change things, I'll be just another one of his victims, or worse, an accomplice, dragged down with him.
  Jeremy: Brother, sister! Come join the fun! This one's almost broken! Its eyes are begging!
  Roxana (thinking): (thought) No. I refuse to be a pawn in this family's grotesque game of death. I will survive. And to do that, I need to save Cassis Pedelian, the man destined to destroy us.
  Roxana (thinking): (thought) The novel said he was brought to the Agriche estate recently. Imprisoned, tortured, slowly driven to madness... until he finally escapes and brings utter ruin upon us all. A beautiful, bloody revenge.
  Roxana (thinking): (thought) My survival hinges on turning him into my ally, not my enemy. But how does one tame a rabid dog, especially one destined to bite the hand that feeds it? It requires a delicate touch, a venomous charm.
  Roxana: Jeremy, dear brother. You'll tire yourself out. Save some energy for father's next 'lesson'. You wouldn't want to disappoint him, would you?
  Jeremy: Roxana? You're no fun. Always so boring, always so cold.
  Roxana: Perhaps. But a dull blade lasts longer than a sharpened one that's used too often. And a cold heart often outlives a passionate one, especially here.
  Jeremy: Hmph. Whatever. This thing is dead anyway. I'm bored.
  [THUD! (The stag's body hits the ground)]
  Roxana (thinking): (thought) He's distracted, his attention already shifting to his next cruel whim. Good. This is my chance.
  (Roxana discreetly slips away from the blood-stained courtyard, her silken dress barely rustling as she navigates the labyrinthine, shadow-drenched corridors of the mansion. Her destination: the dungeons.)
  Roxana (thinking): (thought) The dungeon. It's rumored to be beneath the west wing. A place where even the shadows have teeth, where screams are swallowed whole.
  [CREAK... (An old door groans open)]
  Roxana (thinking): (thought) The air grows colder, heavier with each step. The stench of iron, decay, and despair is overwhelming. He's here. Cassis Pedelian.
  Guard: Lady Roxana? What brings a delicate flower like yourself to this... unpleasant part of the estate?
  Roxana: Just a stroll, good sir. My thoughts often wander to the more... secluded corners of our home. Is there anything of interest down here? Any new 'acquisitions' for father's collection?
  Guard: Nothing for a lady's eyes, I assure you. Just the usual rabble. And one particularly stubborn piece of merchandise.
  Roxana: Oh? I heard father brought in a rather 'special' guest recently. A rare specimen, perhaps? One from a rather prominent family?
  Guard: Ah, you mean the Pedelian. Yes, he's... quite resilient. Still hasn't broken, despite our best efforts.
  Roxana (thinking): (thought) Resilient. That's an understatement. He's a future emperor, a man who will raze this entire family, this entire empire, to the ground.
  Roxana: May I have a peek? Just a quick glance. I'm curious about father's new 'toy'. I promise not to disturb him.
  Guard: Well... it's against orders, Lady Roxana. But... very well. Just a moment. Don't touch anything.
  [CLANG! RATTLE! (A heavy peephole cover slides open)]
  (The guard opens a small, grated peephole. Roxana peers through, her golden eyes, usually so serene, now sharp and calculating as they adjust to the dim, oppressive light within the cell.)
  Roxana (thinking): (thought) There he is. Cassis Pedelian. Even in chains, even bruised and battered, his eyes hold a terrifying defiance. A golden gaze that promises vengeance, a storm waiting to break.
  Roxana (thinking): (thought) He's younger than I imagined, perhaps only a few years older than me, but the intensity is already there. The novel described him as a beautiful, dangerous beast. They weren't wrong.
  Roxana (thinking): (thought) This is the man I need to save. This is the man who will decide my fate, and the fate of the Agriche family. And I will make sure he chooses wisely. I will make him my golden butterfly.
  Roxana: Interesting. Very interesting indeed. He truly is a magnificent specimen.
  [CLICK! (The peephole cover slams shut)]
  Guard: That's enough, Lady Roxana. Wouldn't want you to catch a chill, or for him to get any ideas.
  Roxana: Of course. Thank you, good sir. You've been most... accommodating. I shall remember your kindness.
  Roxana (thinking): (thought) My first move. A simple observation. But the game has begun. And I, Roxana Agriche, intend to win. I will weave a web of deceit and charm so intricate, even fate itself won't escape.

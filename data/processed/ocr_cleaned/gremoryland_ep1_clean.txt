=== GremoryLand Episode 1 ===

  (The flickering fluorescent light hummed, casting long, sickly shadows across the stack of unpaid bills. Another ramen packet, another day closer to eviction.)
  JI-HOON (thinking): (Thought) This can't be my life. Not forever. There has to be a way out.
  [RUSTLE]
  (Among the junk mail and final notices, a sleek, black envelope stood out. No return address. Just a single, embossed crimson 'G'.)
  JI-HOON (thinking): (Thought) What's this? Some kind of scam?
  [RIP]
  JI-HOON: (Reading aloud, voice laced with skepticism) 'GremoryLand invites you to a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity. A chance to redefine your future. Transportation provided. Reply by midnight.'
  JI-HOON (thinking): (Thought) 'Redefine my future'? Sounds like a cult initiation. Or a human trafficking ring. Still... what if...?
  (Desperation is a powerful motivator. By midnight, Ji-hoon had replied. By dawn, a luxury car idled outside his dilapidated building.)
  [ENGINE HUM]
  JI-HOON (thinking): (Thought) This is insane. I'm actually doing this. For a 'chance to redefine my future'.
  (The drive was long, taking them further and further from the city's familiar sprawl, into a landscape of dense, ancient forest. No cell signal. No sign of civilization.)
  JI-HOON (thinking): (Thought) Okay, this is officially creepy. No other cars on the road for miles.
  [WHOOSH]
  (Suddenly, the trees parted, revealing a colossal wrought-iron gate. Beyond it, a sprawling, gothic theme park. Towers, spires, and a massive, grinning jester's face carved into the main archway. GremoryLand.)
  JI-HOON (thinking): (Thought) It's... magnificent. And utterly terrifying. It feels like a dream, or a nightmare.
  [CREAK]
  (The gate swung open silently. The car glided through. The air grew colder, heavy with an unnatural stillness.)
  MR. SMILES: Welcome, welcome! To GremoryLand! We've been expecting you, Ji-hoon!
  (A figure emerged from the shadows of the main archway. Tall, slender, wearing a pristine, old-fashioned ringmaster's suit. His face was hidden behind a permanent, unsettlingly wide smile mask, painted with rosy cheeks and vacant eyes.)
  JI-HOON: Uh, hello. You can call me Ji-hoon.
  MR. SMILES: Of course, of course! I am Mr. Smiles, your humble guide. And you, dear guest, are one of the chosen few! The lucky ones!
  JI-HOON (thinking): (Thought) 'Chosen few'? 'Lucky ones'? This guy is giving me serious bad vibes. His voice is too cheerful.
  MR. SMILES: Come, come! The others are waiting. We have so much fun planned for you! Challenges, games, and rewards beyond your wildest dreams!
  (He gestured towards a grand plaza where about twenty other individuals stood, looking equally bewildered and nervous. One young woman, clutching her bag, caught Ji-hoon's eye. Her face was pale, her eyes wide with undisguised terror.)
  JI-HOON (thinking): (Thought) What's wrong with her? She looks like she's seen a ghost.
  MR. SMILES: Remember, dear guests, in GremoryLand, there are only two rules: participate wholeheartedly, and never, ever, try to leave.
  (The last words hung in the air, the cheerful tone suddenly dripping with a chilling finality. Ji-hoon felt a cold dread seep into his bones. He glanced back at the massive gate, now firmly closed. A small, almost imperceptible red light pulsed above it.)
  JI-HOON (thinking): (Thought) Never try to leave? What have I gotten myself into?
  [CLICK]
  (A heavy, metallic *click* echoed from somewhere deep within the park, like a lock engaging.)

=== Shotgun Boy Episode 1 ===

  (The day started like any other. The usual morning announcements. The usual boredom of first period. Then, the screams.)
  HAE-MIN: —Just stay quiet, Jisu. Please.
  JISU: (sobbing, barely a whisper) I want my mom… I want to go home…
  HAE-MIN (thinking): (thought) *The sounds… they’re getting closer. Thumping. Growling. Not human.*
  [THUMP. THUMP. THUMP.]
  [SCRAPE. A low GROWL.]
  HAE-MIN (thinking): (thought) *The classroom door… it’s not going to hold.*
  [CRACK! SPLINTER!]
  JISU: (a choked gasp)
  (A grotesque hand, fingers unnaturally long and twisted, burst through the splintered wood. Then, a face.)
  [SHATTER! (The door rips from its hinges)]
  MR. KIM (TRANSFORMED): (A guttural, wet ROAR)
  HAE-MIN (thinking): (thought) *Mr. Kim? No… it can’t be…*
  JISU: (screams, pure terror)
  HAE-MIN: Run! Jisu, run!
  [LUNGE! GRAB!]
  JISU: NO! HELP ME! HAE-MIN!
  HAE-MIN (thinking): (thought) *Frozen. Why am I frozen? Move!*
  [WHOOSH! (Hae-min grabs a fire extinguisher)]
  [HISS! (Chemical spray)]
  MR. KIM (TRANSFORMED): (A pained SNARL)
  HAE-MIN: (gasping) Go! Get out!
  [SCRAMBLE! (Jisu escapes)]
  (The hallway was a nightmare. More of them. Students, teachers… twisted into something monstrous. The air thick with the smell of blood and fear.)
  [SCREAMS! DISTANT ROARS! WET RIPPING SOUNDS!]
  HAE-MIN (thinking): (thought) *I can’t help them. I can’t. Just run.*
  [PANT! PANT! THUMP-THUMP-THUMP! (Hae-min’s frantic heartbeat)]
  HAE-MIN (thinking): (thought) *Another classroom. Lock the door. Hide.*
  [CLICK! (The lock engages)]
  HAE-MIN: (whispering, trembling) What… what are these things? What’s happening?
  (He peered out the window. The city, once vibrant, was now a canvas of smoke and fire. Sirens wailed, then abruptly died, replaced by an eerie silence.)
  [SIRENS (fading to static), CRASH (distant explosion)]
  HAE-MIN (thinking): (thought) *The world… it’s gone. Everything is gone.*
  (His eyes fell on a discarded poster, half-torn from the wall. A hunting rifle, advertised for a local sporting goods store. A weapon.)
  HAE-MIN (thinking): (thought) *I need… something. Anything.*
  HAE-MIN: (curling into a ball, shaking) I just want to go home…
  [DISTANT ROAR. A low, continuous GROAN from somewhere in the school.]

=== Noblesse, Episode 10 ===

SUMMARY: Frankenstein reports to his Master about the mysterious circumstances of his awakening and the significant changes in the world, particularly the rise of humans. He also mentions a problem with his own power, which became apparent during a math class taught by the impressive Gatekeeper.

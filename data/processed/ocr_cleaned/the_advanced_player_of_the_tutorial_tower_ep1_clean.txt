=== The Advanced Player of the Tutorial Tower Episode 1 ===

  (The air was thick with the scent of fear and ozone. Another beginning. Or was it just another end?)
  Kim Hyunsung (thinking): How many times have I stood here? How many 'first floors' have I endured?
  Kim Hyunsung (thinking): A thousand? Ten thousand? The faces change, the screams echo differently, but the tower... it always remains.
  SYSTEM: [Welcome, Player, to the Tutorial Tower!]
  SYSTEM: [Your journey to become a true Awakened begins now.]
  SYSTEM: [Current Objective: Clear Floor 1 - The Goblin's Den.]
  Kim Hyunsung (thinking): The same old lines. The same old lies. A cruel mockery of hope for the unsuspecting.
  Kim Hyunsung (thinking): Look at them. Fresh meat. Wide-eyed, trembling, clutching their rusty swords and dull axes as if they're holding onto life itself.
  New Player A: G-Goblins?! They're everywhere! What are we supposed to do?!
  New Player B: Someone help! My shield just... shattered! This isn't fair!
  [SCREAMS]
  [CLANG! CRACK! (Of a weak weapon breaking)]
  Kim Hyunsung (thinking): This 'tutorial' is a cruel joke. A loop of despair for everyone but me. For them, it's a death sentence. For me... it's just another Tuesday.
  [GOBLIN SNARL (A particularly large goblin charges)]
  New Player C: Agh! It's coming for me! I can't move!
  ((He sighs, a barely perceptible movement, his gaze cold and distant.))
  Kim Hyunsung (thinking): Enough. Let's get this over with. The faster I clear, the faster I can observe the changes.
  [WHOOSH! (Hyunsung moves, a blur of motion, too fast for the eye to follow)]
  [THWACK! (A goblin's head explodes into green mist)]
  [CRACK! (Another goblin's neck snaps with a sickening sound)]
  New Player A: W-what was that?! Did... did someone just move?
  New Player B: The goblins... they're all falling! Who's doing this?!
  Kim Hyunsung (thinking): They don't even see me. Good. Less hassle. My presence only complicates things for now.
  [SQUELCH (The final goblin falls, its body dissolving)]
  SYSTEM: [You have defeated the Goblin Chieftain!]
  SYSTEM: [Floor 1 has been cleared!]
  SYSTEM: [You have gained 500 EXP.]
  SYSTEM: [You have leveled up!]
  SYSTEM: [You have acquired the skill 'Basic Sword Mastery'.]
  Kim Hyunsung (thinking): Basic Sword Mastery. Again. The irony never gets old, I suppose. A skill I mastered millennia ago.
  Kim Hyunsung (thinking): My true skills are beyond their comprehension. Beyond this 'tutorial' itself. They are forged in countless deaths and rebirths.
  SYSTEM: [All players will now be moved to Floor 2.]
  [WHOOSH (A bright flash of light engulfs the players, transporting them)]
  Kim Hyunsung (thinking): Another floor. Another step closer. This time, I won't fail. I can't.
  Kim Hyunsung (thinking): This time, I will break the loop. I will escape this prison that has held me captive for so long.
  Kim Hyunsung (thinking): And I'll make sure no one else gets trapped here with me. Not again.
  (His eyes, ancient and weary, held a flicker of cold, unyielding determination. The advanced player had begun his final ascent.)

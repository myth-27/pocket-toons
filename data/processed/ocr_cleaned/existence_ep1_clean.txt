=== Existence Episode 1 ===

  (Death. It's a sensation I've grown intimately familiar with. A fleeting darkness, a brief respite from the endless cycle.)
  (But it's never truly an end. Merely a transition. A reset. And then, the awakening.)
  Ryu Han: Ugh...
  Ryu Han (thinking): The first sensation is always the most telling. A dull ache, a strange pressure... and the overwhelming scent of damp earth and something acrid.
  [GROAN]
  Ryu Han (thinking): My eyes flutter open. Or rather, my new eyes. They're not human. A murky green hue, with vertical pupils that struggle to adjust to the dim light.
  Ryu Han (thinking): I try to move. My limbs feel heavy, uncoordinated. This body... it's small. And green. Rough, leathery skin. Short, tusklike protrusions from my lower jaw.
  Ryu Han (thinking): An orcling. A newborn, by the feel of it. Pathetic. But then again, I've started as far worse.
  [SCRAPE]
  Ryu Han (thinking): I push myself up, my new muscles protesting. The cave floor is cold, rocky. I'm in some kind of crude nest, surrounded by other, equally small and grunting creatures.
  [GRUNT GRUNT]
  Ryu Han (thinking): They're my siblings, I presume. All equally helpless. Instinct tells me to stay, to wait for a parent. But instinct has never been my master.
  Ryu Han (thinking): My memories... they're all here. The countless lives. The dragon's scales, the demon's fury, the human's cunning, the elf's grace. A tapestry of existence woven through millennia.
  Ryu Han (thinking): And with each life, a fragment of power, a piece of knowledge, is retained. It's a burden, yes. But also my greatest weapon.
  Ryu Han (thinking): This body is weak. Fragile. But the essence within it? That remains indomitable.
  [SNIFF SNIFF]
  Ryu Han (thinking): A foul odor, stronger than the rest, wafts from deeper within the cave. A larger presence. The 'mother' or 'father', perhaps. Or simply another, stronger orc.
  Ryu Han (thinking): Regardless, this place is temporary. Survival is paramount. Growth is inevitable.
  Ryu Han (thinking): I need to move. To find food, to find strength. To begin the climb, once more.
  [SHUFFLE]
  Ryu Han (thinking): My small, clawed hands grip the cold rock. My new legs, still wobbly, propel me forward. Away from the nest. Towards the unknown.
  Ryu Han (thinking): The world is vast, dangerous, and utterly indifferent. Just as it always has been.
  [DRIP DRIP]
  Ryu Han (thinking): And I, Ryu Han, am its most persistent visitor.

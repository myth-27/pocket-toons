=== Operation: True Love Episode 1 ===

  (They say true love is a miracle. A serendipitous encounter, a spark that ignites into an everlasting flame.)
  (But from where I stand, it looks more like a meticulously crafted performance.)
  Su-ae (thinking): (thought) Exhibit A: Han Eun-hye and Lee Jin-woo. The 'it' couple of Seoyeon High. Always perfectly coordinated, always perfectly affectionate.
  Eun-hye: Jin-woo, your hair is a little messy here. Let me fix it.
  Jin-woo: Ah, you're always so attentive, Eun-hye. What would I do without you?
  [CHIRP CHIRP]
  Su-ae (thinking): (thought) See? Textbook. Every gesture, every word, perfectly optimized for maximum romantic impact. Probably for the benefit of the other students watching.
  Eun-hye: Su-ae! Stop lurking in the shadows!
  Su-ae: W-what? I wasn't lurking! I was... observing local wildlife.
  Jin-woo: (chuckles) Su-ae, always the eccentric one.
  Eun-hye: Don't mind her. She just doesn't understand true love yet. She's too busy analyzing everything.
  Su-ae: I understand it perfectly. It's a complex algorithm of social cues, emotional manipulation, and a dash of genuine affection to keep things interesting.
  [SWISH]
  Eun-hye: You're impossible! Come on, let's get to class.
  (Later, in the bustling hallway, my 'observations' continued. Not of couples, but of the general chaos that is high school life.)
  [RUMBLE]
  Su-ae (thinking): (thought) Another day, another wave of hormonal teenagers crashing into each other. Truly a spectacle.
  [THUMP]
  Su-ae: Oof!
  Do-hwa: Ah, sorry! My bad. Didn't see you there.
  Su-ae (thinking): (thought) Oh, great. The 'Prince of Seoyeon High', Go Do-hwa. Just what I needed. His fan club is probably already glaring at me.
  Do-hwa: Wait, you're... Su-ae, right? Han Su-ae?
  Su-ae: That's me. What do you want, Prince Charming?
  Do-hwa: (leans in conspiratorially) I heard you're the best at this kind of thing. The 'Love Doctor' of our school, they call you.
  Su-ae: Who calls me that? Eun-hye? She's exaggerating.
  Do-hwa: No, no. Everyone knows you're secretly a genius at understanding people's hearts. Even if you act like you don't care.
  Su-ae (thinking): (thought) My heart just did a weird little flutter. Why? Because the most popular guy in school is saying I'm a 'genius'? No, it's just the surprise.
  Do-hwa: Look, I need your help. Desperately.
  Su-ae: Help with what?
  Do-hwa: I... I want to fall in love. And I need you to orchestrate it.
  [GASP]
  Su-ae (thinking): (thought) He wants me to... what?! Orchestrate his love life? This is a new level of absurd. And yet... the challenge. The sheer, ridiculous, fascinating challenge.
  (And just like that, my perfectly cynical, perfectly detached world of observation was about to be turned upside down. Operation: True Love had officially begun.)

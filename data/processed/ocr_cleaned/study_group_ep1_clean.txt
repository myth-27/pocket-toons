=== Study Group Episode 1 ===

  (Yonggang High. A name that strikes fear into the hearts of students across the city. Not for its academic prowess, but for its… unique student body.)
  Yoon Gamin: This is it. Yonggang High.
  Yoon Gamin (thinking): My last chance. My only chance.
  [MURMURS. JEERS. DISTANT SHOUTS.]
  Yoon Gamin (thinking): (Looking around at the intimidating students) The rumors weren't exaggerated. Every single person here looks like they could snap me in half.
  Yoon Gamin (thinking): But I can't back down. Not now. I promised Mom. I promised myself.
  (Yoon Gamin. A scrawny, unassuming boy with one goal: to get into university. A goal that seems laughably impossible in a place like Yonggang.)
  Yoon Gamin (thinking): Okay, first things first. Find a quiet spot. Somewhere I can actually… study.
  [THUMP! CRASH!]
  Bully 1: Hey, watch it, nerd!
  Yoon Gamin: (Stumbling back as a desk is violently overturned) S-sorry!
  Bully 2: Look at this guy. Fresh meat.
  Bully 1: What's with the glasses, four-eyes? You think you're smart or something?
  Yoon Gamin: N-no! I just… I was looking for the library.
  Bully 2: The library? Hah! What's that, some kind of club for losers?
  Bully 1: You hear that, Minho? He wants to read books! In Yonggang!
  [LAUGHTER. SNICKERS.]
  Yoon Gamin (thinking): (My heart is pounding. This is exactly what I was afraid of. But I can't fight them. I can't afford to get expelled.)
  Bully 1: Let's see what kind of books you're into, huh?
  [RIP! PAGES TEAR.]
  Yoon Gamin: Hey! My textbook!
  Bully 2: Oops. Guess you won't be needing that anymore.
  Yoon Gamin (thinking): (Rage flares inside me. This is my future they're tearing apart. But I have to hold it in. I have to.)
  Yoon Gamin: Please… just leave me alone.
  Bully 1: And what if we don't, huh? What are you gonna do about it, little study bug?
  [SHOVE! Gamin stumbles.]
  Yoon Gamin (thinking): (My hands are clenched. My knuckles are white. This isn't how it's supposed to be. I just want to study.)
  Bully 1: Look at him, shaking like a leaf. Pathetic.
  Bully 2: Let's go. He's not even worth the effort.
  [FOOTSTEPS RECEDE. LAUGHTER FADES.]
  Yoon Gamin (thinking): (Collapsing against the wall, picking up the torn pages) Damn it… damn it all!
  Yoon Gamin (thinking): This is Yonggang. This is my reality now. But I won't let them win.
  Yoon Gamin (thinking): I came here to study, and I will study. No matter what it takes. No matter who stands in my way.
  (With a torn textbook and a burning resolve, Yoon Gamin's journey at Yonggang High had just begun. A journey where 'study' might mean more than just books.)

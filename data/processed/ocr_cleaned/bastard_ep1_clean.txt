=== Bastard Episode 1 ===

  (My world is a constant blur, a muted echo of what others see and hear. One eye, a glass orb. The other, clouded by fear.)
  (Every morning, the same ritual. The cold porcelain of the sink. The careful placement.)
  [CLICK]
  Jin Seon (thinking): (thought) Another day. Another performance.
  [CREAK]
  (The floorboards groan under my weight, each sound a potential betrayal. He has ears everywhere.)
  Dongsoo: Good morning, Jin. Sleep well?
  (His voice, smooth as polished stone. Always. Too smooth.)
  Jin Seon: Yes, Father.
  Jin Seon (thinking): (thought) No. I never sleep well. Not in this house.
  Dongsoo: Pancakes today. Your favorite.
  [SIZZLE]
  Jin Seon (thinking): (thought) He knows my favorites. He knows everything. That's the problem.
  (The syrup drips, thick and sweet. A sickly sweetness that coats my throat.)
  Dongsoo: You're quiet this morning. Something on your mind?
  Jin Seon: No, Father. Just... thinking about school.
  Dongsoo: Good. Focus on your studies. You're a smart boy, Jin. You just need to... apply yourself.
  Jin Seon (thinking): (thought) Apply myself to what? To being your accomplice? Your witness?
  [CHIRP (birds outside)]
  (Outside, the world pretends to be normal. Inside, the air is thick with unspoken rules.)
  (School is no escape. Just another cage, with different bars.)
  Bully 1: Hey, one-eye! Still staring at nothing?
  [SHOVE]
  Jin Seon (thinking): (thought) Just ignore them. Don't cause trouble. Trouble always finds its way back home.
  Bully 2: What's wrong, mute? Can't even talk back?
  (I keep my head down, my gaze fixed on the cracked pavement. Invisible. That's the goal.)
  (Evening. The house is quiet again. Too quiet.)
  [DOOR CREAKS OPEN]
  Jin Seon (thinking): (thought) He's back. I can feel it.
  Dongsoo: Jin? Are you home?
  Jin Seon: Yes, Father. In my room.
  [FOOTSTEPS (slow, deliberate)]
  Dongsoo: The news is reporting another missing person. A young woman, just like the others.
  Jin Seon (thinking): (thought) My blood runs cold. He's talking about it. Right in front of me.
  Dongsoo: Such a shame. The world can be a dangerous place, can't it, Jin?
  Jin Seon: Yes, Father.
  Dongsoo: But some dangers... are closer than you think.
  [THUMP (a muffled sound from downstairs)]
  Jin Seon (thinking): (thought) What was that? No, don't ask. Don't look. Don't know.
  Dongsoo: I need you to do something for me, Jin.
  Jin Seon (thinking): (thought) Here it comes. The request. The order. The complicity.
  Dongsoo: There's a package in the basement. It needs to be... disposed of. Carefully.
  (My breath catches. A package. In the basement. I know what that means.)
  Dongsoo: You know the drill, don't you, son? Don't leave any traces.
  Jin Seon (thinking): (thought) My hands are shaking. My stomach churns. I'm a monster, just like him. A bastard.
  [DRIP (slow, rhythmic, from an unseen source)]
  Dongsoo: Good boy. You always were so good at following instructions.
  (The darkness of the basement calls. And I, the 'good boy,' have no choice but to answer.)

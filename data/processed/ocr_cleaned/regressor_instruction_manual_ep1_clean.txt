=== Regressor Instruction Manual Episode 1 ===

  (The world ended not with a bang, but with a flash. One moment, I was scrolling through my phone, the next... this.)
  Lee Kiyoung (thinking): What the hell? Where am I? This isn't my apartment. It looks like... some kind of ruin? And why is everyone else looking so confused?
  (A translucent window flickered before my eyes.)
  Lee Kiyoung (thinking): Status Window? Seriously? Is this some kind of sick joke?
  Lee Kiyoung (thinking): [Name: Lee Kiyoung] [Title: None] [Age: 25] [Occupation: None] [Stats] [Strength: 05] [Agility: 06] [Stamina: 07] [Magic Power: 01] [Luck: 08] [Divine Power: 00] [Endurance: 09] [Intelligence: 10] [Talents: None] ...Are you kidding me?! My stats are worse than a common house cat! Magic Power 1?! I'm practically a negative number!
  (Panic began to set in, but a lifetime of mediocrity had taught me one thing: adapt or die. And dying wasn't on my to-do list.)
  Lee Kiyoung (thinking): Okay, okay, calm down. This is clearly some kind of fantasy world summoning. Everyone else looks just as lost. But... wait. Some of them look different. Stronger. Like they were *meant* for this.
  (My eyes scanned the group, quickly identifying the archetypes. The muscle-bound jock, the graceful swordswoman, the quiet, brooding type...)
  Lee Kiyoung (thinking): And then there's me. The cannon fodder. The guy who dies first to show how dangerous the world is. No, not me. I refuse to be that guy.
  [GRRRRROOOOWL!]
  Woman 1: What was that?!
  Man 1: Stay back! Something's coming!
  [CLANG! THUD!]
  (A grotesque, green-skinned creature, barely taller than a child but wielding a rusty club, stumbled into the clearing. A goblin.)
  Lee Kiyoung (thinking): Oh, for crying out loud. A goblin. The tutorial monster. This is exactly how it starts.
  Man 2: Get away from me!
  [SCREAM!]
  (The goblin lunged, its club swinging wildly. Chaos erupted. People scattered, some tripping over themselves in fear.)
  Lee Kiyoung (thinking): This is it. This is where I die. Unless...
  (My gaze snapped to the quiet, brooding man I'd noticed earlier. He hadn't panicked. His eyes were sharp, assessing. He moved with a practiced ease.)
  [SWISH!]
  (Before anyone else could react, he was there. A simple, elegant movement.)
  [SLICE!]
  Goblin: G-GAAAH?!
  [THUD!]
  (The goblin's head rolled, its body collapsing to the ground. Silence fell, broken only by ragged breaths.)
  Lee Kiyoung (thinking): Holy crap. He just... one-shotted it. No hesitation. No fear. And that sword... it wasn't just any sword. It looked like it had seen battle.
  Kim Hyunsung: Everyone, calm down. More will come. We need to stick together.
  Lee Kiyoung (thinking): Kim Hyunsung. That's his name. I saw it on his status window earlier, briefly. Strength: EX. Agility: EX. Luck: EX. Wait... EX?! That's not normal. That's... regressor level.
  (A cold, calculating realization dawned on me. This wasn't just some random strong guy. This was *the* strong guy. The one who knew what was coming. The one who had done this before.)
  Lee Kiyoung (thinking): He's the protagonist. He's been here, done that, and probably died doing it. He knows the future. He knows the dangers. And he's my ticket. My lifeline.
  Kim Hyunsung: Is everyone alright? Does anyone have any injuries?
  Lee Kiyoung (thinking): My stats are trash. My combat ability is non-existent. I'm literally the weakest link. But I have one thing they don't: a brain that knows how to scheme. And a complete lack of shame.
  Lee Kiyoung (thinking): Okay, Kiyoung. Time to activate the 'Lee Kiyoung Survival Manual.' Step one: Identify the strongest. Step two: Attach yourself to them like a limpet. Step three: Make yourself indispensable, even if it means lying through your teeth.
  Lee Kiyoung: (A little shaky, but trying to sound brave) I-I think everyone's okay, sir! Thank you for saving us! That was incredible!
  (Kim Hyunsung turned his gaze towards me, a flicker of surprise in his eyes. Most people were still too stunned to speak.)
  Kim Hyunsung: It was nothing. Just a goblin.
  Lee Kiyoung (thinking): "Just a goblin," he says. For him, maybe. For me, that was a death sentence. But I need to play my part. The grateful, slightly timid, but observant survivor.
  Lee Kiyoung: No, really! You were so fast! I... I don't think any of us would have made it without you. You seem to know what you're doing.
  Kim Hyunsung: I... I've just been in a few tough situations before.
  Lee Kiyoung (thinking): "A few tough situations." Yeah, like dying and coming back to life. He's trying to be humble. Good. That means he's probably a good person. Even better for me.
  Lee Kiyoung: (Taking a step closer, trying to look earnest) Sir, if... if it's not too much trouble, could we perhaps stick close to you? We're all so lost, and you seem to be the only one who knows how to handle this. We'll try not to be a burden!
  (Kim Hyunsung looked at me, then at the terrified faces of the others. A sigh escaped him, almost imperceptible.)
  Kim Hyunsung: Alright. But you need to follow my instructions precisely. No heroics. Understand?
  Lee Kiyoung (thinking): Bingo. Hook, line, and sinker. The first step is complete. Now, to cement my position.
  Lee Kiyoung: Yes, sir! Absolutely! We'll do whatever you say!
  (My heart pounded, not from fear, but from a surge of triumph. I was weak, pathetic, and utterly useless in a fight. But I was alive. And I had just found my golden ticket to survival in this hellish new world. The Regressor Instruction Manual had officially begun.)

=== Reality Quest Episode 1 ===

  (The rooftop of Seonjin High. For most, a place for quiet lunches or stolen moments. For Ha Do-yoon, it was a stage. A stage for his daily, inescapable torment.)
  Kim Min-woo: Look at this pathetic worm, trying to hide again. Did you think we wouldn't find you, Do-yoon?
  Bully 1: He's always so good at playing the victim. Makes me sick.
  Ha Do-yoon (thinking): (thought) My heart pounds like a trapped bird. Every day, it's the same. Just endure it. Just endure...
  Ha Do-yoon: I... I have it. The money you asked for.
  [CRUMPLE]
  Kim Min-woo: (Snatching the crumpled bills) Is this a joke? This isn't even enough for a single meal, you scumbag!
  [SLAP!]
  Ha Do-yoon (thinking): (thought) The sharp sting on my cheek. The familiar taste of blood. My head rings. Why can't I fight back? Why am I so utterly, hopelessly weak?
  Bully 2: He needs to learn his place. Again.
  [KICK! THUD!]
  Ha Do-yoon (thinking): (thought) My vision blurs. The pain is a dull, all-consuming roar. My body feels like lead. I wish... I wish I could just disappear. Or better yet... make *them* disappear.
  (As Do-yoon lay crumpled on the grimy concrete, his consciousness fading, a strange, ethereal light flickered before his eyes.)
  [SHIMMER! BEEP!]
  ([Welcome, Player. Reality Quest has been activated.])
  Ha Do-yoon (thinking): (thought) What... what is this? Am I dreaming? Is this some kind of concussion hallucination?
  ([Player Ha Do-yoon. Current Status: Helpless. HP: 12/100.])
  Kim Min-woo: What's he staring at? Still got some fight left in him, huh? Let's fix that.
  [SWING!]
  ([New Quest: Survive the Bullying. Reward: Basic Combat Skill 'Punch'.])
  Ha Do-yoon (thinking): (thought) Survive? Punch? What good is that? I can't even stand up! He's going to hit me again!
  ([Tutorial: To use a skill, focus your intent and activate. Current Skill: Punch (Lvl 1).])
  Kim Min-woo: Time to put you in your place, Do-yoon!
  Ha Do-yoon (thinking): (thought) No... not again. I can't take this anymore. If this is real... if this is my only chance... I have to try. I have to!
  ([Skill 'Punch' available. Do you wish to activate?])
  Ha Do-yoon (thinking): (thought) YES! YES, I DO!
  [FWOOSH! (A faint, almost imperceptible aura surrounds Do-yoon's trembling fist)]
  Kim Min-woo: What the hell is he doing?
  (For the first time in years, Ha Do-yoon didn't just cower. He struggled, pushed himself up, and with a desperate, raw surge of will, threw his fist forward.)
  [THWACK!]
  Kim Min-woo: ARGH! What was that?!
  [STUMBLE!]
  ([Skill 'Punch' used. Damage dealt: 5. Opponent surprised. Quest updated: Survive the Bullying (1/1). Reward: Basic Combat Skill 'Punch' acquired.])
  Ha Do-yoon (thinking): (thought) It actually... worked? I actually hit him? I... I felt something. A tiny spark. A flicker of hope.
  (The first step had been taken. The game had begun.)

=== The Last Golden Child Episode 1 ===

  (The city of Neo-Seoul hummed with a restless energy, a constant symphony of ambition and anxiety. For most, it was just home. For me, it was a cage.)
  JAE-HYUN (thought) (thinking): Another day. Another year of trying to blend in. Of pretending I don't feel… this.
  [CLATTER]
  MIN-JI: Jae-hyun! You okay? You almost dropped your tray again.
  JAE-HYUN: Ah, Min-ji. Yeah, just… lost in thought.
  MIN-JI: You're always lost in thought. What's on your mind today? The upcoming history exam? Or that new 'Awakened' incident downtown?
  JAE-HYUN (thought) (thinking): The Awakened. They're everywhere now. People with powers, heroes, villains… I just want to be normal.
  JAE-HYUN: Just… the usual. Nothing exciting.
  MIN-JI: Well, try to focus. Mr. Kim's pop quiz is no joke.
  [RUMBLE (distant)]
  JAE-HYUN (thought) (thinking): That tremor… it's not normal. Not for a city this stable.
  [CRASH (closer, louder)]
  STUDENT 1: What was that?!
  STUDENT 2: It sounded like it came from the street!
  [SCREAMS (outside)]
  JAE-HYUN (thought) (thinking): The tingling… it's back. Stronger this time. Like my blood is boiling.
  TEACHER: Everyone, stay calm! Stay inside!
  [EXPLOSION (shaking the building)]
  MIN-JI: The windows! They're cracking!
  (A monstrous figure, cloaked in jagged shadows, hovered outside, tearing chunks from the adjacent building. Debris rained down, threatening to shatter the classroom's reinforced glass.)
  JAE-HYUN (thought) (thinking): No. Not here. Not Min-ji. Not them.
  [SHATTER (glass beginning to give)]
  JAE-HYUN: GET DOWN!
  JAE-HYUN (thought) (thinking): My hands… they moved on their own. A warmth, then a searing heat. A light.
  [WHOOSH! (intense, golden energy)]
  (From Jae-hyun's outstretched palms, a blinding, golden energy shield erupted, slamming into the incoming debris and deflecting it with impossible force.)
  [CRACKLE (energy dissipating)]
  MIN-JI: J-Jae-hyun? What was that…?
  JAE-HYUN (thought) (thinking): My entire body aches, but… it worked. I did that. This isn't just a tingling anymore. This is… real.
  ROGUE AWAKENED: Hmph. A new nuisance? You think a little light can stop me?!
  JAE-HYUN (thought) (thinking): The 'Golden Child'… that old legend my grandmother used to whisper about. It can't be… can it?
  [THUMP (Jae-hyun's racing heartbeat)]
  (As the monstrous Awakened prepared another attack, Jae-hyun stared at his hands, a golden aura still faintly shimmering around them. His life of blending in was over. His true nature had just been violently awakened.)

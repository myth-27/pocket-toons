=== Suitor Armor Episode 1 ===

  (In the grand halls of the Eldrin Kingdom, life for a handmaiden like me, Lucia, is a tapestry of routine. Polishing silverware, mending gowns, serving tea...)
  (And, of course, witnessing the endless parade of suitors vying for Princess Aurelia's hand.)
  Lucia (thinking): (thought) Another one. He looks like he's swallowed a whole jar of honey. Sweet, but ultimately sticky and unpleasant.
  Prince Alaric: My lady Princess, my kingdom of Veridian offers not only vast emerald mines but also the unwavering loyalty of my people.
  Princess Aurelia: How... fascinating, Prince Alaric. We shall consider your proposition.
  Lucia (thinking): (thought) She says that to all of them. I wonder if she even remembers their names.
  [WHISPERS]
  Queen Seraphina: Silence! We have one more suitor to present today. A most... unique candidate.
  (The heavy oak doors creaked open, and two guards struggled to push in a large, covered object.)
  [CREAK... THUD]
  Noble 1: What in the blazes is that?
  Noble 2: A new piece of furniture?
  Queen Seraphina: This, my dear court, is the enchanted armor, Kian. A suitor crafted by the most powerful mages of the Eldrin line. He is designed for unwavering protection and loyalty.
  (The guards pulled back the cloth, revealing a magnificent suit of silver and gold armor, intricately engraved with ancient runes. It stood perfectly still, almost radiating a silent power.)
  [GASPS]
  Lucia (thinking): (thought) An armor? As a suitor? This is a new level of ridiculous, even for this court.
  Queen Seraphina: Lucia! You will be assigned to Kian. You are to instruct him on the customs of the court and assist him in his duties as a suitor.
  Lucia: Me, Your Majesty? But...
  Queen Seraphina: No 'buts', handmaiden. Your quiet diligence makes you perfect for this delicate task. Now, take him to the west wing and begin.
  (With a sigh, I approached the armor. It felt... cold. And strangely heavy, even though it wasn't moving. This was going to be a long day.)
  [CLANK... CLANK (as Lucia guides the armor)]
  Lucia: Alright, Kian. This way. We'll start with the basics. Like, how to walk without sounding like a stampeding warhorse.
  Kian: Query: 'Stampeding warhorse' is an undesirable auditory output for locomotion?
  Lucia (thinking): (thought) It spoke! I mean, I knew it could, but it's still jarring. And so... formal.
  Lucia: Yes, Kian. Undesirable. We aim for 'graceful' or 'dignified'. Less 'about to flatten the royal gardener'.
  Kian: Understood. Adjusting gait parameters for optimal 'graceful' and 'dignified' output. Primary objective: impress Princess Aurelia.
  [WHIRR (a soft internal sound from Kian)]
  Lucia: Yes, that's the idea. So, first lesson: when addressing royalty, you bow. Not too low, not too shallow. Just enough to show respect.
  Kian: Demonstrate.
  Lucia: Alright, watch closely.
  (I performed a small, practiced curtsy, just as I'd done countless times for the Queen. Kian watched, his visor reflecting my image.)
  Kian: Replicating gesture.
  [CLANK-SCRAPE (Kian attempts to bow, stiffly)]
  Lucia: Whoa! Careful! You almost took out that vase. It's less of a 'lurch forward' and more of a 'smooth bend'.
  Lucia (thinking): (thought) This is going to be harder than I thought. He's not just armor; he's like a giant, very literal child. A very, very powerful child.
  Kian: My apologies. Recalibrating. My purpose dictates efficient learning for successful suitor engagement.
  Lucia (thinking): (thought) 'Efficient learning.' He sounded so earnest. There's something... disarming about him. He's not like the other suitors, all pretense and ambition. He just... is. And now, he's my responsibility.
  (As the afternoon light faded, casting long shadows across the west wing, I realized my life as a quiet handmaiden had just taken a very unexpected, and very metallic, turn.)

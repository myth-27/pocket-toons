=== Eaternal Nocturnal Episode 1 ===

  (Most people dream of a normal 9-to-5. A desk job, maybe. A quiet office. Me? I dream of a 9-to-5 where my coworkers don't sparkle in the sun and my customers don't eye my neck like it's a five-star buffet.)
  (Welcome to Nocturne Cafe. Where the 'iced coffee' is actually blood, and the only thing colder than the drinks are the stares from our patrons.)
  Eve: Order up! One 'Midnight Bloom' for table seven! And a 'Crimson Dew' for... oh, Mr. Elias!
  [CLINK (of glasses), WHIRR (espresso machine), WHOOSH (steam)]
  Eve: Oops! Almost forgot your usual, sir.
  Eve (thinking): Okay, Eve, deep breaths. It's just another Tuesday. A Tuesday filled with immortal beings who find human clumsiness... endearing? Terrifying? I can never tell.
  (My name is Eve. And I'm the only human barista at Nocturne Cafe. Which means I'm constantly on edge, constantly trying not to spill, break, or accidentally offer a garlic breadstick to a creature of the night.)
  [THUD (Eve bumps into counter), SPLASH (drink sloshes)]
  Eve: Agh! My apologies!
  Elias: Careful, little human. You wouldn't want to waste such a... vibrant shade.
  (He was there. Standing by the counter, as if he'd materialized from the shadows themselves. Elias. The Patron. Always impeccably dressed, always with that look in his eyes that seemed to see right through you.)
  Eve: Mr. Elias! Good evening! Your 'Crimson Dew' is almost ready!
  Eve (thinking): Oh no, not him. He always looks like he's judging my life choices. Or my blood type. And he’s so… perfect. Too perfect. His hair, his eyes, the way he just *exists* in a room. It's unfair. And he's a vampire. A very, very handsome vampire.
  Elias: There's no rush. I find your... enthusiasm... quite entertaining.
  Eve (thinking): Entertaining? Is that a compliment? A threat? A philosophical musing? With vampires, it's always a guessing game.
  [CLINK (ice in glass)]
  Eve: Here you are, Mr. Elias. Freshly... drawn.
  Elias: Thank you, Eve.
  Eve (thinking): He remembered my name! My heart just did a little human flutter. Get it together, Eve. He's a predator. A very charming predator.
  Elias: Tell me, Eve. What is it that draws a human such as yourself to a place like this?
  Eve: Well... the pay is good? And... it's never boring?
  Elias: Never boring. Indeed. Perhaps I understand your fascination with the mundane, then.
  Eve (thinking): Mundane? My life is anything but mundane here!
  (The cafe emptied out as the first hints of dawn painted the sky. For most, that's the start of the day. For us, it's the end of the night shift. And for me, it's another night survived.)
  Eve (thinking): No bites, no major spills, and I even managed to make Elias's Crimson Dew without shaking. Progress!
  [SWEEP (of broom), CREAK (door closing)]
  (I might be the only human working here, surrounded by creatures of the night. It's weird, it's dangerous, and honestly? It's kind of thrilling. Maybe one day I'll understand what's so 'fascinating' about my struggles. Until then, I'll just keep spilling coffee and trying not to get eaten.)
  (And maybe, just maybe, I'll figure out what Elias truly means when he looks at me like that.)

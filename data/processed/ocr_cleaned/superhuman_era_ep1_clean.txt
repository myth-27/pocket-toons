=== Superhuman Era Episode 1 ===

  (The world changed. Not with a bang, but with a shudder. And then, the screams.)
  [CRASH! RUMBLE! SHATTER!]
  NEWS REPORTER (V.O.): —live from District 7, where a Class A Calamity has just emerged from the Seoul Bay! Evacuation orders are in effect, but the creature is moving inland at an alarming rate—
  CITIZEN 1: Run! It's coming this way!
  CITIZEN 2: Where are the Superhumans?! Why isn't anyone doing anything?!
  [ROAR! (a monstrous, echoing sound)]
  GANG HAN (thinking): (Thought) 'Superhumans.' They always ask where we are. Never 'how' we are. Never 'what it costs.'
  [BEEP BEEP BEEP (phone ringing insistently)]
  GANG HAN: Yeah, I saw the news. What's the damage report?
  TEAM LEADER KIM (on phone): Han! Thank god you picked up! It's bad, really bad. Class A, confirmed. We've got multiple casualties, infrastructure collapse... It's already taken out three blocks. Our conventional forces are useless.
  GANG HAN: Coordinates.
  TEAM LEADER KIM (on phone): Sending them now. Look, I know you just got back from the Hokkaido incident, but you're our only hope. The other active Superhumans are too far or already engaged.
  GANG HAN (thinking): (Thought) 'Only hope.' The same old song and dance. How many times have I heard that line? How many more times will I?
  [WHOOSH! (Gang Han leaps from a skyscraper)]
  GANG HAN: I'm on my way. Tell them to clear the perimeter. I don't want any civilians or soldiers getting caught in the crossfire.
  TEAM LEADER KIM (on phone): Understood. Be careful, Han. This one looks... different.
  GANG HAN (thinking): (Thought) 'Different.' They always say that too. They're all different, and they're all the same. Just something else to break.
  [THWIP! (a web-like appendage lashes out)]
  GANG HAN: There it is.
  [BOOM! (Gang Han lands, cracking the pavement)]
  [SKREEEEEEE!]
  GANG HAN: Alright, ugly. Let's get this over with.
  [FWOOSH! (Gang Han charges, leaving a trail of displaced air)]
  [CRACKLE! (energy begins to coalesce around his fist)]
  GANG HAN (thinking): (Thought) Another day, another monster. Another city to save, another burden to bear. This is my era. The Superhuman Era.
  [IMPACT! KABOOM! (a devastating punch connects, sending shockwaves through the city)]
  (And so, the cycle continued. A hero's weary resolve against an endless tide of destruction.)

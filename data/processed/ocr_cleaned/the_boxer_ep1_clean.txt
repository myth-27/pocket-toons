=== The Boxer Episode 1 ===

  (The world moved, a blur of meaningless actions. People strived, they fought, they wept. For what? A fleeting moment of joy? A temporary escape from the inevitable?)
  YU (thinking): My own body felt like a distant vessel, responding to stimuli, but never truly *feeling*.
  BULLY 1: Hey, look at the freak. Still staring at nothing?
  BULLY 2: He probably thinks he's too good for us. Let's teach him a lesson.
  [SHOVE]
  YU (thinking): To move, to react... it was merely an efficient calculation, a path of least resistance. Nothing more.
  [WHOOSH (Yu effortlessly sidesteps)]
  BULLY 1: What the hell?! How'd you do that?!
  BULLY 2: Just lucky, probably. Get him!
  [SWIPE]
  [DUCK]
  BULLY 1: He's fast for a zombie!
  YU (thinking): Their movements were telegraphed, slow. Predictable. Like everything else.
  (Later that day, in a dimly lit gym, a man watched a sparring match with a critical eye. Coach K, a legend in his own right, always searching for the 'fire'.)
  (Another one lost to the fire... or never found it to begin with. This sport... it demands everything.)
  ASSISTANT: Coach, that kid from the cafe... he's still here. Just watching.
  K: Hm?
  K: (Observing Yu, who stands motionless, eyes fixed on the ring) You like boxing, kid?
  YU: No.
  K: (Intrigued) Then why do you watch?
  YU: They move. They hit. They fall. It's all the same. Predictable.
  K (thinking): Predictable? This kid... that blank stare. It's either nothing, or everything. A void, or a universe waiting to explode.
  K: You think you could do better?
  YU: Better at what? Hurting? Being hurt? I don't know.
  K: Come here. Let's see your hands.
  (Yu extended his hands, pale and unblemished.)
  K: No calluses. Never thrown a punch in your life. But your eyes... they see everything.
  K: Tell you what. Hit this.
  (K held up a focus mitt, a challenge in his gaze.)
  YU (thinking): A target. A simple task.
  [SWISH (Yu's arm moves, a blur)]
  [THWACK! (Impact)]
  K: (Stunned) No form, no power... but the speed. The *snap*. Again.
  [SWISH]
  [THWACK!]
  (It wasn't a punch. It was a *flick*. But it was faster than anything I'd ever seen. No passion, no fire... yet, that *speed*. It wasn't trained. It was inherent. A natural phenomenon.)
  K: What's your name, kid?
  YU: Yu.
  K: Yu... I'm Coach K. How would you like to learn how to box?
  YU (thinking): Learn to box? What's the point? Will it change anything?
  YU: Why?
  K: Because you're a genius, Yu. And geniuses don't belong on the sidelines.
  (The world kept turning. The sun kept setting. And I was still just... Yu.)

=== Limit Breaker Episode 1 ===

  (The world changed ten years ago. Gates appeared, monsters poured out, and humanity fought back with awakened abilities. Hunters became the new elite. I… was not one of them.)
  Kim Suho (thinking): Another D-rank dungeon. Another day of barely surviving.
  Kim Suho: (Panting) Just a few more… Goblins…
  [SWISH! CLANG!]
  Team Leader Park: Suho! Keep up! We don't have all day for these weaklings!
  Kim Suho: I’m trying, hyung!
  Kim Suho (thinking): My rusty sword barely cuts through their hide. My 'awakened' strength is a joke compared to theirs. Always the weakest link. Always the one holding them back.
  [GROWL!]
  Team Member Lee: Leader! A Goblin Shaman! This wasn't on the intel!
  Team Leader Park: Damn it! Suho, distract it! We'll flank!
  Kim Suho (thinking): Distract?! With what?! My life savings?
  [WHOOSH! ZAP!]
  Kim Suho: AGH!
  Kim Suho (thinking): The shaman's magic bolt hit my shoulder. It burns! They're already retreating. Cowards! They’re leaving me!
  Team Leader Park: Sorry, Suho! We'll send help!
  [THUD! THUD! (Footsteps receding)]
  Kim Suho (thinking): No… not again. This is how it ends? Eaten by a glorified goblin?
  [ROAR! (The Shaman raises its staff)]
  Kim Suho (thinking): My vision blurs. My body screams. Is this all I am? A stepping stone for others? A weakling who dies alone in a forgotten dungeon?
  [CRACKLE! (Magic gathers)]
  (Just as the final blow descended, a blinding light erupted, not from the shaman, but from within Suho.)
  [DING!]
  ([ERROR: System Overload Detected. Recalibrating…])
  Kim Suho: What… is this?
  ([Limit Breaker System Activated. Welcome, Player Kim Suho.])
  Kim Suho (thinking): A… system? Like in the novels? But… I was dying!
  ([Current Status: Critical. Emergency Protocol Initiated. Skill: 'Overdrive' unlocked. Use now?])
  Kim Suho: Yes! Use it! Anything!
  [WHOOSH! (A surge of power)]
  Kim Suho (thinking): My body… it feels like it's burning, but also… alive? Stronger? The pain is still there, but it’s distant. I can move!
  [SLASH! CRACK!]
  Kim Suho (thinking): The shaman’s staff… I just shattered it with a single swing! My rusty sword… it feels like an extension of my will!
  [ROAR! (The Shaman charges in desperation)]
  Kim Suho: You want to play, Shaman? Let’s play.
  [SWISH! CHOP! THUD!]
  (The Goblin Shaman fell, its life extinguished in a single, decisive strike. Suho stood over it, his chest heaving, a strange new power coursing through his veins.)
  Kim Suho (thinking): This… this is what it feels like. Power. Real power. No more being weak. No more being left behind. From now on… I break every limit.
  [DING!]
  ([Goblin Shaman defeated. Experience gained. Level Up!])

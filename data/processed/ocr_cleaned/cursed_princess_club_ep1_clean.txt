=== Cursed Princess Club Episode 1 ===

  (The kingdom of Imperia was renowned for many things: its sprawling emerald forests, its towering, majestic castles, and most notably...)
  (...its incredibly beautiful royal family.)
  Gwen (thinking): (thought) And then there's me. Princess Gwendolyn, the 'Cursed Princess'.
  [CREAK]
  Gwen (thinking): (thought) Another morning. Another day to face the mirror.
  [SIGH]
  Gwen (thinking): (thought) My sisters, Maria and Lorena, wake up looking like goddesses. Their hair perfectly coiffed, skin glowing, eyes sparkling.
  Gwen (thinking): (thought) Me? I wake up looking like I wrestled a badger in my sleep. And lost.
  Gwen (thinking): (thought) My nose is a bit... prominent. My chin, a tad too strong. And my hair... well, it has a mind of its own. A very chaotic mind.
  Gwen (thinking): (thought) Father says it's 'character'. Mother says it's 'unique'. But I know what they really mean. I'm the 'plain' one. The 'unfortunate' one.
  [KNOCK KNOCK]
  Maid: Princess Gwendolyn? Breakfast is served!
  Gwen: Coming!
  Gwen (thinking): (thought) Time to put on my brave face. Or, at least, a face that won't scare the royal chef.
  [CLINK CLATTER]
  King: Ah, Gwendolyn, my dear! There you are! Just in time for my famous royal pancakes!
  Gwen: Good morning, Father. Mother. Maria, Lorena.
  Queen: My sweet Gwendolyn, you look... rested.
  Maria: Your hair has such... volume today, Gwen! It's like a fluffy cloud!
  Lorena: Yes, so... natural! Not like our boring, sleek locks.
  Gwen (thinking): (thought) A fluffy cloud. That's one way to put it. A fluffy, untamed, slightly singed cloud.
  King: Nonsense, girls! Gwendolyn has a noble countenance! A face that tells a story!
  Gwen (thinking): (thought) The story of a princess who accidentally walked into a beehive.
  King: Speaking of stories, Gwendolyn, I've arranged for you to attend the Royal Ball in the neighboring kingdom of Eldoria next month. Perhaps you'll find a suitor!
  [CHOKE]
  Gwen: A suitor? Father, you know how these things usually go.
  Maria: Oh, but you're so charming, Gwen! You'll sweep them off their feet with your wit!
  Lorena: And your lovely, warm smile!
  Gwen (thinking): (thought) They mean the one where my nose wrinkles a bit too much and I look like I'm about to sneeze.
  King: Exactly! Now, eat up! You'll need your strength for all that charming!
  [GULP]
  Gwen (thinking): (thought) Later that afternoon, I decided a walk in the gardens might clear my head. Or at least, help me avoid thinking about forced charming.
  [RUSTLE RUSTLE]
  Gwen (thinking): (thought) The roses were particularly vibrant today. Unlike my prospects.
  [THUMP]
  Gwen: Oh! What's this?
  (Lying on the path, almost hidden by a patch of lavender, was a small, intricately folded piece of parchment.)
  Gwen (thinking): (thought) It has a peculiar crest. A broken crown, surrounded by thorns...
  Gwen: (reading) 'To Princess Gwendolyn of Imperia. You are cordially invited to a gathering of kindred spirits. Tonight, under the crescent moon. The Cursed Princess Club.'
  [GASP]
  Gwen (thinking): (thought) The Cursed Princess Club? Is this some kind of cruel joke? Or... could it be real?
  Gwen (thinking): (thought) My heart is pounding. For the first time, I feel a flicker of something other than resignation. Curiosity. And maybe... a tiny bit of hope.

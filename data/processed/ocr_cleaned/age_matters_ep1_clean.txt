=== Age Matters Episode 1 ===

  (The relentless chirping of the alarm clock. A sound that, for some, signals the start of a new day. For others, it's merely a cruel reminder that sleep is a luxury they can't afford.)
  [BEEP BEEP BEEP!]
  Rose Choi: Ugh... Five more minutes... just five...
  [SMACK! (alarm silenced)]
  Rose Choi (thinking): (thought) Five minutes turned into twenty. Again. Why do I do this to myself?
  [THUD! (Rose falls out of bed)]
  Rose Choi: Ow! Seriously, Rose? You're 29, not 9. Get it together!
  (A whirlwind of hurried movements, a half-eaten toast, and a dash of morning chaos later, Rose found herself on the bus, battling the morning commute.)
  [RUMBLE RUMBLE (bus)]
  Rose Choi (thinking): (thought) Today's the day. No mistakes. No clumsy moments. Just pure, unadulterated professionalism. I'm an executive assistant, not a walking disaster.
  Rose Choi (thinking): (thought) Especially not in front of Mr. Yoon. He's... perfect. Too perfect.
  (Arriving at the sleek, modern offices of Yoon Corp, Rose took a deep breath. The scent of expensive coffee and ambition filled the air.)
  Rose Choi (thinking): (thought) Okay, Rose. You can do this. Just deliver the reports, schedule the meeting, and try not to trip over your own feet.
  [CLICK (heels)]
  (She pushed open the door to the executive floor, her eyes immediately drawn to the figure already seated at the large, glass desk in the corner office.)
  Daniel Yoon: Good morning, Ms. Choi. You're precisely two minutes late.
  Rose Choi: Mr. Yoon! Good morning! My apologies, traffic was... unexpectedly heavy.
  Rose Choi (thinking): (thought) He's already here. And he looks like he stepped out of a magazine. How does he do it? It's 8 AM!
  Daniel Yoon: I see. The reports for the Q3 projections are on your desk. Please review them and prepare a summary for the board meeting at 10. And confirm the catering.
  Rose Choi: Yes, Mr. Yoon. Right away.
  Rose Choi (thinking): (thought) Q3 projections? Catering? It's barely past my first coffee! And I haven't even opened my laptop yet!
  [RUSTLE (papers)]
  (Rose rushed to her desk, a pile of documents already waiting. She grabbed her coffee mug, but her hand, still trembling slightly from the morning rush, missed the coaster.)
  [SLOSH! (coffee spills)]
  Rose Choi: Oh, no! Not again!
  Rose Choi (thinking): (thought) My life is just a series of minor catastrophes. And of course, it had to be on the Q3 reports. The most important reports!
  Daniel Yoon: Ms. Choi?
  Rose Choi: N-nothing, Mr. Yoon! Just... a minor incident with my beverage. Everything is under control!
  Rose Choi (thinking): (thought) He's looking. He's definitely looking. I can feel his perfectly composed gaze burning a hole through my perfectly stained shirt.
  [SIGH (Rose)]
  (As Rose frantically dabbed at the coffee stain, Daniel Yoon simply returned to his work, a faint, almost imperceptible smirk playing on his lips. Another day, another chaotic start for his assistant.)
  Rose Choi (thinking): (thought) Twenty-nine and still spilling coffee on myself. He's thirty-five and probably runs a multi-million dollar company in his sleep. Age really does matter, doesn't it?

=== Designated Bully Episode 1 ===

  (Every school has its pecking order. A silent, brutal hierarchy that dictates who eats, who starves, and who gets to breathe without fear.)
  (For Kang Minho, Class 2-3, the bottom rung was a familiar, painful place.)
  Kang Minho (thinking): Another day. Another empty lunchbox. Another set of eyes looking through me, or worse, at me.
  Kang Minho (thinking): Just get through it. Head down. Don't make eye contact. Don't exist.
  Lee Jihun: Well, well, if it isn't our resident ghost, Kang Minho.
  Kang Minho: ...
  Kim Dojin: Heard you got some new sneakers, Minho. Pretty flashy for someone who's supposed to be invisible.
  Kang Minho: They're... they're old. My mom bought them.
  Lee Jihun: Mommy's boy, huh? Pathetic. You know the drill, Minho. Lunch money. Now.
  Kang Minho: I... I don't have any today. I spent it on bus fare.
  Lee Jihun: Bus fare? What, are you too good to walk? You think you can just lie to me, Minho?
  [GRAB!]
  Kang Minho: Hey! Let go!
  Lee Jihun: Check his pockets, Dojin. He's probably hiding it. Little rat.
  Kim Dojin: Got it, Jihun. Let's see what treasures our little Minho is hoarding.
  [RIP!]
  Kang Minho: My... my textbook! You ripped it!
  Lee Jihun: Oops. Guess you'll have to buy a new one. With what, though? Oh, right. Nothing.
  Kang Minho: That's... that's my sister's textbook. She needs it.
  Lee Jihun: And that's my problem how? You should've just coughed up the cash. Now, about those sneakers...
  Kang Minho: No. Not the sneakers. My mom worked overtime for those.
  Lee Jihun: Oh? Suddenly you're brave? What are you gonna do, cry? Or are you gonna fight, Designated Bully?
  Kang Minho (thinking): Designated Bully... That's what they call me. The one everyone can pick on. The one who never fights back.
  Kang Minho (thinking): But not today. Not for this.
  [GRIT!]
  Kang Minho: Get your hands off me.
  Lee Jihun: What was that, worm? Did you just talk back?
  [SHOVE!]
  Kang Minho: I said, get your hands off me!
  [WHAM!]
  Kim Dojin: Agh! My nose!
  Lee Jihun: You little punk! You actually hit him!
  Kang Minho: My sister's textbook. My mom's sneakers. You don't touch them.
  Kang Minho (thinking): This feeling... it's been buried for so long. The adrenaline. The clarity. The urge to protect.
  Kang Minho (thinking): They think I'm weak. They think I'm just a target. But they don't know what I used to be. What I *can* be.
  Lee Jihun: You're going to regret that, Minho! Get him, guys!
  [SWING!]
  [DUCK!]
  [CRACK!]
  Bully 1: Ugh!
  Kang Minho: Too slow.
  [KICK!]
  [THUD!]
  Bully 2: My gut!
  Lee Jihun: What the hell? Since when could you fight like that?!
  Kang Minho: You don't want to know.
  Kang Minho (thinking): The fear is gone. Replaced by something cold and sharp. This is what it feels like to finally push back.
  [PUNCH!]
  [CRASH!]
  Lee Jihun: Gah!
  Park Seulgi: Minho...?
  Kang Minho: Silence.
  Kang Minho (thinking): The 'Designated Bully' just found his designation.

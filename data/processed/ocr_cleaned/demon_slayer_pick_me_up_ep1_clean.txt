=== Pick Me Up Episode 1 ===

  (For ten years, I was 'Loki'. The undisputed top player of 'Pick Me Up!'. A master strategist, a gacha god, a legend.)
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): Another S-rank clear. Pathetic. Do these noobs even try?
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): My team composition was flawless. My resource management, impeccable. Who else could clear 'Hell's Gate' with a single 1-star unit?
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): The only thing left to do is… grind for more stars. Or maybe finally touch grass. Nah, who am I kidding? There's always another challenge.
  (But then, something... unexpected happened.)
  [ZZZZZZZZT!]
  [CRACKLE! WHIRRRRR...]
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): What the—? My screen's going haywire! Is this a new event? A bug?
  [BRIGHT FLASH!]
  [WHOOSH! ZAP!]
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): My head... it feels like it's being ripped apart. What is this sensation?
  [THUD!]
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): Ugh... Where am I? This isn't my room. This isn't even... a room.
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): Stone walls, flickering torchlight, the smell of damp earth and... monster guts? This looks exactly like the 'Slime Caverns' dungeon.
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): Wait. Why do I feel so... heavy? And stiff? I can barely move my neck.
  [CLANK! SCRAPE!]
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): What was that sound? It came from me. My hands... they're not hands. They're crude, rock-like appendages. And my body... it's made of stone.
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): No. No, no, no. This can't be happening. I'm a Golem. A basic, 1-star Golem. The lowest of the low-tier summons!
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): Me? Loki? The man who commanded entire legions of S-rank heroes? Reduced to this pathetic form? This is a nightmare!
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): System. Status window. Anything! Show me my stats!
  [[STATUS WINDOW APPEARS]]
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): Name: Golem (Han Seol). Rank: 1-star. Level: 1. HP: 100/100. Attack: 5. Defense: 10. Skills: None. Passive: Stone Body (Reduces physical damage by 10%).
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): This is a joke, right? My attack is 5? A slime could probably take me down!
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): And who's this 'Han Seol'? That's my real name! So I'm not just a Golem, I'm *me* inside a Golem. This is beyond messed up.
  Summoner (off-screen): Finally! A Golem! Took you long enough, you useless gacha system!
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): That voice... so whiny. And that mana signature... weak. Pathetically weak. This must be my summoner.
  (Through the gaps in the other summoned units, Han Seol saw her. A young girl, maybe in her early twenties, clutching a staff, her face streaked with dirt and despair.)
  Summoner: Alright, Golem! Front line! Protect the others! We're almost out of here!
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): Front line? With these stats? Is she trying to get me killed? What kind of incompetent summoner is this?
  [SQUELCH! GROWL!]
  (From the shadows, a monstrous, multi-eyed creature with razor-sharp claws lunged forward.)
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): A Cave Lurker! Level 5, high agility, decent attack. A single hit would probably shatter me.
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): This is bad. Very bad. I'm a 1-star Golem, stuck with a clueless summoner, facing an enemy that outclasses me by a mile.
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): But... I'm Loki. The 'King of Pick Me Up'. I didn't get to the top by panicking. I got there by strategizing. Even as a Golem, I'm still me.
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): First, assess the situation. Second, identify weaknesses. Third, survive. This isn't a game anymore. This is reality. And I refuse to be fodder.
  [ROAR!]
  Summoner: Golem! Block it!
  Han Seol (Loki) (thinking): Block it, she says. Fine. Let's see what this 'Stone Body' can really do.

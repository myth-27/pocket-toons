=== Doom Breaker Episode 1 ===

  (The world had fallen. Humanity, once proud, now clung to the last vestiges of hope, embodied by a single warrior.)
  Zephyr: Haa... haa... Is this all... I can do?
  Zephyr (thinking): My body screams in protest. Every bone shattered, every muscle torn. But I can't stop. Not now.
  Tartarus: Still standing, 'Battle God'? How pathetic. Even with all your strength, you are but a fleeting spark against my eternal darkness.
  [CRACKLE]
  (Before Zephyr, the Demon King Tartarus loomed, a monstrous entity of pure malice, radiating an aura that twisted reality itself.)
  Zephyr: I... won't... let you... destroy everything!
  Zephyr (thinking): The faces of my fallen comrades flash before my eyes. Seraphina... Orion... They sacrificed everything for this moment. For me.
  [ROAR! WHOOSH!]
  Tartarus: Foolish mortal. Your defiance is meaningless. Witness the true power of a god!
  [BOOOOM! CRASH!]
  (A torrent of abyssal energy erupted from Tartarus, engulfing Zephyr. The world shook, then fell silent.)
  Zephyr (thinking): Ah... My body... it's... disintegrating.
  Zephyr (thinking): Is this... the end? After all this... I failed. Humanity... is doomed.
  Zephyr (thinking): If only... if only I had one more chance. To do things differently. To save them all.
  Mysterious Voice: Do you desire a second chance, warrior?
  Zephyr: Who... who's there?
  Mysterious Voice: A chance to rewrite your fate. To break the chains of doom. But know this: the path will be fraught with greater peril.
  Zephyr: Peril? What does it matter? If it means saving them... I'll face anything. Give me that chance!
  Mysterious Voice: Then so be it. May your resolve be as unyielding as your blade.
  [FWOOSH! ZAP!]
  (A blinding light enveloped Zephyr, pulling him through an endless void.)
  [GASP!]
  Zephyr: Ugh... My head...
  Zephyr (thinking): What... Where am I?
  (Zephyr's eyes fluttered open. He lay in a soft bed, in a simple, familiar room. Sunlight streamed through a window.)
  Zephyr (thinking): This room... it's mine. My old room.
  Zephyr (thinking): My body... it's not broken. It's... young. Full of energy.
  [RUSTLE]
  (He quickly found a discarded newspaper on a nearby table. His eyes scanned the date.)
  Zephyr (thinking): Year 2038... The Demon King's invasion... it hasn't happened yet.
  Zephyr (thinking): This is it. I've truly returned. Before everything went to hell. Before I lost everyone.
  (A cold, determined glint entered his eyes. His fists clenched.)
  Zephyr: Tartarus... You won't win this time. I'll make sure of it. I'll save them all.
  Zephyr: This time, I will break doom itself.

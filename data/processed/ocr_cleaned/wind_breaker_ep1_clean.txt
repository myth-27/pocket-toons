=== Wind Breaker Episode 1 ===

  (Jay Jo. A name that barely registered on the school roster, let alone in the minds of his peers.)
  (His world was a meticulously organized schedule of textbooks, study guides, and the relentless pursuit of perfect grades.)
  Jay (thinking): Another day. Another 14 hours until I can truly breathe.
  [CLACK, RUSTLE]
  (He moved through the crowded hallways like a ghost, his eyes fixed forward, a silent barrier around him.)
  Minu: Yo, Jay! Jay Jo!
  Jay (thinking): Not again.
  Minu: Still ignoring me, huh? You're a tough nut to crack!
  Jay: What do you want, Minu? I have a study session.
  Minu: Always studying! Come on, man, you gotta live a little!
  Minu: The cycling club is having tryouts this week. You should totally come!
  Jay: No.
  Minu: Just "no"? Come on, I've seen you. You're fast.
  Jay (thinking): He saw me? How much?
  Jay: I don't cycle. Not seriously.
  Minu: Don't cycle? Then what was that blur I saw zipping past the park yesterday? Looked like you were flying!
  [WHOOSH]
  Jay: It wasn't me. You must be mistaken.
  Minu: No way! That technique, that speed... it was definitely you! You've got talent, Jay! Real talent!
  Minu: We need a rider like you. Someone who can actually keep up. Our crew, Hummingbird, we're building something special.
  Jay: I'm not interested in joining any clubs. My focus is academics.
  Minu: Academics, academics... You'll burn out, dude! A little fresh air, some adrenaline... it's good for the brain!
  Minu: Just think about it. We ride, we chill, we compete. It's a blast!
  Jay: I said no. Please leave me alone.
  [Footsteps receding]
  Minu: Fine, be that way. But I'm not giving up!
  Jay (thinking): Why can't he just understand?
  Jay (thinking): The bike... it's just a way to get around. A means to an end.
  Jay (thinking): I can't afford distractions. Not anymore.
  Jay (thinking): The last time I got involved, the last time I let myself enjoy something... it ended badly.
  Jay (thinking): The whispers, the stares, the trouble. I swore I'd never go back to that.
  Jay (thinking): I just want to be invisible. To finish school, get into a good university, and live a quiet life.
  [THUMP-THUMP]
  (The school day finally ended, a prison sentence commuted.)
  (Jay walked out, a heavy backpack slung over his shoulder, a familiar emptiness in his chest.)
  (But then, a subtle shift. His eyes caught the glint of chrome.)
  [CLICK]
  (His hands, usually stiff from holding pens, moved with a practiced ease.)
  Jay (thinking): Just a quick ride home. No detours. No showing off.
  [WHOOSH]
  (The wind, a gentle caress, began to whisper past his ears.)
  (The rhythm of the pedals, a silent song only he could hear.)
  Jay (thinking): This feeling...
  (For a fleeting moment, the weight of the world lifted. The grades, the expectations, the self-imposed isolation... they all faded.)
  [WHOOSH-WHOOSH-WHOOSH]
  (He leaned into a turn, his body a natural extension of the machine, a silent blur of motion.)
  Jay (thinking): Just for a moment. Just this.
  Minu: Told you it was you!
  [SKID]
  Jay: Minu! What are you doing?
  Minu: Following you, obviously! You're trying to hide that talent, aren't you?
  Minu: Look, I know you don't want to join the club, but...
  Minu: Just one race. One friendly race. Me against you.
  Minu: If I win, you come to tryouts. If you win, I'll never bother you about cycling again. Deal?
  Jay (thinking): A race? He's serious.
  Jay (thinking): If I win, he'll leave me alone. It's the only way to get rid of him.
  Jay (thinking): But if I race, I'll have to... show them.
  Minu: What's it gonna be, Jay? Afraid you'll lose?
  Jay (thinking): Afraid? No. I'm afraid of what happens if I *don't* lose.
  Jay: Fine. One race.
  Minu: YES! Alright! Let's do it! To the bridge and back!
  [WHOOSH]
  Jay (thinking): This is a mistake. A huge mistake.
  Jay (thinking): But now... I have to win.

=== Episode 9: Someone That People Need (2) ===

SUMMARY: Maehwa (also known as Hajin) insists on accompanying Jayeong on a mission to learn to control his powers and overcome his fear, despite Jayeong's strong objections. Doctor Seo eventually permits Maehwa to go unofficially, leading to Jayeong reluctantly agreeing but warning him to stay out of the way. During the mission, Maehwa observes Jayeong fighting bugs and reflects on his own abilities and a mysterious bleeding cut, before being targeted by a bug himself.

  Jayeong: What?

  Maehwa: I won't get in your way! I promise.

  Jayeong: I told you. If you get in the way... I'll cut you down first!

=== Boyfriends Episode 1 ===

  (The soft morning light filters through the blinds, painting stripes across the messy, beloved apartment.)
  Goth (thinking): (Thought) Another morning. Sometimes, even after all this time, it feels a little… overwhelming. Four people. Four very different lives, tangled up in one messy, beautiful knot.
  (Goth blinks, adjusting to the light. He feels a warm weight against his side.)
  Jock: (Muttering, half-asleep) Mmm, Gothy… five more minutes…
  Goth (thinking): (Thought) That’s Jock. Always radiating warmth, even in his sleep. He’s like a human-sized golden retriever.
  (Across the room, perched on the edge of the sofa, is Nerd, already engrossed in a thick textbook, a half-empty mug of tea beside him.)
  Goth (thinking): (Thought) And there’s Nerd. Probably been up for hours, dissecting some obscure theory before the sun even thought about rising. How does he do it?
  Nerd: Ah, good morning, Goth. Did you sleep well? I was just contemplating the implications of the latest findings in theoretical astrophysics.
  Goth: Morning, Nerd. Sounds… intense.
  Jock: (Stretching, yawning loudly) Morning, everyone! *YAWN* Time to conquer the day!
  [STRETCH! YAWN!]
  (Jock springs up, full of energy, already doing a few quick stretches.)
  Prep: (From the kitchen) Jock, don't forget to wipe down the counter after your protein shake. And Nerd, please try not to spill coffee on your textbook again. That was a nightmare to clean.
  Goth (thinking): (Thought) And that, of course, is Prep. The anchor. The one who keeps us all from spiraling into utter chaos. He’s the reason we have matching socks and a semblance of a schedule.
  [CLINK CLATTER (of dishes)]
  Jock: Got it, Prep! Hey, Gothy, wanna go for a run later? Or maybe hit the gym? Gotta keep those muscles toned!
  Goth: A run? Jock, it’s barely 8 AM. My preferred morning activity involves a dark room and minimal human interaction.
  Nerd: Statistically, early morning exercise can significantly improve cognitive function, Jock. However, Goth's preference for a calm environment is also valid for mental well-being.
  Prep: (Entering the living room, holding a tray with mugs) Breakfast is ready. Scrambled eggs and toast. Jock, your protein shake is by your plate. Nerd, I made you green tea.
  [HUM (of the coffee maker)]
  Jock: You're the best, Prep! *SMOOCH*
  (Jock plants a quick kiss on Prep’s cheek, making Prep roll his eyes good-naturedly.)
  Prep: Don't get grease on my shirt. Goth, your coffee is black, as usual.
  Goth: Thanks, Prep. You always know.
  Goth (thinking): (Thought) He always knows. They all do, in their own ways. Jock knows how to pull me out of my head with a spontaneous hug. Nerd knows when I need quiet companionship. And Prep… Prep just knows how to make everything work.
  Jock: So, what's on the agenda for today, lovebirds? Movie marathon? Board games? Or maybe we try that new escape room?
  Nerd: I have a few chapters to review for my thesis, but I could allocate an hour for a recreational activity.
  Prep: I have to run errands and organize the weekly budget. But we could do something together this evening. Maybe order takeout?
  Goth (thinking): (Thought) See? It’s never simple. Four different schedules, four different preferences. It could be a disaster.
  Jock: Takeout sounds great! And then a movie? We could all cuddle on the couch!
  Nerd: That sounds agreeable. Perhaps a documentary on the cosmos?
  Prep: As long as it's not another one of Jock's action movies. My ears can only take so much simulated gunfire.
  Jock: Hey! Action movies are fun! But okay, okay, cosmos it is. As long as Goth gets to pick the food!
  Goth: (A small smile) Deal. I'm thinking… spicy noodles.
  Goth (thinking): (Thought) And just like that, it works. It always does. It’s chaotic, a little overwhelming, and sometimes I wonder how we manage. But then I look at them, these three incredible, infuriating, loving men… and I wouldn't have it any other way.
  [CHUCKLE (from Prep)]
  [GIGGLE (from Jock)]

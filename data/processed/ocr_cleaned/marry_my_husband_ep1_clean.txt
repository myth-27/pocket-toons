=== Marry My Husband Episode 1 ===

  (My body... it was giving up. Terminal stomach cancer. Only a few months left.)
  Kang Ji-won (thinking): Min-hwan... where are you?
  Kang Ji-won: Even on my last days... I'm alone.
  (I mustered all my strength, just to go home. To see him. To be with him one last time.)
  [KEY JINGLE, DOOR CREAK]
  Kang Ji-won (thinking): What's that sound...?
  [MOAN, GASP (from inside)]
  Kang Ji-won: Min-hwan...?
  [CRASH (something falls inside)]
  Kang Ji-won (thinking): No... it can't be.
  [DOOR SWINGS OPEN]
  Kang Ji-won: Park Min-hwan! Jung Soo-min!
  Park Min-hwan: Ji-won?! What are you doing here?!
  Jung Soo-min: Oh my god, Ji-won! It's not what it looks like!
  Kang Ji-won (thinking): My best friend... my husband... naked... in my bed.
  Kang Ji-won: How... how could you?!
  Park Min-hwan: Why are you home so early? You should be in the hospital!
  Kang Ji-won: So you could do this?! While I'm dying?!
  Jung Soo-min: Ji-won, please, it's a misunderstanding! He forced me!
  Kang Ji-won (thinking): Lies. All lies. Her eyes... they're full of triumph.
  Kang Ji-won: Forced you? You've been eyeing him for years, haven't you, Soo-min?!
  Park Min-hwan: Enough! What's the big deal? You're going to die anyway! I need someone to take care of me!
  Kang Ji-won (thinking): He said it. He actually said it.
  Kang Ji-won: You... you monster! All this time... you were just waiting for me to die so you could get my insurance money!
  Park Min-hwan: And what if I was?! You're useless! A burden! You're just a walking corpse!
  Jung Soo-min: Min-hwan, don't say that!
  Kang Ji-won: You two... you deserve each other! I hate you! I hate you both!
  [SLAP!]
  Park Min-hwan: How dare you hit me, you dying hag!
  Park Min-hwan: You want to die faster?! Fine!
  [PUSH, STUMBLE]
  Kang Ji-won: Ah!
  (He pushed me. Hard. My head hit the marble table.)
  [THUD, CRACK!]
  Kang Ji-won: G-gasp...
  Kang Ji-won (thinking): The pain... it's blinding. My blood... so warm.
  Jung Soo-min: Min-hwan! What have you done?!
  Park Min-hwan: She deserved it! She was going to tell everyone!
  Kang Ji-won (thinking): My life... ending like this? Betrayed by everyone... I just wanted to be happy...
  Kang Ji-won (thinking): If only... if only I could go back. I would never make the same mistakes. I would never marry him. I would never trust her.
  [GURGLE, LAST BREATH]
  (Darkness consumed me. The bitter taste of betrayal, the sting of regret... my last sensations.)
  [GASP, COUGH (sudden, sharp intake of breath)]
  Kang Ji-won: H-huh?
  Kang Ji-won (thinking): What is this? I'm... I'm breathing?
  (The dull ache in my head... it was gone. The smell of antiseptic... replaced by something familiar. My old room.)
  Kang Ji-won: This isn't... the hospital. This isn't... my apartment.
  [RUSTLE (of bedsheets)]
  Kang Ji-won (thinking): My body... it feels different. Lighter. Younger.
  (I rushed to the mirror. A face I hadn't seen in years stared back. My younger self. No gaunt cheeks, no sickly pallor.)
  Kang Ji-won: My hair... it's long. My skin... clear.
  Kang Ji-won (thinking): Is this... a dream? Am I dead? Is this hell?
  (Then I saw it. The calendar on the wall. The date. April 12, 2013.)
  Kang Ji-won: 2013...? No... it can't be. That was... ten years ago.
  Kang Ji-won (thinking): Ten years ago... before I married Min-hwan. Before I got sick. Before everything went wrong.
  Kang Ji-won: I... I went back in time?
  Kang Ji-won (thinking): This is my chance. My second chance. I won't let them destroy me again.
  Kang Ji-won (thinking): Park Min-hwan... Jung Soo-min... you will pay. I will marry my husband... but it won't be *my* husband.
  [FIST CLENCH]
  (A cold, burning resolve ignited within me. The game had just begun.)

=== SSS-Class Suicide Hunter Episode 1 ===

  (The Tower. A colossal structure that appeared one day, challenging humanity. Climb it, conquer its floors, and earn unimaginable power, wealth, and glory. Or... fail, and be forgotten.)
  GONGJA (thinking): Another day, another floor to scrub. While those 'Hunters' are out there, risking their lives for fame and fortune...
  GONGJA (thinking): I'm stuck here, scrubbing away the grime left by their glorious battles.
  GONGJA: Ugh, this stench... Is that goblin blood or just another Hunter's spilled energy drink? Probably both.
  [SCRUB SCRUB]
  GONGJA (thinking): S-Class, A-Class... I'm not even a D-Class. Just a pathetic F-Class cleaner. My 'skill' is literally 'Cleaning Proficiency (F)'. What a joke.
  (Among the countless Hunters, one stood above all. The undisputed strongest. The SSS-Class Hunter, the Flame Emperor.)
  GONGJA (thinking): The Flame Emperor... He just cleared the 40th floor in record time. They say he's already eyeing the 50th. While I'm here, eyeing a stubborn stain.
  [DISTANT CHEERS]
  GONGJA: SSS-Class... If only I had a skill like that. Something that could actually *do* something.
  GONGJA (thinking): I'm tired of this. Tired of being weak. Tired of being invisible. I just... I just want a chance. A single, tiny chance to be something more.
  [SHIMMER]
  GONGJA: Huh? What was that? Did I... did I just feel something?
  (A faint light enveloped Gongja. A phenomenon rarely seen outside of high-tier dungeons. A skill awakening.)
  GONGJA (thinking): A skill? Me? After all this time? Please, let it be something good. Something powerful!
  [WHOOSH]
  [TING!]
  GONGJA: My status window! It's... it's showing a new skill!
  GONGJA (thinking): [I Want To Live (EX)]... What kind of name is that? And 'EX'? Is that good? It sounds... vague.
  GONGJA (thinking): "Upon death, you will return to a point in time 24 hours prior to your death. This skill can only be activated by dying." ...What?!
  GONGJA: I have to *die* for it to work?! Is this some kind of sick joke?!
  FLAME EMPEROR: You. What are you doing in my private quarters, cleaner?
  GONGJA: F-Flame Emperor-nim! I... I was just... uh... admiring your... uh... decor!
  FLAME EMPEROR: Decor? Or were you trying to steal something, you F-Class rat? I felt a strange energy fluctuation near my skill stone.
  GONGJA (thinking): He knows! Oh god, he knows! This is it. I'm dead. Literally.
  FLAME EMPEROR: Pathetic. You think an F-Class like you could ever touch what's mine?
  [CRACKLE]
  GONGJA: Please, wait! I didn't mean any harm! I just... I just wanted to be strong!
  FLAME EMPEROR: Begging won't save you. You're a nuisance.
  [FLAMES ROAR]
  [WHOOSH]
  GONGJA (thinking): This is it. My life... ending like this? No... I don't want to die!
  [BLAST!]
  [CRASH!]
  GONGJA: A-aaargh...
  (The world went dark. But then... a familiar sensation. A jolt.)
  GONGJA (thinking): What...? I'm... I'm back?
  [STATIC]
  [FADE IN]
  GONGJA: I'm... in my bed? It's morning?
  GONGJA (thinking): The status window! It's still there! "I Want To Live (EX)"... It worked! I really died... and came back!
  GONGJA: He killed me... and I came back. This skill... it's insane!
  GONGJA (thinking): This isn't just a skill. This is a second chance. A chance to change everything. A chance to... finally get stronger.
  (Kim Gongja had died. And in doing so, he had truly begun to live.)

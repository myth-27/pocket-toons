=== Dreaming Freedom Episode 1 ===

  (The fluorescent lights of the classroom hummed, a dull, constant drone that mirrored my existence.)
  SIYOON (thinking): Another day. Another blur of faces that don't see me.
  CLASSMATE A: Hey, Siyoon. Can I borrow your notes? I missed class yesterday.
  SIYOON: Oh, uh, sure.
  SIYOON (thinking): They only ever talk to me when they need something. Am I just a walking textbook?
  CLASSMATE A: Thanks! You're a lifesaver!
  SIYOON (thinking): (Muttering) A lifesaver for your grades, maybe.
  (Lunch break. The cafeteria was a cacophony of laughter and chatter, none of it directed at me.)
  [CLATTER]
  SIYOON (thinking): Just me and my half-eaten sandwich, a monument to social awkwardness.
  (The day dragged on, a monotonous cycle of invisibility and quiet despair. But there was always an escape.)
  [DOOR CREAK]
  SIYOON (thinking): Home. Finally. The only place I can truly be myself. Or, rather, become someone else.
  (I changed into my pajamas, the soft fabric a familiar comfort. The world outside could wait.)
  SIYOON (thinking): Just a few more hours until I can truly live.
  [SHEETS RUSTLE]
  (Darkness enveloped me, a gentle embrace. And then, the shift.)
  [WHOOSH]
  SIYOON (thinking): (Dreaming) The air is different here. Crisp. Alive. And I... I'm different.
  (I stood on a towering skyscraper, the city lights twinkling below like scattered diamonds. My hair whipped around me, not limp and dull, but vibrant, free.)
  SIYOON (thinking): No one can touch me here. No one can ignore me.
  [WIND WHISTLE]
  MYSTERIOUS FIGURE: Enjoying the view, Siyoon?
  SIYOON: (Startled, but not afraid) You... You're here again.
  (He emerged from the shadows, a silhouette against the moon. Tall, impossibly handsome, with eyes that held a dangerous glint.)
  MYSTERIOUS FIGURE: Where else would I be? This is your stage, isn't it? Your freedom.
  SIYOON (thinking): It is. And you're... a part of it.
  MYSTERIOUS FIGURE: Tell me, Siyoon. What do you truly desire in this world of yours?
  SIYOON: To be seen. To be powerful. To make them regret.
  (A slow, predatory smile spread across his face. He reached out, his fingers brushing my cheek, sending a jolt through me.)
  [THUMP-THUMP (heartbeat)]
  MYSTERIOUS FIGURE: Then let's make it happen. Together.
  SIYOON: (Gasp) Yes...
  [ALARM BLARE]
  (The harsh reality of my alarm clock shattered the illusion. The vibrant city faded, replaced by my faded wallpaper.)
  SIYOON (thinking): No... Not yet. Just a little longer...
  [GROAN]
  (But the dream was gone, leaving behind only a lingering warmth on my cheek where his hand had been.)
  SIYOON (thinking): It felt so real. He felt so real.
  (As I got ready for school, the world seemed duller, colder. The memory of his eyes, however, stayed with me.)
  SIYOON (thinking): Who is he? And why does he only exist when I close my eyes?
  (Little did I know, the lines between my two worlds were about to blur in ways I couldn't possibly imagine.)

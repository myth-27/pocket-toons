=== Remarried Empress Episode 1 ===

  (The Eastern Empire. A land of prosperity, guided by a lineage of powerful emperors and their empresses. Among them, Empress Navier Trovi was considered the pinnacle of perfection.)
  Navier: The trade agreement with the Western Kingdom requires careful review of the tariffs on silk imports. Ensure the revised figures are presented to the council by tomorrow morning.
  Countess Jubel: Yes, Your Majesty. It shall be done.
  Navier: And the charity ball preparations? Have the invitations been sent to all the noble houses?
  Marquess Ketron: All dispatched, Your Majesty. We await their RSVPs.
  Navier (thinking): (My life has always been a meticulously crafted schedule. Every moment dedicated to the Empire, to my role.)
  [RUSTLE of silk, GENTLE CLINK of teacups]
  Maid: Your Majesty, His Imperial Majesty, Emperor Sovieshu, has returned.
  Navier: Oh? So soon? I was not informed of his early return from the hunting trip.
  Countess Jubel: Perhaps he simply wished to surprise you, Your Majesty.
  [FOOTSTEPS approaching, MURMURS from the hallway]
  Sovieshu: Navier. I've returned.
  Navier: Welcome back, Your Majesty. I trust your hunting trip was fruitful?
  Sovieshu: Indeed. More fruitful than I could have imagined. I found something quite... precious.
  [GASPS from the ladies-in-waiting]
  Sovieshu: This is Rashta. She was captured by slave traders, and I rescued her. From now on, she will reside in the Western Palace as my concubine.
  Rashta: (Trembling slightly) Your... Your Majesty... Empress Navier...
  Navier (thinking): (A concubine? Brought directly into the palace, without so much as a prior word. And so openly.)
  Navier: I see. Welcome to the Imperial Palace, Rashta. I trust you will find your stay comfortable.
  [WHISPERS among the court ladies]
  Lady Eliza: Did you see her dress? So plain... and her hair, unkempt!
  Countess Jubel: Hush, Eliza. But to bring her directly before the Empress...
  Sovieshu: Navier, Rashta has been through a traumatic experience. I expect her to be treated with the utmost care and respect.
  Navier: Of course, Your Majesty. All imperial residents are treated with respect.
  Rashta: (Tears welling up) Rashta... Rashta is so grateful to His Majesty. He is Rashta's savior.
  Sovieshu: (Gently patting her shoulder) There, there, Rashta. You're safe now.
  Navier (thinking): (His touch... he hasn't touched me like that in years. Not since we were children.)
  Navier: If you'll excuse me, Your Majesty. I have further duties to attend to.
  Sovieshu: Very well. I will ensure Rashta settles in.
  [DOOR CLOSES softly]
  Navier (thinking): (A concubine. It was bound to happen eventually. But to be so... blatant. It's not the concubine herself that bothers me, but the disregard for protocol, for me. Our marriage was a political alliance, a duty. But even duties require a certain level of decorum.)
  Navier (thinking): (I am the Empress. My emotions are a luxury I cannot afford. I will simply observe.)
  Countess Jubel: Your Majesty, are you quite alright?
  Navier: Perfectly, Countess. Let us resume our work. The Empire does not stop for trivial matters.

=== Villain to Kill Episode 1 ===

  (They called him Killer. The strongest hero. The one who always got the job done.)
  (But even the strongest can fall.)
  Killer: Gah!
  [BLADE! SLASH!]
  Killer: You... you bastards! After everything...
  Killer: This isn't how it ends for me. Not like this!
  [THUD! CRACK!]
  Killer (thinking): (My vision... fading. My body... breaking. They surrounded me. All of them. The 'heroes' I fought alongside.)
  Killer (thinking): (The Hero Killer... he got to them. Or did he just expose the rot that was always there?)
  Killer (thinking): (My power... gone. Drained. Betrayed.)
  Killer: You... will... regret... this...
  [GURGLE. SILENCE.]
  (And so, the hero known as Killer died. Betrayed by those he protected.)
  (But death, it seems, was merely a transition.)
  [BEEP... BEEP... BEEP...]
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (What is this sound? My head... it's pounding.)
  [GASP!]
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (My body... it feels so... weak. Fragile. Not my own.)
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (Where am I? This isn't the battlefield. This isn't my apartment.)
  [PANT... PANT...]
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (I can barely move. My muscles ache with just this much effort.)
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (This room... it's a dump. A squalid, forgotten corner of the city.)
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (And this face... in the cracked mirror shard... it's not mine.)
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (Who is this scrawny kid? Why am I in his body?)
  [GROAN.]
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (A villain's uniform? Tattered, but unmistakable. A low-tier grunt, by the looks of it.)
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (The last thing I remember... the betrayal. The Hero Killer's smirk as I bled out.)
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (They thought they could just erase me. Dispose of me like trash.)
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (But I'm still here. In this pathetic body, yes, but I'm here.)
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (Gwon Gyeol, huh? That's the name stitched onto this uniform.)
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (Fine. Let them think Killer is dead. Let them celebrate their victory.)
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (I'll take this new life. This new identity. And I will make them pay.)
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (The 'heroes' who betrayed me. The Hero Killer who orchestrated it all.)
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (They will all fall. And this time, it won't be as a hero.)
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (I will become the ultimate villain. A villain to kill them all.)
  [CRUMBLE (a piece of the wall as he pushes himself up).]
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (First, I need power. This body is useless. But I know how to get strong.)
  Gwon Gyeol (Cassian) (thinking): (This world will learn to fear the name Gwon Gyeol.)
  [SMASH (his fist against the crumbling wall, a small crater forms).]

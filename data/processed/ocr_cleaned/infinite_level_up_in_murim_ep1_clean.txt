=== Infinite Level up in Murim Episode 1 ===

  (The biting wind tore through my tattered robes, mirroring the gaping wound in my chest.)
  Jinmu (thinking): Is this... the end? After all these years... still just a third-rate martial artist. A disposable pawn.
  (My vision blurred, the faces of my enemies, the Blood Sect assassins, fading into darkness. I had failed. Failed to protect anyone. Failed to become strong.)
  Jinmu (thinking): If only... if only I had another chance...
  [GASP!]
  Jinmu: (Wakes up suddenly, disoriented) What...?
  Jinmu (thinking): This isn't... the alleyway. This is my old room. The one in the sect's outer disciples' dormitory.
  Jinmu (thinking): My body... it feels so light. So... young.
  (He looked down at his hands. Uncalloused, unscarred. The hands of a fifteen-year-old boy.)
  Jinmu (thinking): This can't be... Am I dreaming? A vivid nightmare?
  [SHIMMER!]
  SYSTEM: [Welcome, Player. The 'Infinite Level Up System' has been activated.]
  Jinmu (thinking): (Eyes widen) A system window? Just like the legends... No, this isn't a dream. This is real. I've regressed! I've returned to the past!
  Jinmu (thinking): The year... it's 20 years before the Blood Sect's rise. Before the war. Before my death.
  Jinmu (thinking): This is my chance. My second life. I won't waste it again. I won't be a weakling, a pawn to be discarded.
  Jinmu (thinking): The memories... they're all here. Every technique I saw, every secret I overheard, every mistake I made.
  Jinmu (thinking): The 'Infinite Level Up System'... what does it do?
  SYSTEM: [Player 'Jinmu' has received a 'Beginner's Package'. Do you wish to open it?]
  Jinmu (thinking): (A slight smirk plays on his lips) A beginner's package? This is truly like a game. Good. I'll play this game to win.
  Jinmu: Open.
  [CHING!]
  SYSTEM: [Beginner's Package contents: 1x 'Basic Internal Energy Cultivation Manual (Rank F)', 5x 'Minor Health Potions', 100x 'Copper Coins'.]
  Jinmu (thinking): Rank F? Pathetic. But a start. I remember this manual. It's what every outer disciple gets. Most never master it beyond the first few stages.
  Jinmu (thinking): But I'm not 'most'. I have two decades of experience, observations, and knowledge of advanced techniques that haven't even been invented yet.
  Jinmu (thinking): The 'Basic Internal Energy Cultivation Manual'... The key isn't just to cultivate it, but to understand its flaws, its potential. I know a way to accelerate it.
  (Jinmu's eyes, once dull and resigned, now burned with a fierce, calculating light.)
  Jinmu (thinking): First, I need to get stronger. Much stronger. The sect's outer disciple training is a joke. I need to find a secluded spot.
  Jinmu (thinking): The Hidden Bamboo Grove... it's rarely patrolled. Perfect.
  [CREAK (door opening)]
  Jinmu (thinking): (Slips out of the dormitory, moving stealthily) I remember the path. Even after all these years. My old self would have been afraid of getting caught breaking curfew. Not anymore.
  (He moved with a newfound purpose, his steps light, his mind racing with plans.)
  Jinmu (thinking): The first thing is to cultivate the 'Basic Internal Energy'. But I won't just follow the instructions. I'll integrate the breathing techniques I learned from the 'Azure Dragon's Scale' manual, which won't be discovered for another five years.
  Jinmu (thinking): It's risky. Combining different cultivation methods can lead to Qi Deviation. But the reward... the acceleration will be immense.
  (He reached the Hidden Bamboo Grove, the moonlight filtering through the dense leaves.)
  Jinmu (thinking): (Sits in a meditative pose) This is where it begins. My second chance. My path to becoming the strongest.
  [WHOOSH (gentle energy flow)]
  (As he began to circulate his nascent internal energy, a subtle warmth spread through his meridians. It was familiar, yet different. This time, it felt... right.)
  SYSTEM: [Basic Internal Energy Cultivation (Rank F) has begun.]
  SYSTEM: [Progress: 1%]
  Jinmu (thinking): Good. Now, let's see how fast I can break through the first stage. The old me took months. This time... it will be days. Hours, even.
  Jinmu (thinking): The future of Murim depends on it. My future depends on it.

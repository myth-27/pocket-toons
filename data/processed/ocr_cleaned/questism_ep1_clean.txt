=== Questism Episode 1 ===

  (Gangbuk. A district where the law of the jungle reigns supreme. Here, strength isn't just power; it's survival. And I, Kim Suhyun, was at the very bottom of the food chain.)
  RYU SIKYUNG: Look at this pathetic worm. Still crawling around after all this time?
  KIM SUHYUN (thinking): (thoughts) Not again... Just endure it. It'll be over soon. Just like every other day.
  [THUD! (Suhyun hits the ground)]
  BULLY MINION: Hey, Sikyung, he looks like he's about to cry! What a loser!
  RYU SIKYUNG: Cry all you want, Suhyun. It won't change the fact that you're nothing but a punching bag. You're the weakest trash in all of Gangbuk.
  KIM SUHYUN (thinking): (thoughts) He's right. I am trash. Every day is the same. A cycle of humiliation, pain, and despair. I can't fight back, I can't run away. I'm trapped.
  [KICK! (Sikyung kicks Suhyun)]
  KIM SUHYUN (thinking): (thoughts) My body aches, but my heart aches more. Is this really my life? Am I doomed to be a punching bag forever? To live in fear, always looking over my shoulder?
  (Later, alone in my room, the bruises on my body were a stark reminder of my helplessness. I stared at my reflection, seeing only a weakling.)
  KIM SUHYUN (thinking): (thoughts) If only... if only I had some strength. Just a little. Enough to not be stepped on.
  [DING!]
  KIM SUHYUN (thinking): (thoughts) What was that sound? My phone?
  [SHIMMER! (A translucent window appears)]
  (A translucent window, like something out of a video game, flickered into existence before my eyes. It displayed text in a font I'd never seen.)
  SYSTEM: Welcome, Player Kim Suhyun. The Quest System has been activated. Your journey to become the strongest in Gangbuk begins now.
  KIM SUHYUN (thinking): (thoughts) The strongest? In Gangbuk? Is this... a dream? Or am I finally losing my mind?
  SYSTEM: Main Quest: [First Step Towards Strength]
Description: Punch the nearest concrete wall 10 times.
Reward: Strength +1, Stamina +1, Random Skill Card (F-rank)
  KIM SUHYUN (thinking): (thoughts) Punch a wall? What good will that do? My knuckles will just break. This has to be a joke. But... the details are so vivid. The rewards... Strength +1?
  (A desperate part of me, a tiny flicker of hope I hadn't known still existed, urged me on. What did I have to lose?)
  KIM SUHYUN (thinking): (thoughts) Just once. To see if it's real.
  [CRACK! (Suhyun's fist hits the wall, pain)]
  KIM SUHYUN (thinking): (thoughts) Ow! It hurts! My knuckles... they're bleeding. This is pointless.
  SYSTEM: 1/10
  KIM SUHYUN (thinking): (thoughts) But... the system registered it. What if...? What if it's not a dream?
  [CRACK! CRACK! CRACK! (Suhyun continues punching, pain turning to resolve)]
  SYSTEM: QUEST COMPLETE! Strength +1, Stamina +1, Random Skill Card (F-rank) acquired!
  KIM SUHYUN (thinking): (thoughts) It... it actually worked? I don't feel like a superhero, but... my arm, it feels... less tired. And my knuckles, they're not as bad as they should be.
  (A strange sensation coursed through my body. A subtle shift. It was barely noticeable, but it was there. A spark. A tiny, fragile spark of hope in the endless darkness of my life.)

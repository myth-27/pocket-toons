=== How to Fight Episode 1 ===

  (My name is Hobin Yu. And my life is a living hell.)
  Hobin Yu (thinking): (Thought) Another day, another beating. It's always the same. Pakgo, his camera, and me.
  Pakgo: Alright, Hobin, time for your daily dose of fame! Say hi to the camera, everyone!
  Jiksae: Yeah, smile for the viewers, loser! Pakgo's NewTube channel needs content!
  Hobin Yu: P-Pakgo... please... I don't have any money today. I already gave you everything yesterday...
  Pakgo: Money? Who said anything about money today? We're doing a special segment! 'Hobin's Humiliation Hour'!
  Jiksae: Ooh, good one, Pakgo! The viewers will love this!
  Pakgo: Alright, Hobin, for today's challenge, I want you to bark like a dog. A really pathetic dog.
  Hobin Yu (thinking): (Thought) My face burns. My stomach twists. I hate this. I hate them. I hate myself for letting this happen.
  Pakgo: What, no bark? Fine. Maybe this will loosen your tongue.
  [SMACK!]
  Hobin Yu: Agh!
  Pakgo: That's for being slow! Now, bark!
  Hobin Yu (thinking): (Thought) My cheek stings. Tears well up, but I can't let them see. Not again. I just want to disappear.
  [KICK!]
  Hobin Yu: Ugh!
  Jiksae: Haha! Look at him squirm! Gold content!
  Hobin Yu (thinking): (Thought) This is my life. Every single day. No one helps. No one cares. I'm just... a punching bag. A joke for their viewers.
  (Lying on the dirty alley ground, Hobin's phone, which Pakgo had snatched earlier, lay forgotten beside him. It was still recording.)
  [BEEP! (Phone notification)]
  Hobin Yu (thinking): (Thought) What was that? A NewTube notification? On my phone...?
  (Through blurry eyes, Hobin saw the screen. A suggested video. 'How to Fight: Basic Self-Defense for Beginners.')
  Hobin Yu (thinking): (Thought) How to fight...? Me? That's ridiculous. I'm the weakest kid in school. I can't even stand up straight.
  Pakgo: Alright, that's enough for today! Jiksae, send that clip to the editor. We'll make a killing!
  Jiksae: You got it, boss! See ya tomorrow, Hobin! Don't forget to practice your barks!
  [FOOTSTEPS FADE]
  Hobin Yu (thinking): (Thought) They're gone. Finally. I pick up my phone, my hand trembling. The video is still there. 'How to Fight.'
  Hobin Yu (thinking): (Thought) What if...? What if I actually learned? Just a little bit? Would it change anything? Could I... could I stop this?
  (A flicker of something he hadn't felt in a long time ignited within him. Not hope, not yet. But a desperate, burning curiosity.)
  Hobin Yu (thinking): (Thought) I have nothing left to lose. Absolutely nothing.
  [TAP! (Finger presses screen)]
  (The video began to play. And with it, the first clumsy, uncertain step towards a new path.)

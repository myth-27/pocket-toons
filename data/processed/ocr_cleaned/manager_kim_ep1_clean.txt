=== Manager Kim Episode 1 ===

  (The fluorescent lights hummed, a constant, dull drone. Another Tuesday. Another stack of invoices. Another day in the life of Kim Hyung-soo, Section Chief.)
  Mr. Park: Seriously, Section Chief Kim, you should try my method. Just 'delegate' the tedious stuff. That's what a manager does, right? Power of connections!
  Kim Hyung-soo (thinking): (Thought) Connections. He talks of them like they’re a shield. He has no idea what a true connection demands.
  Mr. Park: Anyway, I'm off to meet a client. Big fish. You just… keep at it, Section Chief.
  (Mr. Park sauntered out, his laughter echoing down the hall. Kim Hyung-soo didn't even look up. His phone vibrated.)
  Minji (text): Dad, I'm staying late for a study group. Walking home with Hyejin after. Don't worry!
  Kim Hyung-soo (thinking): (Thought) Don't worry. A child's words. Easy to say when you haven't seen the world. Every shadow holds a threat.
  [CLICK. CLACK. (Keyboard sounds)]
  (Hours later, the city lights blurred past his car window. The mundane office life faded, replaced by the familiar tension he carried in his bones.)
  [RING! RING! (Phone)]
  Kim Hyung-soo: Hello?
  Hyejin (frantic): Mr. Kim! It's Hyejin! Minji… she… some guys! At the old park! They won't let us go!
  Kim Hyung-soo: (Calmly) Location. Now.
  (His grip tightened on the steering wheel. The quiet manager was gone. The predator was awake.)
  [TIRES SCREECH. (Car turns sharply)]
  Thug 1: What's the rush, pretty? Just wanna talk. Maybe you can 'study' with us instead.
  Minji: Leave us alone! My dad will be here!
  Thug 2: Oh, 'Dad'? Is he gonna bring his walking stick?
  (A figure emerged from the shadows. Tall, unassuming. Kim Hyung-soo.)
  Thug 3: Look, it's grandpa. Go home, old man, before you get hurt.
  Kim Hyung-soo: You have exactly three seconds to walk away.
  Thug 1: Three seconds? What's he gonna do, lecture us?
  [THWACK! (Impact)]
  Thug 1: GAH!
  (Before the first thug could finish his sentence, Kim's hand had shot out, striking a nerve cluster in his neck. The thug crumpled, unconscious.)
  Thug 2: What the—?!
  [CRACK! (Bone impact)]
  (A swift, precise kick to the knee. Thug 2 screamed, collapsing. Kim pivoted, his eyes cold, locking onto Thug 3.)
  Thug 3: Y-you… you crazy old man!
  [WHUMP! (Body hitting ground)]
  (A short, brutal jab to the solar plexus. Thug 3 gasped for air, his eyes rolling back as he fell.)
  Minji: Dad…
  Kim Hyung-soo: Are you hurt, Minji?
  Minji: N-no… I’m okay.
  Kim Hyung-soo (thinking): (Thought) The world is a dangerous place. And I am its most dangerous guardian, for her.
  (He turned back to his daughter, his expression softening, but the dangerous glint in his eyes remained, a silent promise to any who would threaten what was his.)

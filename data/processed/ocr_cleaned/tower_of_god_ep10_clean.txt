=== Tower of God, Episode 10: 2F Lero Ro's Test ===

SUMMARY: The regulars gather for the second test, where Lero Ro, the Test Administrator and a Ranker, intervenes in a fight and introduces a pretest involving a Shinsu barrier. He explains that teams will fail if any member cannot pass, emphasizing the role of luck in the Tower. Surprisingly, Bam passes the Shinsu barrier effortlessly, causing confusion and frustration among the other participants.

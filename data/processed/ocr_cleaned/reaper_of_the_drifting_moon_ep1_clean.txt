=== Reaper of the Drifting Moon Episode 1 ===

  (The Cave of No Return. A forgotten abyss, carved into the very heart of the underworld. Here, children were brought, not to live, but to be reborn.)
  (Reborn as shadows. As weapons. As reapers.)
  [CLANG! SCRAPE!]
  MASTER GO: Faster, you worms! Do you wish to die a slow, agonizing death in the dark? Or a swift, clean one at the hands of your prey?
  [WHIP! THWACK!]
  PYO WOL (thinking): (My body aches. Every muscle screams. But pain is a luxury I cannot afford.)
  PYO WOL (thinking): (They took everything. My name, my family, my future. All that remains is this hollow shell, honed into a blade.)
  MASTER GO: You, boy! Number 7! Your stance is weak! Your eyes betray hesitation!
  PYO WOL (thinking): (Number 7. That is all I am to them. A number. A tool. But a tool can break free from its wielder.)
  [SHING! SWISH!]
  PYO WOL (thinking): (The 'Thousand Shadow Steps'. Designed to make one disappear, to become one with the darkness. But I will use it to escape the darkness.)
  [THUD! GRUNT!]
  FELLOW TRAINEE: G-Gah! You... you moved like a ghost!
  PYO WOL (thinking): (A ghost. Yes. That is what I must become. Unseen. Untraceable. Until the moment I strike.)
  MASTER GO: Hmph. Not bad, Number 7. You have potential. A cold heart, a sharp mind. The makings of a true killer.
  MASTER GO: But potential is useless without purpose. Remember your purpose, boy. To serve. To eliminate. To be the unseen hand that guides the world.
  PYO WOL (thinking): (My purpose is my own. To reclaim what was stolen. To make them pay.)
  [CRACK! (Sound of bone snapping, from a training dummy)]
  (Years passed in the gloom. Each day, a lesson in death. Each night, a silent vow.)
  (Pyo Wol learned to kill with his bare hands, with a needle, with a whisper. He learned to become a phantom.)
  [SWISH! THWACK! (Rapid, precise strikes)]
  MASTER GO: Excellent, Number 7! Your 'Ghostly Dagger' technique is flawless! No one will see it coming!
  PYO WOL (thinking): (Flawless. Good. Flawless is what I need to survive. Flawless is what I need to escape.)
  PYO WOL (thinking): (They believe they control me. They believe they have broken me. But beneath the ice, a fire burns.)
  [WHOOSH! (Pyo Wol moves incredibly fast)]
  MASTER GO: Incredible! He's faster than any of the others! A true prodigy of the shadows!
  PYO WOL (thinking): (Prodigy or not, I am a prisoner. And prisoners dream of freedom.)
  (The whispers of the outside world were faint, but they reached even the deepest parts of the Cave. Rumors of a great war, of shifting powers in the Murim.)
  (And with these rumors, a new directive came down from the unseen masters.)
  MASTER GO: Listen up, all of you! The time of your deployment draws near! The Murim is in turmoil, and our masters require their finest blades!
  [MURMURS (Among the trainees)]
  PYO WOL (thinking): (Deployment. To the outside world. My chance.)
  PYO WOL (thinking): (They will send me out as their weapon. But I will be my own.)
  (Pyo Wol looked at his reflection in a small, still puddle of water. A boy’s face, but with eyes that held the emptiness of a thousand deaths.)
  PYO WOL (thinking): (The Reaper of the Drifting Moon. Soon, the Murim will know that name. And it will tremble.)
  [DRIP... DRIP...]

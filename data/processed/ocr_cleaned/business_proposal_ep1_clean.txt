=== Business Proposal Episode 1 ===

  (Shin Ha-ri. My life motto? Work hard, eat well, and pay off the family debt.)
  Ha-ri (thinking): (Thought) Ugh, another month, another mountain of bills. Our fried chicken restaurant isn't exactly a goldmine.
  Ha-ri (thinking): (Thought) If only I could win the lottery... or marry rich... but who am I kidding? I'm just an ordinary food researcher.
  []
  Ha-ri: Hello? Young-seo, what's up?
  Young-seo: Ha-ri! My dearest, most beautiful, most amazing best friend! I need your help! Desperately!
  Ha-ri (thinking): (Thought) Oh no, that tone... She's in trouble again.
  Ha-ri: What is it this time? Another blind date your dad set up?
  Young-seo: Exactly! He's trying to marry me off! And this one... this one is a real catch. My dad will kill me if I mess it up, but I just can't!
  Young-seo: Please, Ha-ri! Just one more time! Pretend to be me, go on the date, and scare him off! Make him reject me!
  Ha-ri: Young-seo, we agreed no more! Last time I had to pretend to be a cult leader!
  Young-seo: I'll give you a brand new luxury bag! The one you've been eyeing! And all the money you need for your family's restaurant!
  Ha-ri (thinking): (Thought) A luxury bag... and money for the restaurant... My resolve is weakening. Damn you, Young-seo, you know my weaknesses!
  Ha-ri: Fine! But this is the last time! What's the plan?
  Young-seo: You're the best! Okay, here's the info: Tonight, 7 PM, 'La Belle' restaurant. His name is Kang Tae-mu. Make him run for the hills!
  (Later that evening, at the upscale restaurant 'La Belle'.)
  Ha-ri (thinking): (Thought) Alright, Ha-ri, time to put on your ultimate 'scare-him-off' persona. Operation: Reject Me! is a go!
  Ha-ri (thinking): (Thought) I'll be loud, obnoxious, obsessed with money, and maybe a little unhinged. No man in his right mind would want to marry *that*.
  []
  Ha-ri (thinking): (Thought) Here he comes. Deep breaths. Prepare for battle!
  []
  Tae-mu: Ms. Jin Young-seo? I am Kang Tae-mu.
  Ha-ri (thinking): (Thought) Wait... Kang Tae-mu? That name sounds familiar... No, it can't be...!
  Ha-ri (thinking): (Thought) The CEO of GOFOOD! The one who just took over our company! The infamous 'God of Work'!
  Ha-ri (thinking): (Thought) Oh no, oh no, oh no! This is a disaster! I'm going to get fired! Or worse, he'll recognize me! My life is over!
  Tae-mu: Is something wrong, Ms. Jin? You look a little... pale.
  Ha-ri: P-pale? Me? Ha! Never! I'm just... overwhelmed by your blinding handsomeness, Mr. Kang! (Thought: Keep it together, Ha-ri! Act normal! No, act crazy!)
  Ha-ri: So, Mr. Kang! What's your annual income? Do you own multiple buildings? Are you a chaebol heir with a private jet? Because I only date men who can buy me a new luxury car every month!
  Tae-mu (thinking): (Thought) A luxury car every month? This is... unexpected. Jin Young-seo's profile said she was refined and elegant.
  Tae-mu: I see. You seem to have a very... direct approach.
  Ha-ri: Directness is my middle name! And my first name is 'Money'! So, spill the beans, Mr. Kang! How rich are you, really?
  Ha-ri (thinking): (Thought) This is it! He's definitely going to be disgusted. He'll reject me on the spot! Mission accomplished!
  Tae-mu: Very well. Since you're so direct, I'll be direct too.
  Tae-mu: Ms. Jin Young-seo. I have decided.
  Ha-ri (thinking): (Thought) Yes! The rejection! Freedom!
  Tae-mu: I will marry you.
  [(Ha-ri drops her water glass)]
  Ha-ri: WHAAAAT?!

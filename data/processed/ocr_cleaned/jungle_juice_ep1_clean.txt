=== Jungle Juice Episode 1 ===

  (Suchan Jang. The name echoed through the halls of Daehan University. Top of his class. Impeccable grades. A future as bright as his perfectly styled hair.)
  (He was the epitome of success, the golden boy everyone admired. A life meticulously crafted, every detail polished to perfection.)
  Suchan Jang (thinking): (thought) Another perfect score. Another day closer to my future. Just maintain this pace, Suchan. Don't slip up.
  [ZZZZZ]
  Suchan Jang (thinking): (thought) A mosquito? In my apartment? Ugh, seriously?
  [SLAP!]
  Suchan Jang (thinking): (thought) Got it. Annoying little pest. Now, back to studying.
  (He didn't know that one tiny bite, one insignificant moment, would shatter his perfect world. He didn't know that the cheap 'Jungle Juice' insect repellent he'd bought on a whim was about to rewrite his very existence.)
  [PSHHHHT!]
  Suchan Jang (thinking): (thought) What is this... burning sensation? My back... it feels like it's on fire!
  [RIP! (sound of skin tearing)]
  Suchan Jang: GAH! What the hell?!
  [CRACKLE! POP! (bones shifting, skin stretching)]
  Suchan Jang (thinking): (thought) No... no, this can't be real. My back... something is growing out of my back!
  [FLUTTER! (a faint, papery sound)]
  Suchan Jang (thinking): (thought) Wings. I have... mosquito wings. This is a nightmare. A horrible, disgusting nightmare!
  (The next morning, Suchan Jang, the golden boy, woke up to a new reality. His reflection stared back, a stranger with a monstrous secret.)
  Suchan Jang (thinking): (thought) How am I supposed to go to class? How am I supposed to live like this? My life... it's over.
  [RUMBLE (stomach growling unnaturally loud)]
  Suchan Jang (thinking): (thought) What is this hunger? This... craving? It's not for food. It's for... blood?
  (He tried to hide them, to suppress the monstrous appendages that now defined him. A bulky jacket, hunched shoulders, anything to conceal the truth.)
  Suchan Jang (thinking): (thought) Just act normal. Don't let anyone see. Don't let anyone know. My reputation... everything I worked for...
  [SWISH (a slight movement under his jacket)]
  Suchan Jang (thinking): (thought) Damn it! Control yourself, Suchan! Don't let them twitch! One wrong move and it's all over.
  (The world he knew was gone, replaced by a terrifying struggle for normalcy. But a creature like him couldn't stay hidden forever. Not when his instincts screamed for release.)
  Suchan Jang (thinking): (thought) I need to find answers. I need to fix this. Or I'll become nothing more than a monster.

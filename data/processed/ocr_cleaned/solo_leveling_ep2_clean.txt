=== The Blood of Ae Butterfly - Episode 2: Parasite ===

SUMMARY: Maehwa Baek, haunted by a past incident involving blood, is confronted by former classmates who accuse him of being a 'bug' or parasite. Later, while celebrating his birthday with his friend Huina, Maehwa realizes that Huina has been replaced by a parasite, leading to a violent confrontation.

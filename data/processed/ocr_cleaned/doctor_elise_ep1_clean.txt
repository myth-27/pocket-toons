=== Doctor Elise Episode 1 ===

  (In a world where life and death hang by a thread, one woman stands at the precipice, wielding a scalpel with unparalleled precision.)
  Dr. Song (Elise): Suction. Forceps. Clamp.
  Nurse Kim: Doctor, the patient's blood pressure is dropping rapidly!
  Dr. Song (Elise): Stay calm. We're almost there. I need 10cc of epinephrine, now!
  Dr. Song (Elise) (thinking): This is it. The moment of truth. Every second counts. Every decision is critical.
  (Dr. Song, a brilliant surgeon, dedicated her life to saving others. It was her atonement, her penance for a past life she desperately wanted to forget.)
  [BEEP-BEEP-BEEP (heart monitor flatlining)]
  Dr. Song (Elise): No! Not on my watch! Charge to 200!
  Nurse Lee: Charging!
  [ZAP! (defibrillator)]
  Nurse Kim: Pulse is back! It's weak, but it's there!
  Dr. Song (Elise): Good. Now, let's close this up. Carefully.
  Dr. Song (Elise) (thinking): Another life saved. It's a small comfort, but it's something. Perhaps, one day, I can truly forgive myself for the monster I once was.
  (After a grueling 12-hour surgery, Dr. Song boarded a plane for a medical conference abroad. Exhaustion weighed heavily on her, but her mind was already planning the next challenge.)
  Flight Attendant: Ladies and gentlemen, we are experiencing some unexpected turbulence. Please remain seated and keep your seatbelts fastened.
  [RUMBLE! SHAKE! (plane turbulence)]
  Dr. Song (Elise) (thinking): Turbulence? This feels… different.
  [SCREEEEECH! CRACK! (metal tearing)]
  Passenger: Ahhh! What's happening!?
  Dr. Song (Elise) (thinking): No… not like this. Not when I finally found my purpose.
  [EXPLOSION! (plane crash)]
  (Darkness consumed her. Then, a faint light, a whisper of a forgotten memory…)
  [GASP! (Elise waking up)]
  Elise: What… where am I? My head… it hurts.
  Elise (thinking): This room… these curtains… This isn't my apartment. This isn't a hospital room. This is…
  Elise (thinking): This is my room from my first life! The Clorance Dukedom! But… that's impossible. I died. I was executed!
  [CREAK (door opening)]
  Maid: Young Lady Elise? You're awake! Oh, thank the heavens! You've been unconscious for two days after your fall.
  Elise (thinking): Fall? Two days? No… this can't be real. I'm… I'm back. I'm Elise de Clorance again. The villainess…
  Elise (thinking): But I'm so young. This must be before… before everything. Before I became the empress's rival. Before I was executed by the Crown Prince.
  Maid: Are you feeling alright, Young Lady? You look a bit pale.
  Elise: I'm… I'm fine, Lily. Just a little disoriented.
  Elise (thinking): This is a second chance. No, a third chance. I won't make the same mistakes. I won't be the villainess. I won't pursue the Crown Prince. I will use the knowledge I gained as Dr. Song.
  Elise (thinking): I will become a doctor in this life. I will save lives. That is my new destiny.
  (With a newfound resolve, the notorious villainess, Elise de Clorance, began her journey to rewrite her fate and become the greatest physician the empire had ever seen.)

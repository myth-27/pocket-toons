=== Sidekicks Episode 1 ===

  (In a city perpetually under the shadow of towering heroes and terrifying villains, not all capes are created equal.)
  Jinwoo (Starlight) (thinking): Another morning. Another patrol. Another day of being... Starlight.
  Jinwoo (Starlight) (thinking): My official title? 'Captain Stellar's Junior Associate'. My actual job? Human shield, glorified errand boy, and occasionally, a very shiny distraction.
  Jinwoo (Starlight) (thinking): Don't get me wrong, Captain Stellar is amazing. He's strong, kind, and he genuinely cares about the city. He just... doesn't always see me.
  [BEEP BEEP BEEP]
  Captain Stellar (via comms): Starlight, alert! We have a Code Red downtown. The 'Rubble Rouser' is back. Looks like he's targeting the new financial district.
  Jinwoo (Starlight): Understood, Captain! On my way!
  Jinwoo (Starlight) (thinking): Code Red... Rubble Rouser... This is my chance! I've studied his patterns, his weaknesses. I can actually contribute this time!
  [WHOOSH]
  (Jinwoo, as Starlight, soared through the city, his custom-built flight suit barely keeping pace with his surging adrenaline.)
  [CRASH! RUMBLE! SHATTER!]
  Rubble Rouser: HAHAHA! This city will crumble! Just like all your hopes and dreams, heroes!
  Jinwoo (Starlight): Rubble Rouser! Your reign of terror ends now!
  Jinwoo (Starlight) (thinking): He's distracted! This is it! A focused energy blast to his core generator, just like I practiced!
  [ZAP! WHOOSH!]
  Rubble Rouser: Gah! A pesky fly! Get out of my way, Starlight!
  [THUD!]
  Jinwoo (Starlight) (thinking): Urgh! He's stronger than I anticipated... But I can still create an opening!
  [BOOM! CRACKLE!]
  Captain Stellar: Stand back, Starlight! I've got this!
  Jinwoo (Starlight): Captain! No, wait, I almost had him!
  (In a flash of blinding light, Captain Stellar descended, his iconic 'Stellar Nova' attack engulfing the Rubble Rouser in a dazzling, incapacitating energy field.)
  [KABOOM! FIZZLE.]
  Rubble Rouser: No! Not again! Cur... curse you, Captain Stellar!
  Captain Stellar: Another day, another save! Good work keeping him occupied, Starlight. You're a real lifesaver!
  Jinwoo (Starlight) (thinking): Occupied... Right.
  Reporter (on TV): Captain Stellar once again proves why he's the city's greatest protector, single-handedly neutralizing the notorious Rubble Rouser in a spectacular display of power!
  Jinwoo (Starlight) (thinking): Single-handedly... I was there. I took a hit. I even got a blast in.
  Jinwoo (Starlight) (thinking): But no one ever sees the sidekick. No one ever remembers Starlight.
  Jinwoo (Starlight) (thinking): Is this all I'll ever be? A warm-up act? A footnote in someone else's legend?
  Jinwoo (Starlight) (thinking): No. I won't let it be. I have my own light to shine. One day, they'll see it. One day, they'll see me.

=== The S-Classes That I Raised Episode 1 ===

  (The world changed ten years ago. Dungeons appeared, monsters poured out, and with them, Hunters. Those blessed with abilities to fight back.)
  HAN YOOJIN (thinking): (thought) Me? I was an F-class. The lowest of the low. A useless burden, barely capable of clearing a Rank F dungeon, and even then, I usually came out bruised and battered.
  HAN YOOJIN (thinking): (thought) My younger brother, Han Yoohyun, was different. He was a prodigy. An S-class. The youngest ever. He carried the weight of our family, of the world, on his shoulders. While I... I was just a burden.
  (He remembered the end. The catastrophic dungeon break. The desperate, futile fight. The overwhelming despair as the city crumbled around him.)
  HAN YOOJIN (thinking): (thought) I died. Crushed under a monster's claw, watching the city burn. My only regret was Yoohyun. I couldn't protect him. I couldn't even stand beside him. He deserved so much more than a brother like me.
  [GASP]
  HAN YOOJIN: (dialogue, groggy) Ugh... My head...
  (Han Yoojin blinked, the harsh morning light stinging his eyes. He was in his old, familiar apartment. The one he'd moved out of years ago, before everything went to hell.)
  HAN YOOJIN (thinking): (thought) This... this isn't right. I was just... I was dead. This room, this cheap wallpaper... it's from five years ago.
  [RUSTLE (of sheets)]
  HAN YOOJIN (thinking): (thought) The calendar on the wall... September 1st, 20XX. This date... it's before the Great Dungeon Break. Before Yoohyun became an S-class and started carrying the world alone.
  HAN YOOJIN (thinking): (thought) Is this... a dream? A hallucination before death? Am I just going insane?
  SYSTEM: [Welcome, Awakened. Skill: My Brat is the Best (S-Class Hunter Cultivation) has been activated.]
  HAN YOOJIN (thinking): (thought) My... My Brat is the Best? That useless F-class skill I got when I first awakened? The one that just gave me a tiny buff when I was near my younger sibling? But it says... S-Class Hunter Cultivation?
  HAN YOOJIN (thinking): (thought) No, wait. This isn't just a buff. This is a full-blown system message. Like the ones the high-ranking hunters talked about, the ones that appeared when their unique skills truly manifested. And the skill name itself... it's different.
  HAN YOOJIN (thinking): (thought) I remember now. Before I died, a strange, warm light enveloped me. A whisper... 'A second chance.' It wasn't a hallucination. It was real.
  HAN YOOJIN: (dialogue, voice trembling) I... I went back in time.
  [THUMP-THUMP (heartbeat)]
  HAN YOOJIN (thinking): (thought) This isn't a dream. This is real. I have a second chance. A chance to change things. A chance to save Yoohyun from the path of suffering he walked in my past life.
  HAN YOOJIN (thinking): (thought) In my past life, Yoohyun pushed himself too hard. He fought alone, shouldering every burden, every expectation. He grew distant, cold, burdened by responsibilities. I couldn't help him. I was too weak, too useless.
  SYSTEM: [Skill details: Allows the user to foster the growth of a designated 'Brat' (sibling or close relative) into an S-Class Hunter. Provides various support effects, growth buffs, and unique opportunities. Initial target set: Han Yoohyun.]
  HAN YOOJIN (thinking): (thought) This is it. This is my chance. I won't let him suffer alone this time. I won't be a useless F-class burden. I'll be the best older brother. I'll raise him properly, protect him, make sure he's happy.
  HAN YOOJIN: (dialogue, resolute) Yoohyun... I'm coming for you.
  (He quickly got out of bed, his mind racing with a mix of shock and fierce determination. He needed to find Yoohyun. He needed to understand this powerful, reborn skill. Most importantly, he needed to prepare.)
  [CREAK (floorboards)]
  HAN YOOJIN (thinking): (thought) First, I need to check the news. What's the current state of the dungeons? Who's who in the Hunter Association? I need to remember every detail that could give me an advantage.
  HAN YOOJIN (thinking): (thought) And then... I need to find Yoohyun before he makes any rash decisions. Before he gets involved with anyone shady. Before he pushes himself to the brink, just like last time.
  HAN YOOJIN (thinking): (thought) This time, I'll be the one raising the S-class. And my S-class... will be the strongest, the happiest, and the one who doesn't have to carry the world alone.
  [CLICK (door opening)]
  HAN YOOJIN: (dialogue, to himself, a small, determined smile forming) Let's do this, Han Yoojin. For Yoohyun. For a better future.

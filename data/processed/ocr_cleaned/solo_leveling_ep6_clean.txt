=== Episode 6: Standing on the Brink ===

SUMMARY: Maehwa Baek, the Fourth Butterfly, is formally introduced to the Garden's researchers, who express relief at his return. Despite Doctor Seo's encouragement, Maehwa overhears other researchers expressing distrust due to his past loss of control, leading him to resolve to work harder. The episode concludes with a mysterious fog appearing in the indoor garden, followed by the sudden appearance of a 'Bug'.

=== Hell Is Other People Episode 1 ===

  (The city of Seoul. A sprawling concrete jungle, indifferent to the dreams and anxieties of the millions who flock to its heart.)
  YOON JONG-WOO (thinking): (Thought) Finally. Seoul. No more small town, no more dead-end future. This is it. My fresh start.
  [TRAFFIC ROAR. SIRENS WAIL. CITY HUM.]
  YOON JONG-WOO (thinking): (Thought) The internship at the IT company... it's not much, but it's a foot in the door. I just need a place to live, something cheap.
  (Days blurred into a frustrating search. Every apartment too expensive, every gosiwon too squalid. Until he found it. Eden Studio.)
  YOON JONG-WOO (thinking): (Thought) 'Eden Studio.' The name sounds... nice, at least. And the rent... it's almost too good to be true.
  [CREAK. SQUEAK.]
  LANDLADY: Oh, hello there, young man! You must be Mr. Yoon Jong-woo. Welcome, welcome! Come right in!
  YOON JONG-WOO: Ah, yes. I'm Yoon Jong-woo. I called about the room.
  LANDLADY: Of course! Such a polite young man. Room 303 is perfect for you. It just opened up. Lucky you!
  YOON JONG-WOO (thinking): (Thought) Her smile... it's a bit too wide. And her eyes... they're not smiling at all.
  [FOOTSTEPS ECHO. FAINT WHIRRING SOUND.]
  LANDLADY: Here we are! Cozy, isn't it? Everything you need right here. Bed, desk, even a mini-fridge!
  YOON JONG-WOO (thinking): (Thought) 'Cozy' is one word for it. 'Cramped' is another. This is barely bigger than my old closet. And the smell...
  [DUSTY SMELL. CLOSETED AIR.]
  YOON JONG-WOO: It's... fine. The bathroom is shared, right?
  LANDLADY: Oh, yes! Very convenient. And the kitchen too! Everyone here is like family. We all look out for each other.
  YOON JONG-WOO (thinking): (Thought) 'Family.' The way she said it... it didn't sound comforting. It sounded... possessive.
  [DOOR CREAKS SHUT. LOCK CLICKS.]
  YOON JONG-WOO (thinking): (Thought) Okay, Jong-woo. It's temporary. Just until you get on your feet. You can handle this. It's just a gosiwon.
  [DISTANT COUGH. MUTTERING FROM NEXT ROOM. DRIPPING WATER.]
  YOON JONG-WOO (thinking): (Thought) What was that? Sounded like someone... talking to themselves? Or to something else?
  [SCRAPE. SCRATCH. A LOW CHUCKLE.]
  YOON JONG-WOO (thinking): (Thought) This place... it's not just cheap. It's unsettling. I hope I didn't make a mistake.
  [FAN WHIRS LOUDLY. JONG-WOO SIGHS.]
  (The first night in Eden Studio. The sounds of the city faded, replaced by the strange, intimate symphony of a building that felt like it was holding its breath.)
  YOON JONG-WOO (thinking): (Thought) Just sleep, Jong-woo. You have work tomorrow. Don't think about it. Don't think about them.
  [FLOORBOARD CREAK. A THUMP FROM ABOVE. A SOFT, REPETITIVE TAPPING.]
  YOON JONG-WOO (thinking): (Thought) What the hell is that tapping? Is someone... watching me?

=== The Player Who Can't Level Up Episode 1 ===

  (The world changed ten years ago. Towers, gates to other dimensions, appeared, filled with monsters and untold treasures.)
  (And with them, 'Players' awakened. Individuals blessed with unique skills and the ability to level up, growing stronger with each monster slain.)
  (They climbed the towers, protecting humanity, becoming heroes... and gods.)
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): Ten years. Ten years since the first tower appeared. Ten years since I awakened.
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): And ten years of being stuck at level 1.
  [GROWL]
  Kim Gi-Gyu: Damn it! Another Grolak Goblin.
  [CLANG! (rusty sword scraping against thick hide)]
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): My rusty sword barely scratches its hide. This is all I can do. Swing, parry, dodge.
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): No flashy skills, no powerful attacks. Just the same old grind in this F-rank dungeon.
  [THWACK! (Gi-Gyu kicks the goblin, it stumbles)]
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): My 'skill'... [Absorption]. It lets me absorb the properties of anything I touch.
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): But what good is absorbing the 'hardness' of a rock when I can't even swing a sword properly? Or the 'sharpness' of a dull blade?
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): I've tried everything. Absorbing monster parts, dungeon walls, even other players' equipment (they didn't like that).
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): It just... changes my body slightly. Never enough to make a difference. Never enough to level up.
  [CRACK! (Gi-Gyu finally lands a solid hit, goblin falls)]
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): One down. Only a hundred more to go before I can even think about buying a decent meal.
  [DING!]
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): Status window... don't even bother.
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): [Kim Gi-Gyu, Lv. 1]... Always Lv. 1. No experience gained. No skill points. Nothing.
  [FOOTSTEPS (distant, confident)]
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): Sounds like a party. Better hide. Don't want to get in their way.
  Player A: Hey, isn't that the 'Eternal Novice'?
  Player B: Oh, him? Still trying to clear the F-rank dungeon? Pathetic.
  Player A: He's been at it for years. What a waste of an awakening.
  [WHOOSH (as they pass, uncaring)]
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): Pathetic, huh? Maybe. But what else am I supposed to do?
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): I can't level up. My 'skill' is useless. Everyone says so. But... I have to try. There has to be a way.
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): If I can't level up, then I'll find another path. Even if it means going to places no Level 1 should ever go.
  [RUMBLE (a distant, deeper tremor)]
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): The whispers... of a new gate opening. A C-rank. Far too dangerous for me.
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): But... what if? What if my 'Absorption' skill isn't useless? What if it's just... misunderstood?
  Kim Gi-Gyu (thinking): I've been stuck in the shallow end for too long. It's time to dive into the deep.
  [THUMP (Gi-Gyu's fist hitting his palm, resolute)]
  Kim Gi-Gyu: Fine. Let's see what a Level 1 can do in a C-rank dungeon.

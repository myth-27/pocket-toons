=== Quest Supremacy Episode 1 ===

  (The alarm blared, a harsh siren signaling the start of another day, another cycle of dread.)
  [BEEP BEEP BEEP]
  Kim Su-hyeon (thinking): Ugh... already?
  Kim Su-hyeon (thinking): My stomach already feels like it's tied in knots. Just thinking about school...
  (Kim Su-hyeon, a second-year high school student, dragged himself out of bed. His reflection in the mirror showed a boy with tired eyes and slumped shoulders. A perfect target.)
  [FOOTSTEPS (faint)]
  (He tried to be invisible, hugging the walls of the school hallway, but it was always futile.)
  Bully Leader: Well, well, well. Look what the cat dragged in.
  Kim Su-hyeon (thinking): N-no...
  Bully Minion: Hey, Su-hyeon! Did you bring our 'tribute' today? Or are you planning on being a cheapskate again?
  Kim Su-hyeon: I-I don't have any money today. I really don't...
  Bully Leader: What was that? Did you just talk back, you worm?
  [GRAB!]
  Kim Su-hyeon: Agh!
  (He was dragged into a secluded alleyway behind the school, a familiar ritual.)
  Bully Minion: You think you can just show up empty-handed? We've got to teach you a lesson.
  [THWACK! (Punch to the gut)]
  Kim Su-hyeon: H-huh...!
  Kim Su-hyeon (thinking): Why... why is this always me? Why can't I ever fight back?
  Bully Leader: That's for being late. And this...
  [KICK! (To the leg)]
  Kim Su-hyeon: A-ah! P-please... stop...
  Kim Su-hyeon (thinking): I hate this. I hate being weak. I hate myself for letting this happen every single day.
  Bully Leader: That's enough for today. Don't forget tomorrow, worm. And bring double.
  [FOOTSTEPS RECEDING]
  (Slumped against the cold brick wall, Su-hyeon felt the familiar ache spread through his body. His head throbbed, his vision blurred.)
  Kim Su-hyeon (thinking): If only... if only I could be stronger. Just once. Strong enough to make them regret it.
  [DING!]
  Kim Su-hyeon: Huh?
  (A translucent blue window flickered into existence before Kim Su-hyeon's eyes, shimmering with an ethereal glow.)
  ([NEW QUEST AVAILABLE!])
  Kim Su-hyeon (thinking): What... what is this? A game screen? Am I dreaming?
  ([QUEST: The First Step])
  ([Description: Overcome your fear and confront the bullies who torment you.])
  ([Conditions: Successfully land a punch on the Bully Leader.])
  ([Reward: Stat increase (random), Skill Card (random), Quest Points (10)])
  ([Failure Penalty: Permanent 'Coward' debuff, -5 to all stats])
  Kim Su-hyeon (thinking): A permanent debuff?! This isn't a game! This is real!
  Kim Su-hyeon (thinking): But... a stat increase? A skill card? Could this be... my chance?
  Kim Su-hyeon (thinking): Confront them? Land a punch? They just beat me to a pulp! How am I supposed to do that?!
  Kim Su-hyeon (thinking): But if I fail... a 'Coward' debuff... and -5 to all stats. That would make my life even more hellish than it already is.
  Kim Su-hyeon (thinking): This is it. This is my only chance to change. To stop being the weakling everyone steps on.
  [CLENCH (fist)]
  Kim Su-hyeon: No more. I won't be a punching bag anymore. I have to do this.

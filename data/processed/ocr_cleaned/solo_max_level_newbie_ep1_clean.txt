=== Solo Max-Level Newbie Episode 1 ===

  Jinhyuk: My head... what the hell happened?
  (The familiar ceiling above him was gone, replaced by rough-hewn stone. The air, thick with dust and the metallic scent of blood, was definitely not his apartment.)
  Jinhyuk (thinking): This isn't my room. This isn't even my apartment building. This... this is the 'Tower of Trials'.
  Jinhyuk (thinking): The game I spent ten years mastering, 'Tower of Trials'. It became reality.
  SYSTEM: Welcome, Player. The 'Tower of Trials' has begun.
  SYSTEM: Objective: Conquer the Tower.
  Jinhyuk (thinking): Conquer the Tower? Easy for you to say. Most people are probably freaking out right now.
  (Around him, countless others lay sprawled on the cold ground, just as confused. Some were screaming, others crying, their faces etched with terror.)
  Jinhyuk (thinking): Pathetic. But understandable. This isn't a game for them. It's a death sentence.
  Jinhyuk (thinking): But for me... this is a second chance. A chance to use everything I learned.
  Jinhyuk: Alright, let's see the damage.
  SYSTEM: Player: Jinhyuk
  SYSTEM: Level: 1
  SYSTEM: Strength: 10, Agility: 10, Stamina: 10, Magic: 10, Luck: 10
  Jinhyuk (thinking): Base stats. As expected. But my knowledge... that's my real stat.
  (A faint, ominous glow emanated from a nearby archway. The first floor. The tutorial area.)
  [GROWL]
  (A grotesque green-skinned creature, barely taller than a child, lunged forward with a rusty dagger. A Goblin.)
  Other Player 1: A-a monster!
  Other Player 2: Help!
  Jinhyuk (thinking): Amateurs. They don't even know how to hold a stick.
  Jinhyuk (thinking): Goblins. Weak to blunt force, slow reaction time. Standard tutorial fodder.
  Jinhyuk: Move.
  [WHOOSH]
  [CRACK]
  Jinhyuk (thinking): No weapons yet. Fine. My fists are enough for these low-level trash.
  [THUD]
  [SMASH]
  SYSTEM: You have defeated a Goblin.
  SYSTEM: You have gained 10 EXP.
  SYSTEM: Level Up! Level: 2
  SYSTEM: Stat points acquired: 5
  Jinhyuk (thinking): As expected. The grind begins. Strength: +3, Agility: +2. Prioritize what's needed for early game efficiency.
  (More goblins appeared, drawn by the commotion. Jinhyuk moved like a phantom, a blur of calculated strikes, each one precise and lethal.)
  [SLASH! THWACK! CRUNCH!]
  Other Player 3: H-how... he's so fast!
  Other Player 4: Is he... a pro gamer?
  Jinhyuk (thinking): Pro gamer? More like a max-level veteran trapped in a newbie's body. And this is just the beginning.
  SYSTEM: You have completed the quest 'Clear the First Floor Tutorial Area'.
  SYSTEM: Reward: Skill 'Basic Swordsmanship (F)', 100 EXP, 50 Gold.
  Jinhyuk (thinking): Basic Swordsmanship... useful. But I know where the real treasure lies.
  (A new portal shimmered, leading deeper into the Tower, beckoning him towards the next challenge.)
  Jinhyuk: Alright, Tower. Let's see if you can still surprise me.
  Jinhyuk (thinking): This isn't a game anymore. This is my new reality. And I'm going to dominate it.

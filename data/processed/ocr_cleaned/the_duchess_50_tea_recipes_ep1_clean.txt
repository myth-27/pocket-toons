=== The Duchess' 50 Tea Recipes Episode 1 ===

  Chloe (thinking): Ugh... My head... it feels like it's been split open and put back together wrong.
  (Park Hajeong, an ordinary office worker, had died. A sudden, senseless accident involving a truck and a moment of distraction. One moment, she was worrying about a deadline; the next, she was... here.)
  Chloe (thinking): This isn't my cramped studio apartment. This isn't even my body.
  [GASP]
  Chloe (thinking): These hands... so pale, so delicate. And this room... it's lavish, draped in silks and velvets, yet the air is so heavy, so cold.
  Chloe (thinking): A mirror...
  [SWISH]
  Chloe (thinking): No way... This can't be me.
  Chloe (thinking): Blonde hair, eyes the color of a winter sky... And this sickly pallor, these sunken cheeks. This face... It's Duchess Chloe Vadenberg!
  (The infamous Duchess. The woman known throughout the empire for her fragile health, her lack of social graces, and her utterly neglected existence as the wife of the formidable Duke Alphonse.)
  Chloe (thinking): I died and woke up as *her*? The woman who was practically a ghost in her own mansion, whose husband barely knew her name?
  Chloe (thinking): My chest feels so tight, a dull ache that never leaves. Is this her chronic illness? It's suffocating, like a heavy stone pressing down on my lungs.
  [COUGH]
  [PANT]
  [KNOCK KNOCK]
  [CREAK]
  Alphonse: Duchess.
  Chloe (thinking): !
  Chloe: Duke Alphonse.
  (His voice was as cold and sharp as ever, his eyes devoid of warmth. The man who had never once looked at her with anything but indifference.)
  Alphonse: I heard you've recovered consciousness. See to your duties. The upcoming banquet requires your presence.
  Chloe (thinking): My duties...?
  Alphonse: Do not disappoint me again.
  [SWISH]
  [THUD]
  Chloe (thinking): He didn't even wait for a reply.
  Chloe (thinking): Just like in the novel. Cold, distant, and utterly uncaring. This can't be my life. Not again. Not after everything I went through in my previous one.
  Chloe (thinking): To be sickly, ignored, ridiculed, and eventually cast aside... No. I refuse to live like this. I refuse to be a tragic side character in someone else's story.
  Chloe (thinking): My health is paramount. Without it, I'm nothing. This body is so weak, so fragile. I can feel its limits already.
  Chloe (thinking): What did Park Hajeong do when she was stressed? Or sick? When she needed comfort or clarity?
  (In her previous life, Park Hajeong had been a passionate connoisseur of tea. From robust black teas to delicate green infusions, she found solace, joy, and even inspiration in its myriad forms and complex flavors.)
  Chloe (thinking): Tea... Yes! Tea! It's the perfect solution. It's the one thing I know, the one skill I possess that could actually make a difference here.
  Chloe (thinking): It can improve my health. It can soothe my mind. It can give me the strength I need. And perhaps... it can be my path to independence, to a life where I'm not just a forgotten Duchess.
  Chloe (thinking): I won't just survive. I'll thrive. And I'll start with a single cup, a single step towards reclaiming my life.
  Chloe (thinking): I will make this body strong. I will make this Duchess relevant. I will make my own destiny.
  [RING]
  Maid: Duchess? You rang?
  Chloe: Yes. Please prepare a cup of tea for me.
  Maid: Tea? But... you usually only drink water, Duchess, due to your delicate stomach.
  Chloe: Not today. I want a strong, invigorating black tea. And please, make sure it's brewed properly. Not too weak, not too bitter.
  Maid: A-as you wish, Duchess.
  (The maid, Sarah, looked utterly surprised. The Duchess had never shown such specific interest in anything before, let alone something as common as tea.)
  Chloe (thinking): This is it. My first step. The first of fifty... or perhaps more.
  Chloe (thinking): The Duchess's 50 Tea Recipes... no, it will be *my* 50 Tea Recipes.
  [CLINK]

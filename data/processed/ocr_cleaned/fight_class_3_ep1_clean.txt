=== Fight Class 3 Episode 1 ===

  (The alleyway smells like stale beer and broken dreams. A familiar scent.)
  BULLY 1: Look at this loser, still crawling around. Thought you'd learn your lesson yesterday, huh, Yu?
  Ijin Yu (thinking): (Thought) My head aches. My ribs ache. Everything aches. It's always like this.
  BULLY 2: He's just asking for another beating. What a pathetic worm.
  [THWACK!]
  Ijin Yu (thinking): (Thought) My vision blurs. I taste blood. Why do I always freeze? Why can't I just... fight back?
  BULLY 1: Stay down, trash. Don't even think about getting up.
  [KICK! CRUNCH!]
  Ijin Yu (thinking): (Thought) The pain isn't the worst part. It's the helplessness. The absolute, soul-crushing helplessness.
  (They leave eventually, their laughter echoing as Ijin lies there, a crumpled heap of bruised flesh and shattered pride.)
  Ijin Yu (thinking): (Thought) Another day, another reminder. This is my life. A punching bag. A background character in everyone else's story.
  [GROAN...]
  (Later, limping through the bustling streets, Ijin tries to disappear into the crowd. But the stares, the whispers... they feel like physical blows.)
  Ijin Yu (thinking): (Thought) If I just had a little strength. Just enough to not be this... weak. Is that too much to ask?
  (His eyes drift to a faded poster plastered on a utility pole. It depicts two figures locked in a grappling hold, sweat flying.)
  (The text beneath is simple, stark: "Jitae's Gym. Fight Class 3. Learn to fight. Learn to live.")
  Ijin Yu (thinking): (Thought) Fight Class 3? What even is that? Another place for me to get beaten up?
  [CLANG! THUD!]
  (He glances towards the source of the sound – a grimy building with a heavy, metal door. Through a small, barred window, he catches a glimpse of movement. Shadows grappling, hitting pads.)
  STUDENT 1 (off-screen): Damn, that last spar with Jitae-hyung nearly killed me. Fight Class 3 is no joke.
  STUDENT 2 (off-screen): You think? He barely broke a sweat. But hey, at least we're not like those guys who quit after the first week.
  Ijin Yu (thinking): (Thought) Quit after the first week... I probably wouldn't even last a day. But...
  (He looks down at his scraped knuckles, then back at the poster. The words 'Learn to fight. Learn to live.' seem to burn into his mind.)
  Ijin Yu (thinking): (Thought) What if? What if there's a chance? A tiny, impossible chance to change this? To not be this... pathetic?
  (His hand, trembling slightly, reaches out towards the heavy metal door. He hesitates, fear gripping him. But beneath the fear, a tiny ember of something else sparks.)
  Ijin Yu (thinking): (Thought) Even if it just means getting beaten up in a different place... maybe it's better than getting beaten up for nothing.
  [CLENCH]
  (He takes a deep, ragged breath. The decision, though terrifying, feels like the first real choice he's made in a long time.)

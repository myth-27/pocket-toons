=== The Breaker Episode 1 ===

  (The bell rings, signaling the end of another school day. For most, it's a release. For Shiwoon Yi, it's often when the real torment begins.)
  Jang-ho: Hey, Shiwoon. Where do you think you're going?
  Shiwoon Yi (thinking): (thought) Not again...
  Min-woo: Did you forget something, huh? Like, maybe... our lunch money?
  Shiwoon Yi: I... I already gave it to you guys this morning.
  Jang-ho: Oh? And you think that's enough? You think your pathetic allowance covers our 'protection fee' for the entire day?
  [THUD]
  Shiwoon Yi (thinking): (thought) My back... it hurts. Why can't I ever just fight back? Why am I so weak?
  Jang-ho: Look at him, guys. He's about to cry. What a baby.
  Jun-seo: Just give us what you have, Shiwoon. Or do you want another 'lesson'?
  Shiwoon Yi (thinking): (thought) I hate this. I hate myself for enduring it.
  [CRUMPLER]
  (Shiwoon hands over the crumpled bills, his eyes fixed on the ground. Another day, another humiliation.)
  (The next morning, a new face graced the faculty lounge.)
  Principal: Everyone, I'd like to introduce our new English teacher, Mr. Chun-woo Han.
  Chun-woo Han: (smirking) 'Sup. Call me Han. Or just 'teacher'. Whatever floats your boat.
  (The teachers exchanged uneasy glances. He was dressed casually, hair a bit wild, and carried an air of nonchalant rebellion.)
  Shiwoon Yi (thinking): (thought) He doesn't look like a teacher at all. He looks... like he just rolled out of bed.
  Chun-woo Han: Alright, class. English is a language. Languages are tools. Tools are for getting what you want. So, what do you want?
  Student 1: Uh... good grades?
  Chun-woo Han: Boring! Think bigger. Power? Money? Revenge? English can get you all that. If you know how to use it.
  Shiwoon Yi (thinking): (thought) Revenge? What kind of teacher talks about revenge?
  (Later that day, Shiwoon was heading home, trying to avoid the usual routes. He took a shortcut through a narrow alley.)
  [CLANG]
  Thug 1: Hey, old man! Hand over your wallet!
  (Shiwoon froze. Up ahead, three burly men had cornered... Mr. Han?)
  Chun-woo Han: (sighs) Can't a man enjoy his afternoon stroll without being bothered by mosquitoes?
  Thug 2: Mosquitoes?! You wanna get swatted, old timer?
  [WHOOSH]
  (One thug lunged. Mr. Han barely moved, a blur of motion. Shiwoon blinked.)
  [THWACK]
  Thug 1: Ugh!
  (The thug was suddenly on the ground, clutching his stomach. Mr. Han was standing exactly where he was before, hands in his pockets, a bored expression.)
  Thug 3: What the—?!
  [CRACK]
  (Another thug went down, seemingly tripping over his own feet, but Shiwoon could have sworn he saw Mr. Han's foot subtly extend and retract.)
  Chun-woo Han: Honestly, you kids need to learn some manners. And maybe some actual fighting skills.
  Thug 2: (stuttering) Wh-what are you?!
  Chun-woo Han: Just a teacher. Now, run along before I have to give you a pop quiz on pain.
  [SCRAMBLE]
  (The remaining thugs scrambled away, leaving their unconscious comrades behind. Mr. Han glanced around, then continued his stroll as if nothing had happened.)
  Shiwoon Yi (thinking): (thought) What... what did I just see? Was that... real? He didn't even break a sweat. He just... moved. Like a ghost.
  (Shiwoon hurried home, his mind racing. The image of Mr. Han's effortless movements burned in his memory.)
  Shiwoon Yi (thinking): (thought) All my life, I've been pushed around. Kicked. Beaten. I've always wished I could be strong. To stand up for myself. To not be afraid.
  Shiwoon Yi (thinking): (thought) That teacher... Mr. Han... he's different. He's not just strong, he's... something else. Could he... could he teach me?
  Shiwoon Yi (thinking): (thought) No, that's crazy. He's just an English teacher. But... what if?
  (A flicker of desperate hope ignited within Shiwoon, a spark in the darkness of his helplessness.)

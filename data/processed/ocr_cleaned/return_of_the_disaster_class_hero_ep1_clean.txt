=== Return of the Disaster-Class Hero Episode 1 ===

  (The void. A place where time and space cease to exist. A prison for the strongest, or perhaps, a tomb.)
  Lee Geon: Ugh... my head.
  Lee Geon (thinking): So, I'm finally out.
  [CRACKLE]
  (A rift tore open in the middle of a bustling city square, spitting out a man who looked like he'd just walked out of a forgotten legend.)
  Bystander 1: What in the world?! Is that a gate?
  Bystander 2: No, it closed! And... who's that guy?
  Lee Geon (thinking): Twenty years. It felt like an eternity, yet nothing at all.
  Lee Geon (thinking): The air... it's different. Weaker.
  [RUMBLE]
  (Suddenly, the ground trembled. A grotesque creature, a low-tier Calamity, burst from a nearby alley, its eyes glowing with malevolence.)
  Soldier 1: Monster alert! Everyone, evacuate! Get back!
  Soldier 2: Damn it, a Level 3! Where are the Saints?!
  Lee Geon (thinking): A Level 3? This is what passes for a threat now?
  Lee Geon: Pathetic.
  [WHOOSH]
  (Before anyone could react, Lee Geon moved. A blur of motion, a flicker of power that left the soldiers stunned.)
  [CRUNCH]
  (The monster, mid-roar, froze. Its head, cleanly severed, tumbled to the ground with a wet thud.)
  Soldier 1: W-what just happened?
  Soldier 2: Did... did he just kill it with his bare hands?
  Lee Geon (thinking): This world has gotten soft. The 'heroes' must be even softer.
  Lee Geon (thinking): The Twelve Zodiac Saints... they left me to rot in that void.
  Lee Geon (thinking): They took everything. My glory, my power, my life.
  Lee Geon (thinking): And now, they're probably living like kings, basking in the false praise of a world they barely protect.
  Lee Geon: Well, the Disaster-Class Hero is back.
  Lee Geon: And this time, I'm not here to save anyone.
  Lee Geon: I'm here to reclaim what's mine... and make them pay.
  [GLARE]
  (His eyes, once filled with the weariness of an endless prison, now burned with a cold, terrifying resolve. The world was about to remember the true meaning of disaster.)

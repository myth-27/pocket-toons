=== Murrz Episode 1 ===

  (The alarm clock blared, a cruel awakening from the sweet embrace of dreams. Another morning, another battle against the forces of sleep and the comfort of a perfectly warm bed.)
  Murrz (thinking): Ugh... no. Not yet. My bed is a warm, soft trap. A sanctuary from the harsh realities of mornings. Why can't I just hibernate?
  [BEEP-BEEP-BEEP]
  Murrz (thinking): Five more minutes. Just five. The world can definitely wait. It's not like anything truly urgent happens before 9 AM anyway, right?
  [SNORE (from Fiance)]
  Murrz (thinking): He looks so peaceful. How does he manage to sleep through anything? The blaring alarm, my internal panic, the existential dread of a new day... it's a superpower, honestly. I'm a little jealous.
  Murrz (thinking): He's like a human tranquilizer dart. Just completely unfazed.
  Murrz: (Muffled groan) Okay, fine. Reality calls.
  Murrz: Nope! Duty calls! Or, you know, my bladder calls. Same difference.
  [STRETCH]
  Murrz: Mmmph... morning, sleepyhead.
  Fiance: Mmmph... five more minutes...
  Murrz (thinking): The gravitational pull of blankets is definitely stronger in the morning. It's a scientific fact, I swear. NASA should study this phenomenon.
  Murrz (thinking): Time to face the mirror. The ultimate judge of morning self-worth.
  [CREAK (bathroom door)]
  Murrz (thinking): Behold, the bedhead champion, right here. My hair has achieved new levels of chaos. And what is *that*? A new zit? Seriously? The universe truly conspires against my complexion.
  Murrz (thinking): Why do my pajamas always feel so much better *before* I have to take them off? It's like they know their time is limited and decide to maximize comfort just to spite me.
  [SHUFFLE (clothes)]
  Murrz (thinking): Okay, what's the vibe today? 'Attempting to look presentable' or 'Embracing the potato'? Maybe a little bit of both.
  Murrz (thinking): The eternal struggle of comfort versus presentability. Comfort usually wins, let's be real.
  Fiance: (From kitchen) Coffee's almost ready, Murrz!
  Murrz: Be right there! (To herself) The promise of caffeine is the only thing truly motivating me right now. It's my lifeblood, my morning elixir.
  [CLINK (mug)]
  Murrz: Thanks, you're the best.
  Fiance: Morning, sleepyhead. You look cute.
  Murrz (thinking): He says that even when I look like I wrestled a badger and lost. I guess that's love? Or he desperately needs new glasses. Either way, I appreciate the sentiment.
  [SIP]
  Murrz (thinking): This coffee is life. Pure, unadulterated life. How do people function without it? Are they some kind of super-humans? Or perhaps... aliens?
  Fiance: Any plans for today? Besides surviving, of course.
  Murrz: Mostly that. Maybe draw a bit, stare blankly at my computer screen for a few hours, get distracted by cat videos, then realize it's dinner time. The usual. You?
  Fiance: Work, then maybe that new game later? The one with the dragons.
  Murrz: Sounds good. I'll provide moral support and snacks.
  Murrz: Just try not to yell too much. My delicate artistic sensibilities can't handle it.
  [MUNCH (Fiance eating cereal)]
  Murrz (thinking): He eats so fast. Like a tiny, adorable vacuum cleaner. It's impressive, really. Meanwhile, I'm still contemplating my first bite, wondering if it's worth the chewing effort required.
  Murrz (thinking): Okay, brain cells, activate! Or at least pretend to. The day won't procrastinate itself, even if I desperately want it to.
  Fiance: Ready for the day?
  Murrz: As I'll ever be. Let's do this.
  Murrz (thinking): Another day, another adventure... mostly involving snacks, existential dread, and the never-ending quest for comfy pants. But with him, it's always a little brighter. And at least there's coffee.

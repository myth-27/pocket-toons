=== Tales of the Unusual Episode 1 ===

  (Jinwoo had been searching for an affordable apartment for months. The city was ruthless, and his wallet, even more so.)
  (Then, he found it. A small studio, surprisingly spacious for its price, in an older building.)
  LANDLORD: Just sign here, and here. First month's rent and deposit, please.
  JINWOO (thinking): (Thinking) This is almost too good to be true. The landlord was a bit... eager.
  [RATTLE]
  (The next day, Jinwoo moved in. The apartment was simple, but clean. Everything seemed fine, until he entered the bathroom.)
  JINWOO (thinking): (Thinking) Whoa. That's... quite a mirror.
  (Dominating the small bathroom wall was an antique, full-length mirror with an ornate, dark wood frame. It was far too grand for the space.)
  JINWOO (thinking): (Thinking) Well, at least I won't have to buy one. It's a bit creepy, but functional.
  [SPLASH]
  (Days turned into a week. Jinwoo settled into his new routine. The mirror became just another fixture.)
  [BRUSH-BRUSH]
  (One evening, as he brushed his teeth, he noticed something odd.)
  JINWOO (thinking): (Thinking) Did my reflection just... blink slower than me?
  JINWOO: Nah, must be tired. Long day.
  (He shook his head, dismissing it. But the feeling lingered.)
  [DRIP... DRIP...]
  (The next morning, while shaving, he saw it again. A fleeting shadow behind his reflection, in the corner of his eye. But when he turned, nothing was there.)
  JINWOO (thinking): (Thinking) What the hell? Am I seeing things?
  (He started to feel uneasy around the mirror. Its ornate frame seemed to watch him.)
  JINWOO (thinking): (Thinking) Maybe I should just cover it up. It's too big anyway.
  (He found an old sheet and draped it over the mirror. Immediately, a strange chill filled the room.)
  [RUSTLE]
  (Later that night, he woke to a soft scratching sound coming from the bathroom.)
  JINWOO (thinking): (Thinking) What's that noise?
  [SCRAPE... SCRAPE...]
  (He slowly approached the bathroom door. The scratching grew louder, more insistent.)
  [CREAK]
  (He pushed the door open slightly. The sheet was gone. The mirror stood exposed, reflecting the dimly lit bathroom.)
  JINWOO (thinking): (Thinking) No... I covered it!
  (He stared at his reflection. It was him, but not quite. The eyes were too wide, the smile too slow to form.)
  [THUMP-THUMP (heartbeat)]
  JINWOO'S REFLECTION: Come closer, Jinwoo...
  (The voice wasn't spoken, but echoed directly in his mind. His reflection's hand slowly lifted, palm pressed against the glass.)
  JINWOO (thinking): (Thinking) This isn't real. It can't be real!
  (He stumbled backward, fear seizing him. His reflection's smile widened, revealing too many teeth.)
  JINWOO'S REFLECTION: Don't you want to see what's on the other side?
  (The mirror's surface rippled like water. His reflection's hand reached out, phasing through the glass, its fingers stretching towards him.)
  [RIPPLING]
  JINWOO: GET AWAY FROM ME!
  (He screamed, turning and scrambling out of the apartment, not bothering to grab anything. He didn't stop running until the building was a distant blur.)
  (Back in the empty apartment, the mirror reflected the silent bathroom. Then, a slow, knowing smirk spread across the reflection's face.)
  [SMIRK]

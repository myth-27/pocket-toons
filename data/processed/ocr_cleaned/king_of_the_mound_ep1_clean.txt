=== King of the Mound Episode 1 ===

  (The roar of the crowd. The smell of freshly cut grass. The tension that hangs heavy in the air. For some, it's just a game. For others, it's everything.)
  Kim Jinwoo (thinking): Another fastball, right down the middle. Minjun’s arm looks tired. He’s been throwing heat all game, but the pressure… it's getting to him.
  [CRACK!]
  Crowd: OOOHHH!
  Coach Lee: Dammit! A line drive to left field! Another run scores! Park Minjun, what are you doing out there?!
  Kim Jinwoo (thinking): Two outs, bottom of the seventh. We're down 5-3. Bases loaded. The cleanup hitter is up. This is the moment that decides the game. The moment that decides our season.
  [THWACK!]
  Umpire: BALL ONE!
  Kim Jinwoo (thinking): Minjun’s shoulders are slumped. His pitches are losing their bite. He’s trying to force it, but his control is gone. I know that feeling all too well.
  [FLASHBACK SOUND: WHOOSH]
  Kim Jinwoo (younger) (thinking): Just one more strike! You can do it, Jinwoo!
  [CRACK! (sound of a bat connecting hard)]
  Kim Jinwoo (younger) (thinking): No… not again…
  [POP! (sound of a shoulder dislocating)]
  Kim Jinwoo (thinking): I remember the pain. The crushing weight of expectation, then the sudden, searing agony in my shoulder. That was the day I lost my fastball. The day I stepped off the mound for good.
  [THWACK!]
  Umpire: BALL TWO!
  Coach Lee: Minjun, take a deep breath! You're rushing!
  Kim Jinwoo (thinking): He can't hear him. Not now. He's trapped in his own head. Just like I was.
  [THWACK!]
  Umpire: BALL THREE!
  Kim Jinwoo (thinking): Three balls, no strikes. The batter is waiting for his pitch. Minjun is one pitch away from walking in a run, maybe even losing the game right here.
  Coach Lee: This is bad… Who do I even have left? The bullpen is empty. Everyone’s arms are shot.
  Coach Lee (thinking): He glances down the bench, his eyes scanning each player. He pauses, his gaze lingering on me for a fraction of a second. No. He wouldn't.
  Coach Lee: Jinwoo! Get warmed up!
  Kim Jinwoo (thinking): My heart leaps into my throat. Me? After all this time? My shoulder… it hasn't felt a real pitch in years. Not a competitive one, anyway.
  [THWACK!]
  Umpire: BALL FOUR! RUNNER ADVANCES! THAT'S A WALK!
  Crowd: BOOOOOO!
  (The tying run crosses home plate. The score is now 5-4. Bases are still loaded. And the winning run stands on third.)
  Coach Lee: Minjun, come on out. Jinwoo, you're in. Get out there!
  Kim Jinwoo (thinking): The weight of the game, the season, the expectations… it all falls on me. My heart is pounding. My hands are shaking. But as I stand, the familiar scent of the dirt, the distant roar of the crowd… a strange calm washes over me.
  Kim Jinwoo (thinking): It's been so long. But the mound… it's calling to me.
  [THUMP-THUMP (heartbeat)]
  (Kim Jinwoo, a forgotten talent, steps onto the pitcher's mound once more. His past, his injury, his doubt… can he overcome them and reclaim his place as the King of the Mound?)

=== Lore Olympus Episode 1 ===

  (Olympus. A realm of gods, goddesses, and endless parties. Tonight, the god of the Underworld, Hades, is hosting.)
  PERSEPHONE (thinking): Oh, goodness. This is… a lot.
  PERSEPHONE (thinking): Mother would absolutely have my head if she saw me in this dress. A 'scandalous display of youth' she'd call it.
  PERSEPHONE (thinking): But it's just a little pink number. And Aphrodite said it was 'divine.' I think.
  [MUSIC (THUMPING, LOUD)]
  [CHATTER (OVERLAPPING, BOISTEROUS)]
  PERSEPHONE (thinking): Everyone looks so… confident. So at ease. I just feel like a lamb in a den of lions. Or, well, gods.
  HERA: Persephone, dear. Enjoying yourself?
  PERSEPHONE: Oh! Queen Hera! Yes, it's… quite lively.
  HERA: Lively indeed. You look charming in pink. A refreshing change from all the usual drabness.
  PERSEPHONE: Thank you, your Majesty.
  HERA: Don't be shy, child. Mingle. Perhaps even dance. This is Olympus, after all. Who knows what opportunities might arise?
  [CLINK (GLASSES)]
  PERSEPHONE (thinking): Opportunities?
  HERA: Just a thought. Now, if you'll excuse me, I see Ares making a fool of himself again.
  PERSEPHONE (thinking): Right. Mingle.
  PERSEPHONE (thinking): I should probably find a quieter corner. Maybe get some air.
  [FOOTSTEPS (SOFT)]
  HADES: Thanatos, how much longer until I can reasonably escape this… festivity?
  THANATOS: At least another hour, sir. Protocol, you know.
  HADES: Protocol. Right. The bane of my existence.
  HADES: Hmm.
  PERSEPHONE (thinking): Oh, no. I'm lost. This garden is much bigger than I thought.
  HADES: Lost, little goddess?
  PERSEPHONE: Ah! Oh, my apologies! I didn't see you there, Lord Hades.
  HADES: No need for apologies. You're Persephone, aren't you? Demeter's daughter.
  PERSEPHONE: That's me. Just… taking a break from the main party.
  HADES: I understand completely. It can be… overwhelming. Especially for someone new to Olympus.
  PERSEPHONE: It is. Very.
  HADES: I'm about to leave. Would you care for a ride home?
  PERSEPHONE: A ride? Oh, I couldn't possibly impose.
  HADES: Nonsense. It's no imposition. Thanatos, the car.
  THANATOS: Right away, sir.
  [WHIRR (ENGINE STARTING)]
  PERSEPHONE: Well, if you're sure. Thank you, Lord Hades.
  HADES: Hades is fine. And you're welcome, Persephone.
  PERSEPHONE (thinking): This car is… very nice. And so dark.
  HADES: It suits my aesthetic.
  PERSEPHONE: I suppose it does.
  HADES: You're rather quiet for a goddess of spring.
  PERSEPHONE: I… I just moved to Olympus. It's all a bit new.
  HADES: I remember that feeling. It takes some getting used to. Especially for someone as… unblemished as yourself.
  PERSEPHONE (thinking): Unblemished?
  HADES: Yes. Most of us here are quite… jaded. You, however… you're different.
  PERSEPHONE: Oh.
  PERSEPHONE (thinking): I'm not sure if that's a compliment or not.
  (The car sped through the night, carrying the god of the Underworld and the goddess of spring into an uncertain future.)

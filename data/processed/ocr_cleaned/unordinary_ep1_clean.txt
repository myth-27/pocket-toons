=== UnOrdinary Episode 1 ===

  (In a world where superpowers are commonplace, the social hierarchy is absolute.)
  (From the strongest 'high-tiers' to the weakest 'low-tiers', everyone knows their place.)
  (And then, there are the 'cripples'. Those born without abilities.)
  John (thinking): Another beautiful morning at Wellston High. Or, as I like to call it, 'The Daily Gauntlet'.
  John (thinking): Just gotta make it through the day without getting my face rearranged. Simple enough for a cripple, right?
  [THUD]
  Bully 1: Watch where you're going, cripple!
  John: (Rubbing his shoulder) My apologies. My eyes must have missed your overwhelming aura.
  Bully 1: What was that, punk?
  John: Just admiring your impressive physique. Clearly, you've been working out. Or... just born lucky.
  Bully 2: He's making fun of you, dude!
  Bully 1: Oh, I know. And I don't appreciate it.
  [CRACKLE (electricity)]
  John: Whoa, easy there, sparky. Just a compliment. No need to get all... charged up.
  Bully 1: You think this is a joke?
  John: Not at all. Just trying to lighten the mood before someone gets hurt. And let's be honest, it'd probably be me.
  Bully 1: Smart mouth. You're lucky I'm in a good mood today. Get lost.
  John (thinking): (Walking away) Phew. Close one. Another day, another successful de-escalation.
  John (thinking): It's a delicate dance, really. Be just annoying enough to be memorable, but not so annoying they feel compelled to crush you.
  [MURMURS (from other students)]
  John (thinking): There she is. The Queen of Wellston. Seraphina.
  John (thinking): Level 8.0. The strongest student in the school. Probably the strongest in the entire district.
  John (thinking): She walks through the halls like she owns them. Which, technically, she does. No one dares challenge her.
  [WHOOSH (Seraphina passes by quickly)]
  John (thinking): Must be nice. To be so powerful, so untouchable. To not have to worry about every single interaction.
  John (thinking): Me? I just need to get to lunch.
  [CLATTER (lunch tray)]
  John: Hey! That was my last piece of cake!
  Bully 3: Oops. My bad, cripple. Guess you'll have to go hungry.
  Bully 4: He looks so pathetic, begging for food.
  John: Pathetic? Nah. I'm just cultivating mass. And apparently, you're helping.
  Bully 3: You want to fight, cripple?
  John: And lose? Nah, I'm good. I'd rather keep my teeth.
  John: Besides, I'm sure you could use the extra calories. You look like you're about to blow away in a strong breeze.
  Bully 3: Why you little—!
  [GRRRR (Bully 3's aura flares)]
  John: Whoa, easy there. Just a friendly observation. You're looking... robust today. Very robust.
  John (thinking): (Walking away, leaving the bullies confused) Man, being a cripple is exhausting. All this dodging and weaving, verbally and physically.
  John (thinking): But it's better this way. It has to be.
  John (thinking): No one needs to know the truth. No one needs to know what I really am.
  John (thinking): The King of Wellston... That's a title I left behind a long, long time ago.
  [RUSTLE (John's backpack)]
  John (thinking): Just a normal high school student. That's all I am now. That's all I want to be.

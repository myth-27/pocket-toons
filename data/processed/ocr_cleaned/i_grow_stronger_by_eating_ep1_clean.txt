=== I Grow Stronger by Eating Episode 1 ===

  (In a world ravaged by dimensional rifts, humanity clung to survival. But for those like Lee Jinwoo, survival was a daily struggle, not a victory.)
  Lee Jinwoo (thinking): Another day, another empty stomach. This 'safe zone' is a joke.
  [RUSTLE]
  Lee Jinwoo: Huh? What was that?
  [SQUEAK! SCRATCH!]
  Lee Jinwoo: Just a Scavenger Rat. Good. Maybe I can at least get some protein...
  [SWISH! THUD!]
  Lee Jinwoo (thinking): Damn it! Even a rat is too fast for me. My 'E-rank' status is practically a death sentence.
  [GROWL!]
  Lee Jinwoo: No, no, no... Not a Goblin. Not here!
  (A scrawny Goblin, its eyes glinting with malice, emerged from the shadows. For Jinwoo, even this low-level monster was an insurmountable wall.)
  [CLANG! SLASH!]
  Lee Jinwoo: Ugh! My arm! It's too fast!
  [THWACK! CRACK!]
  Lee Jinwoo (thinking): This is it, isn't it? After all this struggling, I'm going to die to a Goblin in some forgotten alley.
  (As the Goblin raised its crude club for a finishing blow, a primal instinct surged through Jinwoo. He wasn't ready to die.)
  [BITE! RIP!]
  Lee Jinwoo (thinking): What... what am I doing?!
  (In a desperate, animalistic frenzy, Jinwoo bit into the Goblin's arm, tearing away a piece of its flesh. A strange, metallic taste filled his mouth.)
  [GULP! CHOMP!]
  Lee Jinwoo (thinking): It's... disgusting. But... I feel something.
  [DING! SHIMMER!]
  SYSTEM: [You have consumed a portion of 'Low-Grade Goblin Flesh'.]
  SYSTEM: [Your unique skill, 'Assimilation', has activated!]
  SYSTEM: [Strength +1]
  SYSTEM: [Endurance +1]
  Lee Jinwoo (thinking): What is this? A System message? I've never seen one before...
  [GROWL!]
  (The Goblin, momentarily stunned, roared and lunged again.)
  Lee Jinwoo (thinking): It's still coming! But... I feel lighter. Stronger, even!
  [PUNCH! CRACK!]
  Lee Jinwoo: Did... did I just punch it? And it actually stumbled?
  (With newfound, albeit slight, power, Jinwoo fought back. He didn't know how, but eating the monster had changed him.)
  [THUD! SPLATTER!]
  Lee Jinwoo (thinking): It's dead... I actually killed it. And I'm not even that tired.
  [DING!]
  SYSTEM: [You have defeated 'Low-Grade Goblin'.]
  SYSTEM: [Experience Gained!]
  Lee Jinwoo (thinking): This isn't just about survival anymore. If I can get stronger by eating... then I'll eat everything.
  Lee Jinwoo (thinking): Every monster, every core, every piece of flesh. I will grow stronger, no matter what.
  [CRUNCH! GULP!]

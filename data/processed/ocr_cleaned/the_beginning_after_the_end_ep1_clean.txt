=== The Beginning After the End Episode 1 ===

  (In my previous life, I was known as King Grey. A monarch whose strength was unmatched, whose wisdom was revered.)
  (But behind the impenetrable facade of power, I was nothing more than a puppet, a weapon forged by circumstance.)
  (I lived a life of solitude, constantly wary, constantly fighting. There was no warmth, no genuine connection, only the cold weight of a crown.)
  (My reign was long, my battles countless. Each victory hollow, each loss a further chip at my humanity.)
  (And then, it ended.)
  (The final confrontation was a blur of steel and magic. A culmination of a life spent in conflict.)
  King Grey: (Gasping) Is this... finally it?
  (My body, once an instrument of absolute power, now felt like a shattered vessel. The world faded to black, a silence I had never known engulfing me.)
  (I expected oblivion. Peace, perhaps. But what I got was... this.)
  (A blinding light. Muffled sounds. A crushing weight, then an unbearable pressure, pushing me forward.)
  (The air was cold, sharp, burning my lungs. My body was small, weak, utterly helpless.)
  NARRATOR (thinking): What in the blazes...?
  Alice Leywin: He's here! Oh, my baby!
  Reynolds Leywin: Alice, you did it! He's beautiful!
  (Warmth enveloped me. A soft, gentle touch I'd never experienced. A woman's voice, full of overwhelming affection.)
  (My eyes, still blurry, tried to focus. Two enormous faces loomed over me, their expressions radiating pure joy.)
  NARRATOR (thinking): Wait... enormous? And why do I feel like I weigh less than a loaf of bread?
  (A sudden, horrifying realization struck me. My limbs were tiny, my movements uncoordinated. I was crying, not from sadness, but from pure, unadulterated shock.)
  NARRATOR (thinking): I... I'm a baby?!
  Alice Leywin: Shhh, it's okay, my little one. Mommy's here.
  (Her voice was like a lullaby, soothing a part of me I didn't know existed. A part that had been starved for affection for decades.)
  Reynolds Leywin: What should we name him, my love? Our little hero.
  Alice Leywin: Arthur. Arthur Leywin. It's perfect.
  (Arthur Leywin. A new name. A new body. A new life. The King Grey was dead. And I, Arthur, was just beginning.)
  (The irony wasn't lost on me. A man who ruled an entire continent, now utterly dependent, unable to even control his own bladder.)
  (This was not the afterlife I expected. But perhaps... perhaps it was the one I needed.)

=== Eleceed Episode 1 ===

  (In a world where extraordinary abilities are hidden from the public eye, there are those who live ordinary lives, burdened by extraordinary secrets.)
  JIWOO (thinking): (Thought) Ugh, I'm late again! If I don't hurry, I'll miss the first period!
  [WHOOSH! WHOOSH!]
  JIWOO (thinking): (Thought) Just a little faster... Almost there...!
  [SQUEAK!]
  JIWOO: Huh?
  [SKID!]
  JIWOO (thinking): (Thought) A kitten stuck in a tree! No, I can't just leave it!
  [THWIP! (Jiwoo leaps up)]
  JIWOO: Gotcha! There, there, little one. You're safe now.
  [PURRR...]
  JIWOO (thinking): (Thought) I really need to control this. One day, someone's going to see me. My super speed... it's a blessing and a curse.
  (Jiwoo Seo. A high school student with an extraordinary secret. He just wants to live a normal life, but his powers make 'normal' a distant dream.)
  JIWOO (thinking): (Thought) Another stray... This alley is full of them. Wait... what's that?
  [WHIMPER...]
  JIWOO: Oh my god! A cat! It's... it's badly hurt!
  (Lying amidst the trash was a large, black cat. Its fur was matted with blood, and its breathing was shallow. But what truly caught Jiwoo's eye were its piercing, golden eyes.)
  JIWOO (thinking): (Thought) It looks like it's been in a terrible fight. I can't just leave it here to die!
  [GENTLE LIFT...]
  JIWOO: Don't worry, little guy. I'll take care of you.
  KAYDEN (CAT FORM) (thinking): (Thought) What is this human doing? His touch... it's so gentle. And annoyingly warm. Damn it, I'm still too weak to move.
  [MEOW! (A weak, irritated sound)]
  JIWOO: You're a feisty one, aren't you? Even when you're this hurt. You'll be okay.
  KAYDEN (CAT FORM) (thinking): (Thought) Feisty? I am Kayden, the strongest Awakened! This pathetic form... this pathetic human! If I had my powers, I'd incinerate him!
  [DOOR CREAK...]
  JIWOO: Alright, we're home. Let's get you cleaned up.
  KAYDEN (CAT FORM) (thinking): (Thought) Home? This cramped, dusty hovel? And what's that smell? Disinfectant? Is he trying to poison me?
  JIWOO: Hold still. This might sting a bit. You have a pretty deep cut here.
  [HISS! (Kayden tries to swat)]
  JIWOO: Whoa! You're really energetic for someone so injured! Good, that means you'll recover quickly.
  KAYDEN (CAT FORM) (thinking): (Thought) Energetic? I'm trying to rip your hand off! This indignity... Being treated like a common house pet by a mere human!
  JIWOO: There. All bandaged up. Now, how about some food? I have some tuna, or maybe some chicken?
  KAYDEN (CAT FORM) (thinking): (Thought) Food? I need to regain my strength, not indulge in these paltry offerings. But... my stomach growls. This form is truly a curse.
  [MEOW! (A slightly less weak meow, demanding)]
  JIWOO: Haha, you're quite the character. I'll call you... 'Kayden'. It sounds strong, just like you.
  KAYDEN (CAT FORM) (thinking): (Thought) He just... named me? Without permission? The audacity! Wait, 'Kayden'? How did he know? No, it must be a coincidence. A very annoying coincidence.
  JIWOO (thinking): (Thought) I hope he gets better soon. He's a bit scary, but he needs help. And I can't just abandon him. Just like I can't abandon my secret. My life just got a lot more complicated.
  (Unaware of the legendary Awakened he had just brought into his home, Jiwoo's ordinary life was about to take a truly extraordinary turn.)

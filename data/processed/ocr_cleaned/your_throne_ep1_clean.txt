=== Your Throne Episode 1 ===

  (The Grand Hall of the Imperial Palace hummed with a tension thick enough to choke on. Every noble, every courtier, every servant held their breath, waiting for the Emperor's decree.)
  MEDEA SOLON (thinking): Another day, another performance.
  MEDEA SOLON (thinking): My family's reputation, my father's ambition, my own damn pride... all hinged on this single moment. The Crown Princess selection.
  [MURMURS]
  PSYCHE CALLISTA: Lady Medea.
  MEDEA SOLON (thinking): The saccharine voice, like honeyed poison. I turned, a practiced smile already plastered on my face.
  MEDEA SOLON: Lady Psyche. You look radiant, as always.
  PSYCHE CALLISTA: Oh, please. You flatter me. I'm merely... nervous.
  [GIGGLE (soft)]
  MEDEA SOLON (thinking): Nervous? The darling of the empire? The one everyone adores? How quaint.
  PSYCHE CALLISTA: We've both worked so hard, haven't we? No matter the outcome, I hope we can remain friends.
  MEDEA SOLON (thinking): Friends? You mean, you hope I'll gracefully accept my defeat and disappear into the shadows.
  MEDEA SOLON: Of course, Lady Psyche. A true friendship is built on mutual respect, wouldn't you agree?
  EROS ORNA DE ARAD: And what a beautiful sight it is, to see two of the empire's brightest stars shining together.
  (Crown Prince Eros, a smile as captivating as it was insincere, approached us.)
  MEDEA SOLON (thinking): The puppet master himself. Always playing to the crowd, always observing.
  PSYCHE CALLISTA: Your Highness!
  [BOW (rustle)]
  EROS ORNA DE ARAD: Medea, my dear. You look stunning. That gown... it perfectly captures your fierce spirit.
  MEDEA SOLON (thinking): He knew exactly what to say. A compliment that sounded genuine, yet subtly reminded me of my 'unfeminine' ambition.
  MEDEA SOLON: Thank you, Your Highness. I merely dress to reflect the times.
  EROS ORNA DE ARAD: And Psyche, your gentle grace is a balm to the soul. You both represent the pinnacle of our nobility.
  PSYCHE CALLISTA: You are too kind, Your Highness.
  (A sudden hush fell over the hall as the Emperor's herald stepped forward.)
  [TRUMPETS (fanfare)]
  HERALD: By the grace of the Emperor, and the will of the gods, the next Crown Princess of the Arad Empire shall be...
  MEDEA SOLON (thinking): My heart hammered against my ribs. This was it. The culmination of years of training, of sacrifice.
  HERALD: ...Lady Psyche Callista!
  [GASPS (collective)]
  [CHEERS (growing louder)]
  (The hall erupted. Psyche's eyes widened in feigned surprise, a hand flying to her mouth. Eros offered her a dazzling, victorious smile.)
  MEDEA SOLON (thinking): My vision blurred. A cold, bitter rage settled deep in my gut. It couldn't be. Not her. Not again.
  MEDEA SOLON (thinking): All my efforts, all my sacrifices... for this? To be cast aside for that fragile doll?
  MEDEA SOLON (thinking): My father's face, a mask of disappointment, flashed in my mind. The whispers of the nobles, their pitying glances.
  MEDEA SOLON (thinking): I will not accept this. I will not be defeated. Not by her, and certainly not by him.
  MEDEA SOLON (thinking): This isn't over. Not by a long shot.
  [CLENCH (fist)]
  (As Psyche was led to the Emperor, bathed in the light of triumph, Medea stood in the encroaching shadow, a silent vow burning in her eyes.)

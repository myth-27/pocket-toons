=== Let's Play Episode 1 ===

  (For as long as I can remember, this has been my world. The glow of the monitor, the rhythmic click-clack of keys, the endless hum of the PC fan – it was my sanctuary, my battlefield, my everything.)
  Sam Young (thinking): Just a few more lines... almost there... The physics engine needs a slight tweak, and then, the final boss's attack pattern...
  (Years of ramen noodles, instant coffee, and sacrificing any semblance of a social life. All for this moment. All for 'Ruminate.' My game. My heart and soul poured into every pixel.)
  Sam Young (thinking): It's not just a game. It's a story. It's a world I built from scratch, a place where players can truly lose themselves. A place where their choices matter.
  (And then, the day finally arrived. After countless delays, debugging nightmares, and moments of pure existential dread, 'Ruminate' was live. Available to the world.)
  Sam Young: Okay, Sam. Deep breaths. It's out there. No turning back now. Just... just check the forums. See what people are saying.
  [MOUSE CLICK. WEB BROWSER REFRESH]
  Sam Young: (Reading) 'Charming art style' ... 'Unique mechanics' ... 'Surprisingly deep narrative.' Oh! Oh my gosh! People actually like it!
  [NOTIFICATION PING! PING! PING!]
  (My heart swelled with a joy I hadn't felt in years. All the sacrifices, all the sleepless nights... they were worth it. My dream was finally taking flight.)
  (Then, a single video thumbnail caught my eye. A familiar face. A name that sent shivers down the spine of every indie developer. A name synonymous with brutal honesty and unparalleled influence.)
  Sam Young: (Reading title) 'Ruminate' - A Waste of Time? By ML_Law.
  Sam Young (thinking): No... No, it can't be. Not him. Please, not him.
  [DRAMATIC MUSIC (from video begins)]
  Marshall Law (from video): Alright, guys. Today we're looking at 'Ruminate.' And honestly? This game is a prime example of style over substance. The story is cliché, the combat is clunky, and don't even get me started on the ending. It's just... bad.
  Sam Young (thinking): Bad? Clunky? He didn't even *understand* it! The ending was supposed to be ambiguous! The combat was designed to be challenging, not clunky!
  Marshall Law (from video): Save your money. Save your time. There are far better indie titles out there. This one? A hard pass.
  [FIST SLAM (on desk) - THUD!]
  Sam Young: He... he just ruined everything. My game... my dream... everything I worked for...
  (My dream, my passion, my entire future... shattered by one man, one review. The comments section was already a wasteland of agreement, echoing his every word.)
  Sam Young (thinking): No. Not shattered. Not yet. I won't let him. I can't let him.
  (A cold, hard resolve settled in my chest, replacing the despair. He thought he could just dismiss years of my life with a few harsh words? He thought he could just crush my world?)
  Sam Young: Marshall Law... I'm coming for you.
  [STING! (dramatic sound)]

=== Leveling With the Gods Episode 1 ===

  (The acrid smell of dust and blood filled his nostrils. A familiar scent, one he thought he'd forgotten forever.)
  KIM YUWON (thinking): This... this isn't right. I died. I remember dying.
  KIM YUWON (thinking): The final battle... against the Outer Gods... I failed. Everything was consumed. My body, my power, my very existence.
  SYSTEM: [Welcome, Player. You have entered the Tutorial.]
  KIM YUWON (thinking): The Tutorial? This voice... it's been so long. Too long.
  KIM YUWON (thinking): My body... it's young again. Weak. Pathetic. But... this is it. I'm back.
  KIM YUWON (thinking): The past... the future... it's all laid out before me. This time, I won't make the same mistakes. I won't let them fall.
  KIM YUWON (thinking): The Gods... the Outer Gods... they will pay. Every single one of them who watched, who betrayed, who laughed.
  SYSTEM: [First Test: Eliminate all Goblins in the designated area. Remaining: 10.]
  KIM YUWON (thinking): Goblins. How quaint. I remember this. The first test. The one where everyone struggles, where the weak are culled.
  [GRRRR!]
  KIM YUWON (thinking): They're weak, but numerous. A typical trap for beginners who rush in without thinking. A test of composure, not strength.
  [THWIP!]
  KIM YUWON (thinking): But I'm not a beginner. Not anymore.
  [WHIZZ! CRACK!]
  SYSTEM: [You have defeated a Goblin. Remaining: 9.]
  KIM YUWON (thinking): Pathetic. Even with this weak body, my senses, my experience... they remain. The muscle memory, the knowledge of their pressure points.
  [SHANK! GURGLE!]
  SYSTEM: [You have defeated a Goblin. Remaining: 8.]
  KIM YUWON (thinking): This body is a cage. But it's a cage I know how to break out of. Faster this time. Much, much faster.
  GENERIC PARTICIPANT: H-Help! There are too many! My sword... I dropped it!
  [CLANG!]
  KIM YUWON (thinking): Fool. Panic is the first step to death in the Tower. And a waste of my time.
  [THUD! THUD! SLASH! STAB!]
  SYSTEM: [You have defeated a Goblin. Remaining: 1.]
  KIM YUWON (thinking): Just one left. And it's hiding. A common tactic for the 'leader' type, thinking itself cunning.
  [SNIFF SNIFF]
  KIM YUWON (thinking): Too slow.
  [CRUNCH!]
  SYSTEM: [You have defeated all Goblins in the designated area.]
  SYSTEM: [First Test Cleared!]
  SYSTEM: [Reward: 100 CP. Basic Strength Potion (F).]
  SYSTEM: [You have gained 1 point in Strength.]
  KIM YUWON (thinking): CP... Strength Potion... it's all exactly as I remember. The first steps of a journey I've already walked.
  KIM YUWON (thinking): This is just the beginning. A long, arduous path lies ahead. A path filled with trials, betrayals, and the eyes of the Gods.
  KIM YUWON (thinking): But this time, I know the way. I know the shortcuts, the hidden quests, the weaknesses of the Gods themselves.
  KIM YUWON (thinking): The Gods who watched as the world burned. The Gods who betrayed us for their own games.
  KIM YUWON (thinking): They will regret letting me return. The Tower... it will tremble.

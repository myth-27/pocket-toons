=== I Love Yoo Episode 1 ===

  (Another day. Another sunrise I barely registered through the blinds.)
  Shin-Ae (thinking): (Thought) Ugh. My alarm. Or what's left of it. It's probably past noon. School's definitely out of the question.
  Shin-Ae (thinking): (Thought) My stomach's doing its usual opera. I wonder if there's any instant ramen left. Or maybe just... stale bread.
  Shin-Ae: Dad?
  Shin-Ae's Dad: (Coughing) Just resting, kiddo. Don't worry about me.
  Shin-Ae (thinking): (Thought) Right. 'Don't worry.' As if I have a choice. Being broke isn't exactly a luxury.
  [PHONE VIBRATE]
  Shin-Ae (thinking): (Thought) Rika. What does she want now? Probably to drag me to some 'fun' event where I'll feel even more out of place.
  Shin-Ae: Hello?
  Rika: Shin-Ae! You finally picked up! Where are you? You missed school again!
  Shin-Ae: I'm home. Sleeping. What's it to you?
  Rika: Don't be like that! Guess what? There's this super exclusive party tonight! My cousin got us invites! It's going to be amazing, all the rich kids will be there!
  Shin-Ae: Pass. I'm not into watching rich kids flaunt their parents' money. And I have nothing to wear.
  Rika: Nonsense! I'll pick out something for you! We HAVE to go! It's a chance to meet someone rich and handsome!
  Shin-Ae (thinking): (Thought) 'Rich and handsome.' Yeah, because that's what I need. Another problem.
  (An hour later, I found myself being shoved into a dress that was definitely not my style, and dragged to a mansion that screamed 'old money.')
  [CHIC MUSIC, CHATTER]
  Shin-Ae (thinking): (Thought) This is hell. Everyone's dressed like they're going to a fashion show. I just want a burger.
  Rika: Shin-Ae, try to mingle! Don't just stand there by the food table!
  Shin-Ae: The food table is the only reason I'm here. Besides, I'm starving.
  Shin-Ae (thinking): (Thought) Finally, some orange juice. Maybe I can chug this and make my escape.
  [CLINK]
  Shin-Ae: Whoa!
  [SPLASH]
  (In my haste, I bumped into someone. Hard. And the entire glass of orange juice went flying.)
  Kousuke: ...
  Shin-Ae (thinking): (Thought) Oh, crap. It's on his ridiculously expensive-looking suit. And he's staring at me like I just murdered his pet unicorn.
  Shin-Ae: Oh, my bad. Uh... sorry.
  Kousuke: Do you have any idea how much this suit costs?
  Shin-Ae: No, and frankly, I don't care. It's just juice. It'll wash out.
  Kousuke: You are incredibly rude. And you're making a scene.
  Shin-Ae: I'm making a scene? You're the one glaring at me like I personally offended your ancestors. Just tell me how much I owe you, and I'll pay you back. Eventually.
  Kousuke: You can start by leaving. Now.
  Shin-Ae (thinking): (Thought) Fine by me. This party sucked anyway.
  Shin-Ae: Gladly. And next time, try not to stand so close to the buffet if you don't want to get stained.
  [STOMP]
  (I stormed out, the stares of the 'elite' burning into my back. Just my luck. Another perfect day, perfectly ruined.)

=== Trash of the Count's Family Episode 1 ===

  [GROAN]
  Cale Henituse (thinking): Ugh... My head feels like it's been repeatedly smashed by a hammer. What in the world did I drink last night?
  (Kim Rok Soo, a man who had lived a perfectly average, if somewhat cynical, life as a team leader in a dangerous ability-user organization, slowly opened his eyes.)
  Cale Henituse (thinking): Wait. This isn't my apartment. This isn't even my body.
  [WHOOSH (memory recall)]
  Cale Henituse (thinking): Red hair. A face I recognize… No way. This is… Cale Henituse. The trash of the Count's family from 'The Birth of a Hero'?
  (Memories flooded his mind – not just his own, but Cale Henituse's. The novel he had just finished reading before falling asleep. The protagonist, Choi Han. The villain, the White Star. And Cale, the minor character destined to be beaten to a pulp by Choi Han.)
  Cale Henituse (thinking): This is ridiculous. I just wanted to live a quiet life, retire early, and read books. Not transmigrate into a novel, let alone as *this* guy! The guy who gets brutalized by the protagonist!
  Cale Henituse (thinking): If I remember correctly, Choi Han arrives in Harris Village in about six months. And then, the village gets destroyed, and Cale provokes him and gets half-killed.
  Cale Henituse (thinking): No. Absolutely not. I refuse to be a punching bag. I refuse to get involved in any of that hero nonsense. My goal is a peaceful, lazy life. A life where I don't have to risk my neck every five minutes.
  (He scanned the opulent room, a stark contrast to his old, modest apartment. Wine bottles lay scattered, evidence of Cale's infamous 'trashy' lifestyle.)
  Cale Henituse (thinking): Alright, Kim Rok Soo. Think. How do you avoid getting caught up in the plot? How do you live a comfortable, trashy, but safe life?
  Cale Henituse (thinking): The answer is simple. If I'm already known as trash, I'll just lean into it. No one expects anything from trash. No one asks trash to be a hero. Trash just... exists.
  [CREAK (door opening)]
  Ron Molan: Young Master, you are finally awake.
  (A man with a benign smile, yet eyes that held a chilling glint, entered the room. Ron, the butler. And, as Kim Rok Soo knew, a terrifying assassin.)
  Cale Henituse: Ugh, old man. You're too loud. My head is throbbing.
  Cale Henituse (thinking): He's even scarier in person. Keep up the act, Kim Rok Soo. Don't let him see through you.
  Ron Molan: My apologies, Young Master. Would you like some refreshing lemon tea? Or perhaps, something stronger to soothe your hangover?
  Cale Henituse: Lemon tea? Are you trying to kill me, old man? Bring me a bottle of the finest wine. And make it quick! I need to drown this headache.
  Cale Henituse (thinking): Perfect. Act like a spoiled, demanding brat. That's Cale. That's my ticket to a peaceful life.
  Ron Molan: As you wish, Young Master. I shall be right back.
  (Ron's smile widened, a smile that didn't quite reach his eyes, before he turned and exited the room.)
  [CLICK (door closing)]
  Cale Henituse (thinking): Step one: Survive. Step two: Get rich. Step three: Live a life of leisure. This Cale Henituse persona… it's my shield. My path to freedom.
  Cale Henituse (thinking): Let the hero save the world. I'll just be over here, being trash, and enjoying my retirement.

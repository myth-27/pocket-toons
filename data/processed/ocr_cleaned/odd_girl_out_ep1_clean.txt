=== Odd Girl Out Episode 1 ===

  (Another morning. Another day.)
  [ALARM RING]
  NARI: (Groan) Already?
  NARI (thinking): I drag myself out of bed, the same routine every day. School, homework, repeat.
  NARI (thinking): And then there’s… this.
  [SIGH]
  NARI (thinking): My reflection. Plain. Average. Nothing special. Just… me.
  NARI (thinking): At least my uniform hides most of it.
  [CHIRP CHIRP]
  [FOOTSTEPS]
  (The school gate is already bustling. And there they are. My friends.)
  MIRAE: Nari! Over here!
  NARI (thinking): Mirae, Yuri, Ryun. They shine. Literally. Even in school uniforms, they stand out like celebrities.
  [MURMUR MURMUR (students whispering)]
  STUDENT A: Wow, there’s Mirae again. So pretty.
  STUDENT B: Yuri’s so cute! And Ryun’s style is always on point.
  NARI (thinking): And then there’s me, trailing behind them, practically invisible.
  YURI: Good morning, Nari! You’re just in time.
  NARI: Morning, guys.
  RYUN: Did you finish the math homework? I barely got through it.
  NARI: Yeah, it was tough. But I managed.
  MIRAE: See? Nari’s always so diligent. Unlike some people.
  RYUN: Hey! I try!
  [RINGGGG (school bell)]
  (Class passes by in a blur. My gaze keeps drifting to them. Mirae, confidently answering a question. Yuri, diligently taking notes with her neat handwriting. Ryun, sketching something cool in her notebook during a break.)
  NARI (thinking): They're all so talented, so captivating.
  [CLATTER (lunch trays)]
  (Lunchtime. The loudest part of the day.)
  MIRAE: Ugh, this kimchi stew is spicier than usual today.
  YURI: Really? I think it’s just right! Here, Nari, try some of my fish cakes.
  NARI: Oh, thanks, Yuri.
  [CHEW CHEW]
  RYUN: Did you guys see that new café that opened near the station? Their pastries look amazing on Instagram.
  MIRAE: Oh, I saw that! We should totally go after school tomorrow.
  YURI: Sounds fun! What do you think, Nari?
  NARI: Uh, yeah, sounds good.
  NARI (thinking): I probably won't have anything stylish enough to wear, or know how to pose for their 'aesthetic' photos. I'll just be… there.
  [CHATTER CHATTER]
  (After school. The girls are gathered, making plans.)
  MIRAE: So, Saturday night, my place. We can watch a movie.
  YURI: Ooh, a horror movie? I'll bring snacks!
  RYUN: I’m in. Nari, you coming?
  NARI: Oh, um… I’m not sure. I might have plans with my family.
  NARI (thinking): It's a lie. I just don't want to be the one who screams at every jump scare while they're all cool and composed. Or the one who doesn't know what to say.
  MIRAE: Aw, too bad. Well, next time then!
  NARI: Yeah… next time.
  [FOOTSTEPS (fading)]
  NARI (thinking): I watch them walk away, laughing, their voices carrying in the evening air. They're a perfect, dazzling trio.
  NARI (thinking): And I'm just… the odd girl out.
  [SIGH]

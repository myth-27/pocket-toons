=== Episode 2 ===

SUMMARY: Dokja Kim, an ordinary contract worker, reflects on his life and his decade-long habit of reading the web novel "Three Ways to Survive the Apocalypse" (TWSA), which is ending today. He encounters his popular colleague, Sangah, on the subway, and they discuss their lives and hobbies. As Dokja anticipates the final episode of TWSA, he receives a message from the author, TLS123, announcing the series will become paid content and offering a special gift. However, at 7 PM, the world suddenly changes with a system message declaring the end of "free service" and the start of the "main scenario."

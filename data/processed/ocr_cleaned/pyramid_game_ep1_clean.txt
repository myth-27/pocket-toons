=== Pyramid Game Episode 1 ===

  (Another transfer. Another new school. Another fresh start.)
  (My dad's job in the military means moving around is just... normal for me now. I've learned not to get too attached, not to expect too much.)
  (This time, it's Baekyeon Girls' High School. Supposedly one of the best.)
  HOMEROOM TEACHER: Alright, everyone, settle down. We have a new student joining us today.
  HOMEROOM TEACHER: Come on in, Sung Su-ji.
  Sung Su-ji: Hello. I'm Sung Su-ji. Please take care of me.
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (A quick scan of the room. Twenty-five girls. All eyes on me. Some curious, some indifferent. A few even smiling. Seems normal enough.)
  HOMEROOM TEACHER: Su-ji, you can take the empty seat in the back, next to Myung Ja-eun.
  Sung Su-ji: Thank you.
  [SHUFFLE]
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (The girl next to me, Ja-eun, doesn't even look up. Her hair covers most of her face. She seems... quiet.)
  CLASSMATE 1: Psst, did you see her bag? It's pretty nice.
  CLASSMATE 2: Yeah, but she looks a bit plain, doesn't she?
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (Already being judged. Standard. Just keep a low profile.)
  Baek Ha-rin: Welcome, Su-ji. I'm Baek Ha-rin. If you need anything, just ask.
  Sung Su-ji: Thank you, Ha-rin.
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (Baek Ha-rin. The 'it' girl, clearly. Prettiest, most confident, surrounded by a small entourage. Her smile doesn't quite reach her eyes.)
  [BELL RINGS]
  Sung Su-ji: Lunchtime already? This day is flying by.
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (Everyone's heading to the cafeteria. Ja-eun is still just sitting there. Alone.)
  Sung Su-ji: Hey, Ja-eun. Aren't you coming?
  Myung Ja-eun: ...
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (She just shakes her head slightly, not looking at me. Okay, then.)
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (Later that day, just before dismissal. The teacher hands out small tablets.)
  HOMEROOM TEACHER: Alright, girls. Time for the monthly 'Pyramid Game' vote.
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (Pyramid Game? What's that?)
  HOMEROOM TEACHER: Remember, you get five votes. Three for A-rank, two for B-rank. You can't vote for yourself. And no abstaining.
  Sung Su-ji: Excuse me, Teacher. What exactly is this 'Pyramid Game'?
  HOMEROOM TEACHER: Ah, Su-ji. Right, you're new. It's a popularity ranking system we have in Class 2-5. Helps us maintain a healthy social environment.
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (A popularity contest? In high school? This sounds... dangerous.)
  CLASSMATE 3: Just vote for who you like, Su-ji. It's simple.
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (I look at the tablet. A list of names. Myung Ja-eun's name is there. Baek Ha-rin's. Everyone's.)
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (A-rank gets privileges. B-rank is average. C-rank is... less than average. And F-rank... F-rank is the target.)
  [TAP TAP TAP]
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (I cast my votes, feeling a strange unease. This isn't just a game.)
  HOMEROOM TEACHER: Voting is closed! Let's see the results.
  [BEEP BOOP]
  (The projector screen flickers to life, displaying a pyramid with names arranged in tiers.)
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (Baek Ha-rin, top of A-rank, of course. Lim Ye-rim, the idol trainee, right below her. And at the very bottom... F-rank.)
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (Myung Ja-eun. She's F-rank. And my name... Sung Su-ji. D-rank.)
  CLASSMATE 4: Look, Ja-eun is F again. No surprise there.
  CLASSMATE 5: And the new girl, D-rank. Not bad for a transfer.
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (The bell rings. Everyone packs up. But no one talks to Ja-eun. No one even looks at her.)
  Baek Ha-rin: Oh, Ja-eun. Did you forget your bag? How clumsy.
  [THUD]
  (Ha-rin's foot 'accidentally' trips Ja-eun's bag, sending its contents scattering across the floor.)
  Myung Ja-eun: ...
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (Ja-eun slowly, silently, begins to pick up her things. No one helps. Everyone just watches, some with smirks.)
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (This isn't just a game. This is a hierarchy. And F-rank isn't just unpopular. F-rank is the target. The punching bag.)
  Sung Su-ji (thinking): (And I just stepped right into it.)
